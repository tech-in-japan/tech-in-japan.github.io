We drill the flange and fix it on the screws, quickly and reliably. Inside the spindle there are two rather high-quality bearings.



Then we make the winder drive, we need a small speed and managed to adjust the clutch from the same Vidic.

For the lamp, I made such a house and a film channel from aluminum strips. One lane is fixed, and the second is mobile. In terms of making it spring-loaded and coming up with a film channel cover to press the film on top. The window is twice as large as the frame so that there are no shadows.

The camera is secured on a movable platform, the professional milling grooves with a hand mill, and through them the screws are screwed into a lining of the same size. Screws with a diameter less than the grooves and can be moved a couple of mm to the left and to the right.
