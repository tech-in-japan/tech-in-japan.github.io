Where does the creation of a marketplace begin. Part one

Today, this type of IT business, such as marketplace, is becoming more widespread. And there are obvious reasons for this. One of them is a very high liquidity, as well as a great potential for further growth, which with a competent approach can bring very impressive profits. It is about this competent approach that I want to talk with you today.



But before we begin, let's outline what the marketplace is, why it is needed, and what benefits it brings to the business.



As the name implies, the marketplace is a kind of virtual platform for trading. Which allows some users to offer their services or products, while others receive these same services or purchase goods. In fact, there are a huge variety of all kinds of types and types of marketplaces, but all of them are united by the fact that this is a convenient and thoughtful service on which the entire IT business of the owner of the marketplace rests.



Now that we understand what is at stake, let's move on to how to actually create such a project. I want to say right away that with all the seeming simplicity and lightness of the idea itself, the process of creating, and the subsequent success of the marketplace, is by no means a trivial task. So, enough talk, let's go figure it out, what's what.

Monetization



Such a moment for the marketplace, as monetization, is also important. There are many kinds of monetization models. For example, by subscription, a percentage of the transaction, payment for fixed packages and others. Therefore, a business should immediately think about how it plans to make money. It is not always possible to accurately and correctly determine which type of monetization is best for each particular project. That is why sometimes it happens that a business launches a new marketplace, and the chosen type of monetization did not bring the desired result. Then the business begins to change the type of monetization, and thus it turns out to choose the most effective way to make a profit. In fact, this is a rather complicated question, so it needs to be very well considered at the stage of creating business logic. If you immediately lose sight of this moment, then it can turn out to be very difficult to build the correct work of the marketplace so that it starts to make a profit.
