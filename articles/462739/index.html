Game Industry Conference GAMEDEV.HOUSE

On September 22, 2019, a conference from Gamedev.House will be held at the Higher School of Business Informatics of the Higher School of Economics at the Higher School of Economics (HSE) on the creation and launch of gaming projects. The event will be held with the support of educational programs in the game industry of the Higher School of Computer Engineering - “Game Project Management” and “Fundamentals of Game Creation”.









From 12:00 until the evening, guests of the event will be able to listen to reports from industry representatives, ask lecturers questions, get acquainted with the projects of indie developers and graduates of the Higher School of Economics at the Higher School of Economics, presented at the showcase, and also spend the whole day in the company of like-minded people - making acquaintances and useful communication.



In the program of the event



  • Reports from industry representatives;
  • Round tables on the most relevant topics of creating and launching games on different platforms;
  • Showcases of game projects from independent developers and games of graduates of educational programs in the gaming industry at the Higher School of Economics, Higher School of Economics;
  • Game library with exciting board games;
  • Stands of gaming companies;
  • Stands with projects in virtual reality;
  • Prize draws from event partners and much more!


Registration Start: 11:00

Location: st. Trifonovskaya, d. 57, p. 1 (metro station Rizhskaya), conference hall.
