Women's consultation - what is it?

Women's consultation is a medical institution in which a number of specialists provide outpatient medical care. Currently, Moscow has a network of state budgetary institutions, as well as centers that are attached to outpatient clinics and maternity hospitals. As an alternative to such complexes, there is a commercial orientation of the centers.





Work organization

In large cities, the best consultation on performance indicators is basic and, in addition to the standard list of tasks, performs the functions of an obstetric and gynecological center.

















Women's consultation is a complex in which work is arranged on a territorial-precinct basis. One obstetric site is equivalent to two therapeutic ones, and it is served by an obstetrician and an obstetrician-gynecologist. The norm of workload per specialist is 4.75 visits per hour.





Due to the principle of policiness, the doctor can constantly communicate with the therapeutic department and other specialists. This helps the timely registration of pregnant women and, if necessary, with various pathologies, joint dispensary monitoring.





A successful delivery largely depends on the timely admission to the antenatal clinic, registration, completeness and regularity of examination and observation of the expectant mother.









Tasks

That is the goal of these institutions:





  1. Providing quality obstetric and gynecological support to the population.
  2. The implementation of preventive measures that are aimed at preventing pregnancy pathologies, as well as the timely detection of gynecological diseases.
  3. Assisting women in providing social and legal protection in accordance with the law.
  4. Implementation of the practice of modern methods of diagnosis, prevention and treatment of pregnant women, as well as gynecological patients.
  5. Introduction of the current methods and forms of outpatient obstetric and gynecological support.

Structure

Women's consultation is an institution in which there is its own scheme of classrooms and complexes:





  • registry;
  • room for obstetrician-gynecologists to receive women in labor and pregnant women, as well as gynecological patients;
  • treatment room;
  • handling room;
  • laboratory;
  • an abortion room for short periods;
  • rooms where vascular surgeons, therapists, consultants and psychologists take in.













Pros and cons of state consultation

Such institutions, in comparison with private ones, have a number of advantages:





  • close to home;
  • is free;
  • you can get any necessary documents, provided that there are observed at least 12 consecutive weeks;
  • the doctor issues a referral to hospitalization in the maternity hospital, and if necessary, free consultation with a narrow focus doctor (nephrologist, urologist, cardiologist) will be provided.





















Despite all the positive aspects, there are also disadvantages:





  • permanent lines;
  • bureaucracy - starting from attaching to a consultation and ending with examinations of specialists who do not work in this institution, then a woman in labor can be sent to the other end of the city;
  • lack of confidence in the professionalism of working doctors;
  • in fact, very rarely you can choose a doctor yourself;
  • sometimes, there is rough treatment of patients;
  • you will have to forget about new equipment and comfortable conditions.

The main positive factor in pregnancy management in antenatal clinics is that it is all free. Of course, if there is no extra money, then there is nothing to choose from.





Required documents

Women's consultation is a center in which the expectant mother receives the necessary papers for pregnancy:





  1. Certificate of the fact of pregnancy. It may be needed in the registry office as a basis for expediting the registration of marriage or at work.
  2. Certificate of registration issued in the initial 12 weeks of pregnancy. It gives the woman a privilege to receive a one-time allowance from the state in the amount of 412 rubles.
  3. An exchange card is the main document that can be obtained at your site in the antenatal clinic, all the distinctive features of the course of pregnancy and the examinations are indicated there. Serves as a liaison between the hospital and a medical consultant. If there is no such booklet, then the woman is considered unexplored.
  4. The birth certificate is the leading document to enter the hospital for free. It is required to pay for the birth process at public expense. If it was lost, then it cannot be restored. In the case of planning a paid birth, it is not needed.
  5. Leaflet of temporary disability by childbirth and pregnancy. In other words, this is a sick leave. It may be needed in the early stages, if put on preservation, and after the advent of 30 weeks, a sick leave for 140 days will be issued. If the pregnancy is multiple, then the duration of the newsletter is longer, it can be obtained from 28 weeks to 194 days.

According to legislation, it is better to be registered at the 12th week of pregnancy.

















At this point, the woman fills out all the necessary documents and receives recommendations for further visits and analyzes. If a woman chooses a private consultation for herself, then you need to ask if the clinic can provide all the necessary documents, if not, then a parallel examination is required.
