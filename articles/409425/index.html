We are looking for speakers on the 6th DIY-mitap February 18





New Year's holidays have passed, everyone is slowly returning to work and school, and we are starting to prepare the sixth DIY-mitap. Traditionally, it will be held in the Moscow office of the Mail.Ru Group, and will again be in the atrium, where speakers, listeners and poster presentations can freely accommodate. Details and form of recording under the cut.



If you want to speak at the meeting as a speaker, please answer the questions in the form . If you have several topics, fill out the form several times, the most interesting one will be chosen by voting. Other topics can be discussed at the following meetings. And yes, we already have regulars who plan to perform for the third time in a row. :)



We are waiting for your application until January 26th. A further vote will be taken, the results of which will be published on February 2. After that, the speakers will have 2 weeks to prepare. According to the experience of previous meetings, we invite practically all the speakers to speak, so if you want to speak, but you need more than two weeks to prepare, start preparing!
