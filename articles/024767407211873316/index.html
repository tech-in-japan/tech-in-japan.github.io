Recipes

There are several easy to prepare borscht recipes that are great for young mothers. Is it possible for a nursing mother a green borsch? Sure. For women during lactation, recipes are perfect, where instead of cabbage, green sorrel is used or there is no fatty meat in the preparation of the broth.













Standard borsch

The broth will require lean meats: chicken or beef. Cook the broth should be about one and a half hours, constantly removing the resulting foam. After that, add the potatoes. Remember that we will not do the roasting. Chopped onions and grated carrots, put directly into the boiling broth. It is also better not to fry beets, but to stew in a certain amount of broth, adding lemon juice (3-5 teaspoons) or citric acid. After cooking, the meat is removed and cut into pieces. You can add some spices to the borsch. Importantly, do not choose spicy and overly spicy seasonings.













Green borsch

To prepare green borsch, you need a pound of lean meat, which should be boiled for about an hour, removing the foam. Dice potatoes, three carrots on a grater. Do not grill! Stew vegetables only a little in the pan, adding the broth.





Instead of the usual cabbage, we will use sorrel (two small bunches) and parsnip (one piece). Is it possible for a nursing mother borsch with sorrel? Need to! Sorrel contains a lot of useful substances, has an excellent effect on digestion and does not give allergic reactions. Sorrel is added at the end of cooking. When all the vegetables are ready, put it in a pan and cook the borscht for a couple of minutes. Serve such a green soup with sour cream or grated boiled egg.













Borsch without meat

To prepare a dietary borsch, you will need a standard set of vegetables. We put potatoes to cook. Stew carrots, onions, beets and tomatoes in water with seasoning. Cabbage is added last, when other vegetables are already stewed, boiled. By its readiness, the readiness of the dish is also determined. Is it possible for borsch to a nursing mother if there is no meat in it? It is possible and necessary. Vegetable soups cooked on water are much more beneficial for a woman during lactation than those with a fat content.





It seems to some mummies that green soup or borsch without meat is not tasty. And you try adding your favorite seasonings to the dish, chop finely more herbs, add a little low-fat sour cream. And the taste will change. If the baby is allergic to traditional borsch with beets, tomatoes, fatty broth and sour cream, then it is not necessary to categorically refuse borsch. There are many alternative options for cooking your favorite dish, which will be delicious for you and useful for the baby.
