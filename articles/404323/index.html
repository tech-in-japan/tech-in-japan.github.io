How textolite was forged and silicon tempered



Back in 1987, it all started with SIMM memory modules, which have become incredibly popular due to absolute reliability. Five years later (in 1993), the company began producing network devices and data warehouses, and a year later released the first drive of the DataTraveler family.





RAM Kingston and HyperX DDR2. Not just antiques, but well-known components among overclocking fans
