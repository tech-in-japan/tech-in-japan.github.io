OWASP Moscow (Russia) meetup 12/19 CFP



The last meeting of the Russian branch of OWASP this year will be held on December 6, 2019 at the Moscow office of BI.ZONE.



You will find interesting reports and discussions, a program and community plans for 2020, participation in community activities and the reorganization of local OWASP chapters.



OWASP



The Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) brings together large companies, educational organizations and individuals from around the world. Community members write articles and tutorials, create documentation, tools and technologies. All OWASP developments are publicly available.



Join



There are many ways to take part in the life of the Moscow (Russian) branch of OWASP:

  • Participation in any of the active projects (including writing utilities and documentation)
  • Proposal for new projects
  • Translation of existing projects into Russian
  • Assistance in organizing conferences and meetings
  • Promotion and support of the OWASP project as a whole
