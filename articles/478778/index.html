We deal with gifts



The question that tormented me during the beginning of the analysis of all Habra-ADM publications is what to present to my anonymous recipient of a gift. My original plan was quite selfish, and therefore try to be more global. You need to make a list of what was given before. We will assume that if the photo / description of the gift was published in a boast post, then he more or less liked it.







I note that I determined the gifts according to the description / photos presented in the comments. Naturally, the photos should have been available at the time of my analysis. In this regard, the statistics for 2012 and 2013 have a significant number of omissions (images were uploaded to third-party resources and are no longer available).







So, to begin with, I was able to identify 743 gifts received. Be careful, this is not the number of unique gifts, but the number of unique units / groups in gifts. That is, a package containing a postcard and sweets are two different gifts in my systematization. Such a step will help to better identify popular components that are only part of a larger gift.







What boasted the most? In the first place (by the sum for all seasons) sweets (they met 94 times). Next come postcards and letters (93 pieces), and in third place - books - 65 pieces.
