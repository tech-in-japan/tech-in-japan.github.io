How to cook rye bread?

To prepare a delicious dietary product, you need the following ingredients:





  • 500 ml of fatty sour milk.
  • 300 grams of whole grain flour.
  • One and a half cups of rye flour.
  • 2 tablespoons of flax seeds.
  • 2 tablespoons of oatmeal.
  • 1 egg
  • Olive oil.
  • A teaspoon of sea salt.
  • A teaspoon of brown sugar.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of soda.
  • For powder you need a little sesame and caraway seeds.

First add salt, sugar, soda to sour milk, mix the mixture. Next, beat the egg until a lumpy, lumpy liquid is obtained, pour into milk. Then add rye and whole grain flour, flax seeds, oatmeal to the mixture. Mix well. The dough for rye bread should be sticky and thick.













Put the billet for bread in a pan, cover with foil and leave to ripen for 40 minutes. After this time, put the dough on a board sprinkled with flour. Roll it so that you get a loaf of bread, sprinkle with sesame seeds and caraway seeds.





Bread must be baked in the oven, heated to 180 degrees for about half an hour. After cooking, you need to cut it and eat it like regular bread.
