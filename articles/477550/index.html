SPS-2019 at the Nuremberg Exhibition Center

Anyone interested would like to inform or simply remind you that today (Tuesday, November 26, 19), one of the largest international exhibitions in the field of industrial automation, SPS-2019, will start at the exhibition center in Nuremberg.



The exhibition takes place, as usual, for three days - and provides a lot of interesting material to get acquainted with the latest trends in the field of modern production in general - and in particular in the areas of industrial automation, robotics, digital technologies and all kinds of network trends (industrial Internet of things IIoT and other buns).



I highly insistently recommend it to students of industrial and non-industrial areas as a good overview material to get acquainted with the subject, to professionals - to deepen and just interested - to expand horizons. :)
