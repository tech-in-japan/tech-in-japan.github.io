I am lazy doing backups, or the Fast Way



If backups were done for Rook, then the recovery procedure becomes much simpler and boils down to the following:



  1. Scale to zero deployment of the Rook operator;
  2. We remove all deployments except the Rook operator;
  3. Restore all secrets and ConfigMaps from backup;
  4. Restore the contents of the /var/lib/rook/mon-*



    on the nodes;
  5. Restore (if suddenly lost) CRD CephCluster



    , CephFilesystem



    , CephBlockPool



    , CephNFS



    , CephObjectStore



    ;
  6. Scale the deployment of the Rook operator back to 1.


Useful Tips



Make backups!



And in order to avoid situations when you need to recover from them:



  1. Before large-scale work with the cluster, consisting in server reboots, scale the Rook operator to zero so that it does not do too much.
  2. On monitors, add nodeAffinity in advance.
  3. Pay attention to the pre- setting of timeouts ROOK_MON_HEALTHCHECK_INTERVAL



    and ROOK_MON_OUT_TIMEOUT



    .
