How to replace sugar with proper nutrition: product list

During the diet, of course, the question arises of what is better to replace sugar with proper nutrition, as this is a rather harmful product, which not only leads to obesity, but also provokes many diseases. There are many different sweeteners, but not all of them are safe for health, and some even provoke cancer. That is why you need to approach their choice with all responsibility.





What is sugar and how dangerous is it









Sugar is used in the preparation of many products and various dishes, in particular in convenience foods, confectionery, jams, jams, pastries and much more. This product is quite dangerous, since it is rapidly absorbed into the blood and leads to a sharp increase, and then an instant drop in glucose levels. This can lead to extra pounds, and also increases the risk of diabetes. In addition, the particles of sugar remaining on the teeth contribute to the growth of bacteria, which provokes tooth decay. The negative consequences of its consumption include:





  • heart problems;
  • high blood pressure;
  • decreased immunity;
  • fungal infections;
  • nervousness.

That is why nutritionists recommend consuming no more than 10-12 teaspoons of this product daily. Refined sugar is made from sugarcane, however, due to the heat treatment, as well as its bleaching, beneficial substances are destroyed. If possible, you should try to completely eliminate it from your diet and switch to more healthy products.









How can I replace sugar

Sugar can cause very serious harm to the body, as this product contains many empty calories. That is why it is necessary to limit its consumption. Knowing how to replace sugar with proper nutrition, you can choose more wholesome foods. There are natural and synthetic sweeteners. The most famous natural sweeteners are sorbitol and xylitol.





Xylitol is made from birch bark, onion husks, corn cobs. Sorbitol was originally made from mountain ash, and is now made from starch. The calorie content of these sweeteners is the same as that of sugar, their sweetness is also approximately equal. However, do not consume them in large quantities, as this can provoke an upset stomach.













Many are interested in how to replace sugar with a healthy diet. The list of products is quite large. In particular, the most popular products are:





  • honey;
  • Jerusalem artichoke syrup;
  • stevia;
  • agave syrup ;
  • maple syrup.

There are also synthetic sweeteners, such as aspartame, saccharin, cyclamate. They are much sweeter than sugar, therefore they are consumed in minimal quantities, they do not affect the state of health in any way and do not lead to obesity. However, it is worth noting that they have low heat resistance, therefore, are not suitable for baking.













The benefits of honey and a good sugar substitute

How to replace sugar with proper nutrition is a very important question, since you need to consume a sufficient amount of carbohydrates per day, but do not harm your health. It is quite possible to consume honey, as it is a natural product that is healthy. However, it is worth remembering that you need to choose only a high-quality natural product, since otherwise you can only harm the body.













Honey contains useful trace elements, most of which are in human blood. This product is very sweet, so when you add it to different dishes or tea, be sure to consider the proportions. When used correctly, this product can be called its most useful and valuable sugar substitute.

The benefits and features of maple syrup

Being interested in how to replace sugar with proper nutrition, you can use maple syrup, which is made from maple juice. It is a concentrated, viscous, sweet mixture, so you need to limit the consumption of syrup, as it contributes to weight gain. It can also be used for baking, as a good alternative to regular sugar.
