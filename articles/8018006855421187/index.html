There are many other options for using this substance, so you can use far from any flaxseed oil as a dietary supplement or for treatment.

Of course, there is some harm to the body from the use of this substance. Especially obvious are the harm and benefits of flaxseed oil on an empty stomach. Contraindications to the use of oil

The benefits of flaxseed oil for women are undoubtedly quite large. However, there is a category of people who for one reason or another are forbidden to use this tool for treatment. These include:

How to store oil

Like all oily liquids, flaxseed oil should be stored in the refrigerator so that its quality does not change and a rancid aroma does not appear. Before use, shake the bottle with oil, and then pour the required amount into a spoon. The remaining liquid should be well closed with a lid and put in the refrigerator until the next intake.
