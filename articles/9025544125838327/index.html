How to eat less? You can achieve the desired in several ways, you will need patience, desire and a certain willpower, especially at the initial stage, until you get used to the new way of eating and living.

  • Before eating, for half an hour, you need to drink a glass of plain still water. It will fill the stomach and satisfy the feeling of hunger.   • Before eating, take a walk through the fresh air, and for the most motivated and purposeful people, it is recommended to do light exercises with deep breaths and exhalations, as oxygenation dulls the feeling of hunger.
  • Learn to eat from a small dish, with a small spoon, carefully chewing food and enjoying its taste - a feeling of fullness will come faster.
