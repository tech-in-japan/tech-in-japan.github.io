What is Bolt



Bolt develops services for calling taxis and private drivers, as well as renting electric scooters, scooters and motorbikes. As of August 2019, the number of Bolt users is 30 million.



In 2019, Bolt was included in the list of 27 European startups that have achieved unicorn status, that is, companies with a capitalization of more than $ 1 billion. The service operates in more than 30 countries, its headquarters are located in Tallinn.
