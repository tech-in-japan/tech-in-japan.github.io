How now works



Everything is as before: the essence of working with a gadget comes down to a few simple steps:



  • Put on
  • Press, fixing the pose
  • Feel the three vibration feedback (posture - fixed)
  • Watch your posture and straighten up when you forget


In order to observe the changes and improvements - just the application is needed, where you will be able to observe the increasing interval of ‚Äúgood‚Äù posture day after day, so that later on you can completely abandon the assistant corrector.



Lumo Lift is not attached to the body, but to clothes with a magnet, and therefore the clothes must fit snugly. This could be underwear, a shirt, a shirt under a jacket or something like that. Otherwise you may not feel the vibration. The gadget is designed to be worn on the chest.







Lumo Lift works as a corrector when you sit, and sitting posture is the greatest threat to your back. However, we must understand that the gadget works both forward and backward, so that leaning back on the back of the working chair, you will also feel the signal from the corrector.
