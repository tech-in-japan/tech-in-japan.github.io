Acoustic systems for open-type rooms

It is no secret to anyone that in recent years the number of requests for equipping office space of the "open" type has increased - the western version of building office spaces. On the one hand - it is budgetary in terms of company money - to put all employees in one room instead of allocating each to the office, and, accordingly, if costs began to be reduced, then why stop. Therefore, they try to make repairs as simple as possible, as a result of which the ceiling space is not decorated in any way - such a fashionable direction. Usually it is painted in dark colors, but not always, and all communications are left open. Apparently this option is much simpler and cheaper both from the service side and from the budget side. Additionally, a feeling of high ceilings and more space is created. Although this is not always the case.
