Diet therapy is a therapeutic food. Principles, Significance and Efficiency

The concept of a diet for women is usually associated with losing extra pounds, which they seek to get rid of closer to the summer. However, its usefulness is not only to give its figure an external appeal. In fact, this is primarily a therapeutic event. Diet therapy is the basis on which to restore human health in diseases of most internal organs and systems. The stomach, kidneys, liver, even autoimmune diseases are treated with proper nutrition. Based on the state of the body and the disease, the doctor needs to adjust it every time. For convenience, experts have developed diet tables.









Features

Diet therapy is a prerequisite for recovery. Not for nothing in the sanatoriums pay special attention to this. It must be agreed with a specialist. Sometimes diet is the main method. In another case, it can act as an auxiliary tool. And to be more precise, properly selected food creates the conditions for treatment to be effective. According to individual indications, in addition to the main diet, fasting days are practiced: dairy, curd, watermelon, apple.









The Importance of Health Nutrition

Diet therapy is not a measure for losing weight. Nutrition does not imply a restriction on fats, proteins and carbohydrates. It is necessary for the normalization of metabolic processes, the restoration of all body functions.





But you need to remember that diet therapy is primarily a therapeutic diet. It should be built taking into account the localization of the disease, the nature of the process, fatness and lifestyle. It is necessary to take into account both concomitant diseases and complications. With body weight below normal and progressive weight loss, diet therapy involves enhanced nutrition. But you should not resort to it in case of violation of the activity of the stomach, liver and intestines.









The founder of domestic therapy

The first to pay attention to the importance of nutrition in the treatment of certain diseases M.I. Pevzner. He studied the effects of foods on the body and made the right conclusions. And today, experts in the field of dietetics confirm that nutrition in a certain way affects the whole body and individual organs and systems.





That is, what we eat may be a medicine or poison. By changing the content in the food system of certain products, you can affect the course of the disease, accelerate recovery and improve the patient's condition. These principles formed the basis of this direction.









Modern trends

It should be noted that the principles of diet therapy remain the same for all times. But the views of doctors are changing. Previously, diet therapy was practiced, in which the main emphasis was on excess nutrition. Such a measure led to an overload of the digestive organs. And this is an increase in the risk of allergic reactions. The only justified method is the inclusion of a large amount of protein. Without exacerbation, the diet is more like a normal healthy diet. The table is no frills, but without excess fat. Based on the foregoing, the importance of diet therapy is very difficult to overestimate.





But the use of large amounts of fat is not recommended. In this case, the body will direct forces to its processing or storage in reserve. And now they are needed for another. An exception may be the need to add butter to the diet, which is especially important for tuberculosis.









The need for individual selection

The effectiveness of diet therapy will depend on how well it is chosen. Therefore, only the most experienced and competent specialists work in the sanatorium. Diet is needed for various diseases. Moreover, the effectiveness of treatment depends on how the patient eats during recovery. Usually the attending physician gives his recommendations, which the patient should follow. The more severe the disease, the more complete the diet should be.





It is especially important to follow the rules of dietary nutrition in the pathology of the digestive tract and metabolism. Very often it is the diet that gives positive results when other methods of treatment are ineffective. If we are talking about chronic diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, then reasonable restrictions will have to be observed throughout life.





Key factors

Features of diet therapy will depend on a number of points:





  • The correct selection of products, taking into account the composition.
  • Quantitative and qualitative calculations are very important. That is, the content of BZHU, vitamins and minerals.
  • How the dish will be prepared is a fundamental factor.
  • Caloric value of food is taken into account without fail.

That is, the diet should be complete. A competent nutritionist takes into account the following points:





  • A strict diet cannot be long. This is usually an emergency measure, which is necessary only during the acute phase. After this, the diet is expanded, since a strict restriction creates a deficiency of nutrients.
  • The therapeutic diet is gradually expanding.

Correction of the diet is important at different stages of the disease. At the earliest, this helps relieve intoxication and pain, create the prerequisites for recovery. At later stages, it provides the body with the forces necessary for recovery.
