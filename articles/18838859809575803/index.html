Blood Type Diet Theory

The author of the book, who recommends eating only blood type foods, bases his ideas, as already mentioned, on evolutionary theory. Peter D'Adamo believes that in the course of human development, the composition of the blood changed depending on nutrition.





So, the very first people on Earth had the same group - the first. They were engaged in hunting and gathering, they consumed mainly protein food, that is, the meat of animals killed by themselves. The next stage of evolution was marked by the development of agriculture. The menu of people has become more diverse, plant foods have appeared, which changed the composition of the blood. So the second group appeared.













Then mankind began to domesticate wild animals. As a result, it became possible to eat dairy products, which led to the following changes in the composition of the blood.
