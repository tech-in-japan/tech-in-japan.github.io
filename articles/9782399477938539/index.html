How to make juice

This is pretty simple. You will need a good root. At the store, check its quality. It must be tight and resilient. If the root has been warm for a long time, it becomes fibrous and soft. In this case, it is better not to buy it.





To prepare fresh ginger juice, it is necessary to wash the root and peel it with a sharp knife. At the same time, try to remove only the thinnest layer so as not to send the most valuable essential oils to the bin. They just hide in the upper layer of pulp.





Now it is necessary to grind it in any way possible: using a juicer or a meat grinder, on the most ordinary grater. The main thing is that the pulp turn into slurry. Spread it on cheesecloth and squeeze. Put the remaining cake in a jar. It can be used to brew tea, as part of the essential oils preserved in the fibers and go into the drink.





The resulting juice has a very sharp and burning taste. It is not recommended to use it in its pure form. It is best to dilute with vegetable, fruit juices or just water.
