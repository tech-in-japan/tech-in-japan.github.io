Mistakes



Along with fears, we eradicate problems with the legal component, which pushes procurement participants to commit violations in the use of electronic signatures. For example, a top mistake - the head transfers the ‚Äúflash drive‚Äù with the electronic signature to his subordinate. Most often, this is one of his deputies, assistants or secretary. While the employee is working as it should, there are no problems. Until a certain point, this is not a problem. When the manager says that he has not authorized something, the problem begins. Because the "flash drive" is a legitimate means of identifying him as a signatory. And all personal data on it is also stored.



It is impossible to copy such ES from the media without special technical means (but you can issue two copies at once, if necessary), but I also encountered cases when a dismissed employee simply left her (not all managers understand that it is necessary to revoke a certificate or take away the medium) , and when just a person authorized something that he himself had no right to.



The second feature is now so technologically arranged that for registration in the Unified Register of Procurement Participants (ERUZ, introduced on January 1, 2019), the head must be registered first from the legal entity.
