Egg donation cost: how to become a donor, description of the procedure, review of clinics, approximate cost

Egg donation may be required in case of depletion of their own ovarian reserve (stock of follicles capable of further development). Women during menopause, with pathologies of ovarian development, after radiation or chemotherapy, usually can not have children, but if you want, in this case, you can use the donation program. For a donor, this is an opportunity to help someone find the happiness of motherhood.









Egg donation in the USA and Europe

In America and Europe, the fertility rate among mature women remains stably high. With age, the number of own eggs decreases significantly, so the solution is IVF with its own or donor biological material. The cost of an egg donation in the USA for a donor depends on many factors. The average IVF price is about 5 thousand dollars, and the donor can pay from 15 to 100 thousand. In the USA, this service sector is based on the principles of the free market.





In many European countries, the attitude towards donation is ambiguous. This is permitted in France and Belgium, and is prohibited by law in Italy, Austria and Switzerland. In Sweden and the Netherlands, the clinic is required to provide prospective parents or the child with complete information about the donor. In the UK, the program is legal, but the cost of an egg donation is only 250 pounds. In Greece, donation is free. You can only rely on compensation for wages and transportation costs.













Donor programs in Russia

In Russia, either the recipient's relatives or anonymous women can become egg donors. Such programs are not banned at the legislative level, and donors are supposed to pay remuneration.





Egg donor requirements

Not every woman can help a barren couple to feel the happiness of motherhood. An egg donor must meet certain criteria and not have health problems. Women who already have one healthy child or several children can participate in the program. The age of the donor should be in the range from 20-21 to 32-34 years.





A woman should have a normal physique (there is no excess weight or lack of kilograms), physical and psychological health. Patients with hepatitis A, diabetes mellitus, some chronic and any acute diseases, including gynecological, who underwent surgery on the ovaries and mammary glands, are not allowed to donate. The donor should not have pronounced phenotypic traits.

Medical examination of a donor

A medical examination includes: a coagulogram, blood tests for hepatitis B and C, syphilis, HIV, general and biochemical, determination of the group and Rh factor, general urine analysis, cytology of scraping from the cervix, smears from the cervical canal and urethra, determination of purity vagina, fluorography, electrocardiogram. You need to visit a psychiatrist and get an opinion on normal mental health. According to the results of the examination, a conclusion is issued by the genetics, obstetrician-gynecologist and therapist.





Ovarian Stimulation Program

The essence of the donation program is to stimulate the maturation of several eggs in the ovary. To do this, a special hormone therapy course is prescribed, after which the egg will be collected. If the material will not undergo cryopreservation, then the goal of hormone therapy is also to synchronize the menstrual cycles of the donor and recipient.





During stimulation, the donor is advised to refrain from sexual intercourse and physical activity, to completely eliminate bad habits. It is not recommended to limit yourself to food, but you need to eat properly. It is necessary to exclude coffee and not go to baths, saunas. Before the puncture of the eggs, it is recommended not to eat or drink after midnight.
