Coffee Mins

The key to harmony is separate nutrition and optimal physical activity. This formula has long been learned by the most active fighters for an ideal figure. But for those who do not want to go jogging in the morning, the French who understand a lot about beauty have come up with an effective tool that does not require absolutely any sacrifices - Mins coffee. This is a unique product that appeared on the Russian market only a few years ago. Prior to that, it was already represented in many countries and gained there very steady popularity.





Today , Mins Slimming Coffee is the most progressive product that helps to lose weight. It can be consumed regardless of the diet, it is compatible with all food products. Therefore, in order to start losing weight, you do not need to limit yourself to food, resort to special diets, but simply add a few cups of the aromatic drink to the daily menu. One of the features of this product is that it begins to break down fats even at the moment of their direct entry into the body. Also, the product effectively fights against the accumulated fats, which it quickly breaks down and removes.





Mins coffee contains green tea extract, due to which it has the property of natural weight loss. It has a great taste of real coffee.





Using coffee daily, you can lose up to five kilograms per month. The mechanism of action of the product is based on its ability to activate the body's production of stress hormones, which reduce appetite and lead to weight loss due to a slowdown in calorie intake.









Mins coffee consists of 93% instant specialty coffee and a highly concentrated extract of green tea (about 7%), a small amount of rhubarb and pineapple extracts, as well as vitamin B3 (niacin). The tea extract contains the most valuable macro- and microelements necessary for the effective cleansing of the body and the fight against extra pounds.





Mins Slimming Coffee is made up of specially selected ingredients that help lower blood sugar levels. This leads to the effect of using the fat reserves in the body. At the same time, there is a suppression of hunger, which helps to reduce appetite, and this is very important to ensure weight loss.





Green tea extract also has a beneficial effect on digestion, strengthens the immune system, lowers cholesterol and slows down the aging process. The ability to stimulate enzymes leads to the fact that the process of removing fat is accelerated several times. Green tea improves mental activity, promotes the accumulation and intensive absorption of vitamin C, has a good choleretic effect.





Caffeine helps regulate the processes of excitation that occur in the brain, which leads to an acceleration of metabolic processes. The substances contained in coffee slow down the absorption of lipids and carbohydrates, as a result of which the amount of calories entering the body decreases.





Coffee Mins must be brewed at the rate of: a glass of boiling water for one serving bag. It is allowed to add sugar and skim milk to the drink . Every day you need to drink at least one cup. No side effects have been identified. It is not recommended to drink coffee for children under sixteen years of age and adults over sixty, pregnant and lactating women, people with a heart condition, high blood pressure and hormonal diseases. Store coffee in a cool, dry place.





The nutritional value of this type of coffee is 349 Kcal per 100 grams of dry product.
