When is it necessary to see a doctor?

Often, rashes indicate the presence of a serious disease. Specialist consultation is necessary in the following cases:





  • Acne does not disappear within 1 week. It does not matter if they remain externally unchanged, decrease in size or, on the contrary, progress.
  • Rashes are accompanied by the appearance of alarming symptoms. These include: itching and burning in the genital area, swelling, unpleasant odor, unusual discharge.
  • If the formation of red acne is accompanied by peeling of nearby tissues.
  • Rashes are watery.
  • A single formation or multiple acne appeared after sexual contact with a new partner. It is especially worthwhile to beware if in the process of intimacy a barrier contraceptive has not been used.
  • Against the background of acne formation, body temperature rises and lymph nodes increase.
  • At the site of the rash, wounds remain that do not heal for a long time.
  • Acne causes painful sensations of a high degree of intensity.

In the presence of these conditions, only a doctor will be able to accurately determine the cause of the rashes and draw up the most effective treatment regimen.

After the survey, the doctor examines the patient in a gynecological chair. He evaluates the type, shape, size, soreness of acne, the condition of the skin and mucous membrane. Based on the data received, the doctor prescribes a set of studies to identify the causes of rashes.

Treatment

The treatment regimen directly depends on the cause of the appearance of rashes. Drug treatment of acne on the genitals of a woman consists in taking or externally using anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial agents. To prevent the appearance of rashes in an intimate place, you need to regularly visit a doctor and treat all identified diseases in a timely manner.
