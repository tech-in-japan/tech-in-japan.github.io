What delicious diabetic dishes to cook?

Diabetes mellitus is a disease of the endocrine system, which results in an increase in blood sugar. This is due to the fact that insulin levels are reduced (more complex forms of diabetes are observed when it is completely absent). If you do not treat the disease in a timely manner, this can lead to the failure of various systems in the body, as well as lead to metabolic disorders.





Types of diabetes

Diabetes mellitus can be divided into three main types:





  1. The first type of diabetes. This is the most complex form of the disease in which the pancreas produces too little insulin, or does not produce it at all. Typically, such a diagnosis is made to people under the age of 20. The exact causes of this disease are not yet known. Some are inclined to believe that it appears due to a genetic predisposition. The pancreas ceases to function, so it produces too few hormones. Others believe that diabetes appears due to a viral disease that completely affects the pancreas.
  2. The second type of diabetes is the most common form of the disease these days. Appears at a fairly mature age in people who are overweight. This type of disease manifests itself in approximately 90% of all cases of the disease. Its difference from type 1 diabetes is that the pancreas produces enough insulin, but the body does not use it correctly.
  3. Gestational diabetes. This phenomenon is observed in about 4% of women on the planet during the second trimester of pregnancy. Its difference from the previous forms can be called the fact that it disappears after the birth of a child.




Causes of the disease

Type 1 diabetes mellitus develops due to an autoimmune process, which appeared due to malfunctions of the immune system. This leads to the production of antibodies in the body that affect pancreatic cells, destroying it. Typically, type 1 diabetes occurs due to infection (rubella, hepatitis, smallpox, and the like). In particular, the disease progresses rapidly if the patient is predisposed to this disease.









The risk of progression of type 2 diabetes is in people who regularly take dietary supplements, which include selenium. In addition, obesity and heredity are the main factors that result in type 2 diabetes.





Consequence of the disease

Regardless of the reasons that triggered the onset of diabetes, the result is the following: the human body cannot fully absorb glucose. Sugar is the main source of human energy, because its excess is stored in the liver and muscles, and then can be used in critical situations. With diabetes, glucose is not absorbed, but enters the bloodstream and circulates with it throughout the body. As a result, muscle tissue and organs are destroyed. Therefore, fats act as energy in diabetics. When they are split, toxic substances are formed in the body that adversely affect the brain, as well as metabolism.





Diabetes treatment

Any form of diabetes cannot be completely cured. The patient's condition can be somewhat improved by the implementation of special measures.





  • Injections of insulin every day (for patients with type 1 diabetes). The substance is sold in special syringes, making injections very easy. Using special strips, it is necessary to control the glucose content in urine and blood.
  • The use of pills that help lower the sugar level in the body. In this way, people with type 2 diabetes use it. If the disease progresses, the attending physician should prescribe insulin injections.
  • Special exercises that patients should do. In addition, if there is excess weight, it is necessary to get rid of it for medicinal purposes.
  • A special diet that excludes the use of sugar, alcohol, sweet fruits. Delicious dishes for diabetics should be prepared according to all requirements. It is advisable to eat 4-5 times a day and in small portions. You can eat foods that contain sweeteners.

















How to eat a diabetic?

As mentioned above, dishes for the diabetic should contain a minimum of sugar. It is important that they are quite nutritious. After all, sugar is the main source of energy for the body for the whole day. And if it does not come with food, then the body can noticeably weaken. Patients should carefully begin the selection of products. In any case, when preparing a dish for a diabetic, it is necessary to make it as dietary as possible. When composing the menu, several important factors should be taken into account: the type of disease (first or second), age, weight, presence or absence of physical activity.













Nutrition for Type 1 Diabetics

If a person has a disease - type 1 diabetes, then he needs to give up foods high in carbohydrates. Only sometimes you can consume carbohydrates, which are quickly absorbed by the body. This mainly concerns children, because it is difficult for them to completely abandon such products. However, parents should clearly monitor when and how much the child ate carbohydrate-rich foods. It is even advisable to keep a record in order to control blood sugar, as well as insulin in a timely manner.





Dishes for type 1 diabetics include foods such as:





  • steamed meat without salt;
  • boiled fish without salt;
  • black bread;
  • boiled eggs;
  • vegetables;
  • fruits: citrus fruits and currants;
  • dairy products (cottage cheese, sour cream with low fat content and cheeses);
  • chicory;
  • porridge;
  • vegetable salads;
  • rosehip tea.

In this case, the patient should completely abandon the use of coffee, alcohol, fried foods, flour products.

If you are overweight?

Recipes for overweight diabetics of type 2 should be light, with a minimum carbohydrate content. In addition, patients should consolidate their results by performing physical exercises. The complex does not need to be chosen independently: it must be developed in conjunction with the attending physician, taking into account the peculiarities of the course of the disease.

  • beans - about 400 g;
  • onion;
  • flour;
  • lemon juice;
  • garlic - 1 clove;
  • tomato;
  • greens to taste.

Below is how a dish for diabetics is prepared (with a photo that is under the recipe).
