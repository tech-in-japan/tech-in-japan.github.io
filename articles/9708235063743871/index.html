The difference between vegans and vegetarians. What do vegetarians and vegans eat?

Recently, global trends have shifted towards a healthy lifestyle and proper nutrition. People thought about the ecology of the world in which we live, about the purity of the foods we eat, about the relationship of man with the environment as a whole. On this wave, two such trends arose as vegetarianism and veganism. More and more people are choosing a similar lifestyle. What is it - a tribute to fashion, a lifelong diet or a conscious position?





Vegetarians and vegans. Who are they?

According to the mistaken opinion of most people, these are just people who excluded meat and animal products from their diet. Actually this is not true. The difference between vegans and vegetarians is mainly in their fundamental position. For example, vegans do not recognize any exploitation of animals in general, and vegetarians oppose the killing of animals for the benefit of man. This is reflected not only in nutrition.

















Vegan will never go to the circus, the zoo, will not wear woolen clothes, ride horses at the hippodrome, as this is nothing more than the exploitation of animals for the sake of human entertainment. Vegetarians are calm about such moments. But here you will not find a fur coat or leather boots in their wardrobes, as well as other household items for which animals had to be killed. However, vegans are in solidarity with them.









Food

Now let's talk about nutrition. The main difference between vegans and vegetarians is that the former do not eat any food of animal origin at all. That is, they do not eat meat, seafood and fish. So what do vegetarians eat? List of products: milk, eggs, dairy products and honey, that is, that food for which animals were not killed.

















There are those who consume only eggs or milk from animal foods. They are called respectively - ovo-vegetarians and lacto-vegetarians.

Such restrictions can affect both health and physical fitness. There are still small children, infants who need a varied, high-calorie diet. Consider how vegans eat. The menu for every day is not as monotonous as it might seem at first glance. Firstly, do not confuse veganism and raw food diet.

















Yes, the basis of the vegan diet is vegetables, fruits, nuts, herbs and roots, but from them there are many delicious dishes. Analogues of food of animal origin

In addition, marketers of large food concerns in order to increase profits and expand their assortment are constantly conducting research on what vegans eat. The list of food products that replace vegans and vegetarians with their analogues of animal origin is regularly updated.

















The real find was soy protein. It produces a lot of food and convenience foods. There is soy meat, milk, and even tofu cheese. A favorite vegan dish is hummus - chickpea puree, whipped with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, paprika and sesame paste.

Advice for those who decide to change something in life

The main thing that is worth remembering to anyone who has decided to radically change their life and, according to their views, the nutrition system, you can not dramatically change your diet. Meat, fish and other vegan-prohibited foods must be removed from the menu gradually, replacing them with equivalent vegetable counterparts. Be sure to monitor the balance of proteins and fats, the daily calorie intake should not be sharply reduced.





Before switching to a new nutrition system, conduct a complete cleansing of the body. Take a course of strengthening immunity with vitamins. To become a vegan, you need to be prepared for this both mentally and physically. Therefore, visit a doctor and find out if you have any contraindications to such a lifestyle.
