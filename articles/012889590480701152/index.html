  • childhood;
  • second and third trimesters of pregnancy;
  • fragility of blood vessels and frequent bleeding;
  • liver disease and gastrointestinal tract;
  • chronic hypertension;
  • diabetes;
  • heart attacks and strokes;
  • gallstones

  • taking potent drugs.
