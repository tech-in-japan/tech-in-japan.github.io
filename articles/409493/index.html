New Sony: Xperia XA2 Ultra, Xperia XA2 and Xperia L2

At the international exhibition Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas, Sony has presented updated versions of three smartphones in the middle price segment. Focused on a young and active audience, Xperia XA2 Ultra and Xperia XA2 are a new generation of frameless smartphones with an emphasis on content consumption and mobile photography, Xperia L2 is the most affordable solution from a Japanese manufacturer.







Sony Xperia XA2 Ultra and Xperia XA2 are similar in appearance and technical characteristics. The design of the new models continues to develop the previously set trend for screens without side frames and the brand concept of symmetry for the Loop Surface case. The main change was the transfer of the fingerprint scanner from the power button to the back side of the aluminum cases. In terms of ergonomics, this is convenient, since the sensor circle is in the usual place - where the index finger usually goes. In addition, the solution looks aesthetically attractive, the scanner module repeats the circle of the main camera and fits perfectly into the exterior of smartphones.





Photo: Sony Xperia XA2 Ultra Color Solutions



Sony Xperia XA2 Ultra and Xperia XA2 are based on a productive eight-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 630 processor with a frequency of 2.2 GHz paired with an Adreno 508 graphics accelerator. content, the Xperia XA2 got a 5.2 inch display. The amount of RAM in the Xperia XA2 is 3 GB, and in the XA2 Ultra - 4 GB. For data storage, 32 GB is provided with the possibility of expansion by microSD memory cards up to 256 GB. The battery capacity is 3300 mAh and 3580 mAh, respectively, there is support for fast charging standard QuickCharge 3.0. In addition, smartphones use STAMINA technology to increase battery life, Qnovo Adaptive Charging and Battery Care adaptive charging, which reduces battery wear.

The front camera in Xperia XA2 has a resolution of 8 megapixels and a viewing angle of 120 degrees. In turn, Xperia XA2 Ultra gives you even more opportunities to shoot self portraits at the expense of a dual module. Users can take pictures with a 16 megapixel camera with optical stabilization and an 88-degree view, when you need to get the most detailed portrait or frame in low light conditions, or switch to a module with wide-angle optics with a 120-degree view for group selfie photography, or when you need to show more background

Sony Xperia XA2 Ultra and Xperia XA2 also support Smart Amplifier, ClearAudio + and Clear Bass audio enhancement technologies, connecting to wireless speakers and headphones using the latest Bluetooth 5.0 module with support for aptX and A2DP profiles. Smartphones also have an NFC wireless module installed for pairing devices and using the Android Pay service, a dual-band Wi-Fi 802.11 a / b / g / n module and A-GPS navigation, GLONASS. The wired connection is made using a USB Type-C balanced connector.



In Russia, Sony Xperia XA2 Ultra and Xperia XA2 smartphones will be available in black, silver, blue and pink.



Sony Xperia L2 also received an updated design with a fingerprint scanner on the back, a 5.5-inch HD screen and powerful components. The smartphone is built on a MediaTek MT6737T quad-core processor with a frequency of 1.5 GHz, the amount of RAM is 3 GB, and a constant 32 GB with the possibility of expansion by microSD memory cards.
