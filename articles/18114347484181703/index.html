Contraindications

Like all berries, strawberries have not only benefits for the body, but also harm. The restrictions on its admission include:





  1. Predisposition to an allergic reaction.
  2. Taking medications for high blood pressure, because sharing strawberries with them inhibits kidney function.
  3. Diseases of the digestive tract, because the berry has an irritating effect.
  4. Hypertonicity of the uterus during pregnancy, due to the tonic effect of strawberries.
  5. Breastfeeding an infant up to 7 months of age, because allergens can enter its body.




To avoid negative effects on the body, you must do the following:





  • wash the berries thoroughly under running water to rid their porous structure of the pollen of the plant, and also after contact with the ground - from helminth eggs and bacteria;
  • mix strawberries best with dairy products, which allows oxalic acid to bind to calcium, which helps strengthen tooth enamel;
  • Do not eat berries on an empty stomach, so as not to cause irritation of the stomach;
  • observe the measure in the use of strawberries, in order to avoid exacerbation of diseases of the genitourinary system and joints.

In case of external use of berries in the form of masks and oils, cancel their use in such unpleasant situations:





  1. Open wounds and inflammation on the skin.
  2. Fever.
  3. Psoriasis and eczema.

Strawberries should be used with caution in people who are allergic to it.
