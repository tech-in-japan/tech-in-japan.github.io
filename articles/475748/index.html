Alfa Bank School of System Analysis



Kirill Kapranov



With analysts, the thing is about the same as with other good specialists. It seems like we need people with experience, and in principle, satisfying the criteria. Moreover, if such are found, then they most often have to be retrained. Which is not as fun as it seems.
