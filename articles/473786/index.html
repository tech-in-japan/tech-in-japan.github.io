Registration for the hackathon in Riga is ending. Prize - short-term training at the PhysTech

On November 15-16, 2019, the Baltic Sea Digital Event, an international business hackathon, will be held at the University of Latvia (Riga).



The hackathon is focused on the use of the following technologies: distributed registry systems, big data, wireless, industrial Internet, virtual and augmented reality.



Hurry: the online registration of participants closes on October 31, i.e. TOMORROW, at 23:59. You have a little more than a day to apply !







Hackathon organizers: RUDN University and MIPT on the instructions of Rossotrudnichestvo, together with partner companies: Samsung IT Academy Samsung (Russia), IBS (Russia), Qube (Sweden), CANEA (Sweden), Stockholm School of Economics, Association of Electronic Money and Money Transfer Market Participants (Russia).



It is important that the organizers pay for the accommodation and full board of the participant. They will also send an invitation to make it easier to get a visa.



During the hackathon, in addition to working on the assignment, there will be lectures and master classes: “Rise and fall of banks: how deep digitalization affects the industry”, “Digital organization double”, a lecture from the curator of the track on mobile development of the Samsung IT Academy project Andrei Limasov, and of course, buns and snacks from the organizers.



Nominations will be as follows:



  • The best solution to an applied problem in finance and trade
  • The best socially significant decision
  • The best IT service project to expand the range of public services
  • The best educational solution, including gamification


Rather apply! As already mentioned, the deadline is tomorrow, that is, on the night of October to November 2019. Then it will be like this: a week to complete the task in an online format, and then participants from the full-time stage will be selected from the past.



What about the prizes?



  • The main team prize: Short-term training in programming courses of the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology with payment for travel and accommodation!
  • Prizes in nominations: Short-term internships in partner companies and other valuable prizes.
