Diabetes. Nutrition is crucial

Diabetes mellitus is a disease in which the sugar entering the body is not digested enough. The pancreas in this case does not produce the hormone insulin in sufficient quantities. Diabetes mellitus most often develops in older people, as well as in those who are overweight.













To date, there are a large number of different drugs that can lower the blood sugar of a sick person. If a person has diabetes, nutrition is crucial. If the disease manifests itself in mild and moderate form, then dietary treatment is necessary. In case of severe illness, the diet should be combined with medication.
