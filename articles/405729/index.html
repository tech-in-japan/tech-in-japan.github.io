Perhaps, one of the adherents of acoustic equipment probably at least once, but heard about the company Nyne - the American brand with the European spirit and 30 years of experience in engineering and design work in the field of stationary and portable sound. Now the company focuses on high-quality wireless speakers and set its sights on a much wider market, so it's time to get acquainted with this brand closer, especially in Russia, it just came out.



Three decades of development in Silicon Valley were not in vain - Nyne uses all its achievements to produce the largest and loudest Bluetooth-speakers on the market, which are resistant to bad weather. The brand is a joint offspring of several "veterans" of the Valley, so the device produces a decent level. To make it easier to choose from the range, the manufacturer initially divided its speakers into three series - X, C and P (almost like Mercedes Benz, only cost much less).



Serial C is positioned by the company as speakers mainly for indoor parties, but at the same time they can easily be taken out onto a summer terrace, and the sound will be no worse than, as the manufacturer claims. In this case, all models are waterproof with an impressive margin of volume. In Russia, the ruler is represented by the BASS PRO column: the device communicates with a smartphone or tablet via Bluetooth 4.0 (range up to 30 meters), while it has IPX-4 water protection, 3.5 mm aux input and output, the battery life is 10 hours.







The speaker can also be used for hands-free calling due to the presence of a microphone (do not disconnect it every time from the iPhone when they call you), and with Android devices it can be easily connected using NFC. Illuminated buttons located on top.







If the C-series is more for home use, then the Nyne X series is not terrible to take for a walk, to the pool, to the beach, or even to the mountains. This is where the loudest and at the same time the largest portable Bluetooth speaker in the world - ROCK - is presented . And she really is very big.







The speaker also connects to other devices via Bluetooth 4.0, a 60-watt 4.1 sound system with active speakers (2 tweeter, 2 mid-range, subwoofer) is responsible for the sound. You can carry this giant with the help of a hidden carrying handle, there is NFC for one-touch connection, you can use it as an external battery for charging mobile devices. With its size, the ROCK has IPX-3 water protection and works up to 12 hours without recharging.
