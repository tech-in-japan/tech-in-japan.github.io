Large-scale failure of fiscal registrars paralyzed trade in a number of Russian shops

Today, a number of stores in the Russian Federation have difficulty or discontinued trade due to the massive failure of the Shtrikh-M-01F fiscal recorders. Failures are reported by small shops and gas stations, as well as by large chains, such as the Magnolia chain in Moscow, the DNS, the Lukoil, Gazprom Neft gas stations and others.



Fiscal registrars - a unified system of accounting for checks at points of sale to control taxation. Sales of any goods without fiscal checks are prohibited.
