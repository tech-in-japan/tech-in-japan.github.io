By the time I joined the company, our rather long RnD stage was over and we decided to enter international markets. In fact, all our successes in this direction refer to this period. From the point of view of the second direction of our business related to procurement solutions, there was also a rather strong reset. The cumulative procurement turnover that passes through our decisions now exceeds 10 trillion. rubles per year. Also, a major reorganization of the company was carried out. Changed control system. New R'n'D centers opened in Innopolis, Kaliningrad and Tyumen.

You are talking about a level 3 driver assistance system. Explain what these levels mean? How many are there?



Now, according to the new international classification of SAE Inernational, the driver assistance system or the ADS (automated driving system) is already a three-dimensional matrix. This is not just a level 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, but a level with certain restrictions. With certain conditions. For traffic on closed territories, some rules apply, for traffic jams, others, for highways, a third, etc. It clearly states that the level of ADS 0 and 1 is generally a squeaker, hazard warning system, manual control. Level 2 is also not autonomy. The system interferes with control, for example, it brakes in front of an obstacle, holds a runway. Level 3 is a very important stage. This is autonomy in some conditions. For example, traffic on the highway or in traffic. That is, the driver at speeds up to 20 km / h, while driving in a traffic jam, the car lights the lights, ready to drive itself, the driver can conditionally remove his hands from the steering wheel. But it provides for the possibility of human intervention in a critical situation.



In fact, I must say that no one has yet seen level 3. It will appear as legislation changes. The fourth level is autonomous movement of a car in certain modes, and the system will be able to control even in critical situations when a person will not be able to interfere with control. Roughly speaking, it does not require interception of the driver‚Äôs control, but may pull over. Go to safe mode without the driver. The fifth level is completely autonomous movement. In the car, there is simply no steering as such.

Hacker Geohot , offered everyone to download the application and collect all the stream that was sent to the training of the neural network. And you use neural networks too. How do you solve this issue?



We use neural networks to select objects. But here we must understand that even if we recognize every object in the road scene, we will not get the meaning of the whole situation. It will not give the sum of the results of object recognition. To learn to understand the meaning, we have developed a truly revolutionary Cognitive low level data fusion technology. We collect data from sensors and from all systems at the bottom level. The man also acts. We kind of collect a neural network over a neural network, which determines the meaning of what is happening on the current video cut. For example, if a tennis ball rolls out onto the road, then this absolutely does not mean that the control system should ignore a small object. With a ball with a high probability, a child can jump out. Therefore, you need to carefully "look" in this direction for additional data, slow down or take a maneuver. We plan to show this technology at CES in Las Vegas in early 2018. As for the data collection model, the principle of crowdfunding really works here. Two years ago, we threw a cry for all motorists who are interested in participating in the creation of a Russian drone and volunteers from all over the country, from their DVRs sent thousands of kilometers of real video data, and most importantly, rare emergency situations that we later used to train our neural networks.
