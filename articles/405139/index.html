From home office to corporation: new laser devices from the Canon i-SENSYS line

At a time when some organizations began to move or have already partially switched to electronic document circulation, and the employees themselves are moving to home offices, new heroes come to the corporate press sphere - the compact but productive Canon i-SENSYS laser devices.







Today we will talk about the new products of this year: i-SENSYS LBP312x monochrome printer, LBP654 series color printer, and i-SENSYS MF735 color multifunction printer.



New technologies in action



With each new generation of i-SENSYS printers, Canon engineers not only improve the required features such as reliability and performance, but also significantly expand the functionality of devices.

Print







Due to significant changes in the design of printing devices, in addition to scanning speed, the printing speed has also significantly increased, however, as its quality.



New cartridges with a more compact structure of components now contain more toner and, as a result, are distinguished by an increased resource (up to 85%).

The new models, in particular in the i-SENSYS MF735, also implemented a new pinning technology for the A5 press that is gaining popularity in the office print environment.









This solution allows printing of A5 standard documents faster than A4 documents.

Monochrome Laser Printer i-SENSYS LBP312x



Canon i-SENSYS LBP312x - the newest model in the family, which went on sale in March of this year.







A compact, high-performance printer that replaces the LBP6780x takes up as much space as a regular home appliance. At the same time, he is able to really cope with large volumes of work, if we are talking about black and white printing, of course.



Try to imagine that this relatively small device is capable of printing A4 pages at a speed of 43 ppm and an A5 format at a speed of 65 ppm, while the life of the cartridge is almost 2 times longer than the predecessor model.



The device provides high-quality monochrome printing with more dense black color and high sharpness for low-resolution images.



LBP312x is equipped with a built-in paper cassette with a capacity of 550 sheets and allows you to additionally install up to three cartridges with a capacity of 550 sheets each, in order to increase the loading capacity or to print on various media and paper sizes.

Color laser MFP i-SENSYS MF735



High-tech i-SENSYS MF730 Series MFPs are a choice of small and medium-sized enterprises that need a complete solution for printing and working with documents.
