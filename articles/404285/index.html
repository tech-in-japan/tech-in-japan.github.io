Choosing music for Android

After Vkontakte transferred the ability to listen to music offline in the paid BOOM application, many users finally had a reason to look in the direction of alternative music applications. Under the cut is my comparison of the most popular music applications available in the Russian Federation. The topic is very subjective, but I hope someone will help make the right choice.



Participants in the review were:



  • Boom
  • Yandex.Music

Comparison criteria



To begin with, we will determine what parameters we will use to make a comparison:



  • Repertoire
    Perhaps the most subjective criterion. Today, less and less good music that I like, to the labels and official releases. I, as a listener, are not at all interested in the legal troubles - I want to listen to Spor , and the fresh tracks of the novice producer-neighbor.

  • Work offline
    Unfortunately, a fairly fast and unlimited Internet connection, allowing you to listen to quietly listen to music online, remains rare even in cities, not to mention trips, when you need it most. Therefore, the ability to download music to the device is a must. In this case, the management of downloaded music should be convenient, since device memory is also a valuable resource (at least for me).

  • Payment and price: everything is simple - I want cheaper and better
    You can and should pay for music. But only for the one that really pleases. And to understand, like it or not, you need to listen. And in order to listen you have to buy ... As a way to resolve this contradiction, today we are offered a subscription model - to pay less than the maximum cost, but certainly on a regular basis. Well, as long as there are no serious results from alternative monetization models, such as boostframe , this will do.

  • Search and recommendations
    There is so much music today that the search should be very intellectual: fuzzy search, expansion and correction of the request, active use of meta-information: album, genre, year, and at least BPM. In addition, recommendations can be useful - the robot can shovel a much larger amount of data, according to your profile.

  • Ease of use and resource consumption
    Listening to music is an activity, usually background and long-lasting. Therefore, management of the application should be clear, simple, reliable, even in the background, and the application itself should not eat off all the resources of the phone.
