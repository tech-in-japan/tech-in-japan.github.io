First Flutter meetup in St. Petersburg: video reports





September 26 at the St. Petersburg office of Wrike Flutter meetup was held. In anticipation of the second Russian-language conference on Dart and Flutter - DartUP 2019 - we gathered to discuss best practices for developing application architecture on flutter and the nuances of working with graphics and animation. For two years, the community of developers for dart and flutter has grown to 2000+ people, so it was not difficult to find interesting topics and reports.



For those who could not come, we publish videos.



Andrey Smirnov - Work with graphics in Flutter (Wrike)



Using Flutter, you can create beautiful interfaces, but the built-in capabilities are not always enough for the most unexpected requirements of our applications.

In this report, we look under the hood of the Flutter graphics engine. Let's see how the animation works. Let's write a custom widget using Dart. Draw a custom widget using Flare.





Daniil Surnin - Why bloc in the classic view may not always be suitable for your application. Example of combining redux and bloc





Sergey Lavinov - Isolates in battle



How using isolates simplifies the life of the developer, how we use them, and why compute is not always good

We are waiting for your topics and are ready to help with the preparation of the report. Well, we are just waiting for everyone to learn about the use of darts and flutters in production.
