Carrot. Benefits and harm to the body

Despite the fact that this root crop is very useful, there are also categories of people who should not eat this product for medical reasons.

An adult should eat no more than three hundred grams of the product per day. Larger amounts are not recommended.





If we talk about children up to a year, then carrot juice can be given already from six months of age. When babies are still breastfeeding, carrot juice is introduced much later.
