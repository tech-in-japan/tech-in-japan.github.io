For substitution of the number of operators will be fined

A few days ago, Senator Lyudmila Bokova submitted to the State Duma a bill on the need to impose a fine on the operator when changing the number when making a call with a subscriber. The maximum amount of the fine is 300 000 rubles. For legal entities, the fine is up to 200,000 for the first offense and up to 300,000 for repeated violations, Vedomosti reports.
