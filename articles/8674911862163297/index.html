Pancreatitis: which fruits can be eaten and which cannot

Pancreatitis is considered a disease of people who do not adhere to the correct diet and diet, as well as those who abuse alcohol. The article will dwell in detail on the list of such products and the general principles of nutrition in diseases of the pancreas.





What is pancreatitis?





The pancreas is rightfully considered one of the most complex organs, which can hardly be restored. Metabolism in the body and the work of the entire digestive system directly depend on its condition. Pancreatitis is an inflammatory disease of the pancreas. Its cause is a violation of the outflow of digestive juices and enzymes produced by the body into the duodenum. As a result, they begin to act in the pancreas, thereby destroying it.





Symptoms of pancreatitis are:





  • severe girdle pain in the left hypochondrium or extending to the back;
  • vomiting
  • diarrhea;
  • pallor of the skin;
  • increase in pressure;
  • heart palpitations.

To stop these signs, you must definitely seek help from a doctor. But the main condition for recovery is diet. Therefore, patients are often interested in what kind of fruits can be eaten with pancreatitis, and which ones are better to refuse. Let us dwell on the principles of nutrition for inflammation of the pancreas in more detail.

Proper nutrition and compliance with the recommended intervals between meals is the key to the onset of remission in this disease. Fruits and vegetables in the inflammatory process in the pancreas are not prohibited. However, there are a number of restrictions on their use.













General nutrition principles for pancreatic disease





Pancreatitis can occur in acute or chronic form. As mentioned above, one of the conditions for stopping the inflammatory process is diet. In addition, it is recommended that the following nutritional principles and daily routines be observed:





  1. Do not skip meals, observing the daily routine.
  2. Eat portions of 150 grams.
  3. Grind food, as it can cause irritation of the mucosa.
  4. Give preference to proteins over carbohydrates and fats.
  5. Refuse confectionery and foods with high salt content.
  6. With an exacerbation of the inflammatory process and severe pain, stick to fasting, refusing food for two days.

Even having achieved a relapse, when you overeat and abuse products that should be discarded, the disease can again remind itself of bouts of pain.

In the case of acute pancreatitis, the best way to restore the pancreas faster is starvation. After improvement, they should be introduced into the diet gradually, in the form of compotes, jelly, mashed potatoes from baked fruits, juices. General recommendations for eating fruits and vegetables

With inflammation of the pancreas, one should not completely abandon the usual diet. Fresh fruits and vegetables must be on the menu, as they contain vitamins and minerals necessary for the body. However, when using them, you should adhere to a number of rules.





General recommendations regarding what fruits you can eat with pancreatic pancreatitis look like this:





  1. Fruits should be consumed exclusively after heat treatment, and not in raw form.
  2. Preference should be given to ripe fruits with a soft peel having a sweet taste.
  3. During an exacerbation of the disease, it is strictly forbidden to eat raw fruits.
  4. It is not recommended to eat fruit on an empty stomach.
  5. Citrus, sour and bitter fruits should be discarded.

Adhering to the above recommendations, you can avoid problems with the pancreas.
