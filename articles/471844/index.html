The motivation to visit EPAM INSIDER Kazakhstan is already half the battle, but not all that you need. The next step: to understand how to get the most out of the conference. Our speakers shared personal life hacks that help them spend time effectively at events.



  • Do not sit still. Move around, go to reports, get acquainted. If you have a serious current problem, ask questions to speakers, do not hesitate to ask an authoritative opinion. Those contacts that you will gain in the process of communication will be invaluable.
  • Visit the stands. Here you can learn more about the life of the company from the inside, find people open for communication and your questions.
  • Use the Events Mobi mobile app . It will allow you to create your own conference schedule, make notes and, in a format that is convenient for you, turn to speakers and participants with a question or suggestion.
  • Decide on the reports. In advance, review the agent, mark topics that interest you.
