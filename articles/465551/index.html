September IT Events Digest (Part One)

Narrative Design Lecture Evening



When: September 4

Where: Moscow, st. Trifonovskaya, 57, p. 1

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Game industry experts and marketing experts will talk about narrative design and its capabilities in relevant areas all evening. At the same time, students will receive some tips on how to start a career in a game zone, and employees of game studios and indie developers - how to avoid common mistakes.

Moneyday



When: September 7th

Open epic night

Where: St. Petersburg, Kronverksky Prospekt, 23

Terms of participation: free, registration required

C ++ Evening # 1

approx. tech: Cassandra Meetup



When: September 12th

Where: St. Petersburg, st. Kherson 12-14

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Focus mitap: only fault tolerance and only Apache Cassandra from the creators of the Odnoklassniki service, one of the largest users of the system. Representatives of the company use their experience to consistently introduce participants to the course of affairs. The program begins by analyzing the very concept of fault tolerance, choosing the Consistency Level, the effect of replication and the type of queries, then moving on to more substantive questions - how to act in a failure situation, how to test, place replicas and calculate the load. The organizers emphasize that the meeting will be held in an open format, the activity of the audience is welcome in every possible way - you can even prepare and tell your own case.
