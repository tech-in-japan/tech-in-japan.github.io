New iPad Pro



Trying to breathe life into the slowly curving tablet market, Apple introduced a new iPad - the third in the Pro line. The heart of the tablet is a modified processor from the iPhone 7, a six-core A10X Fusion (three high-performance cores and three energy-efficient) with a twelve-core graphics chip.
