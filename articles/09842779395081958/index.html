Who should be careful

Ginger in its composition has active oils and trace elements that inhibit blood clotting. Ginger tea, the properties and contraindications of which are described in great detail, should be used very carefully for those who have a stomach ulcer. A large amount of such tea can contribute to the exacerbation of the disease and even provoke bleeding.













It is also worth giving up this drink to those who are preparing for a planned operation. For 3 weeks, you need to stop drinking ginger tea. Also, do not drink it in the postoperative period.









Tea is contraindicated for those who have stones in the gallbladder. A drink can provoke their activity.





Ginger tea invigorates and accelerates blood, so if you have a fever, you should not use it. It may happen that the body temperature rises even more. But with colds and viral infections, when the temperature has already subsided, tea will help the body fight the disease.





Also, doctors do not recommend drinking tea for those who have skin rashes, ulcers, irritations, allergies. Dermatologists believe that ginger can aggravate skin problems, so it is better to refuse this drink during treatment. If there are no problems with the skin, ginger tea, on the contrary, can only strengthen it, make it smoother and more tender.





Children are not contraindicated

Many parents, having studied the beneficial properties of ginger on their own, are wondering whether children can drink ginger tea and whether there are any restrictions.





In principle, doctors and nutritionists recommend including ginger root for brewing tea in the children's menu. True, it is better not to experiment with children under two years of age; you should not load them with a very active natural component.













But for children over two years old, it is quite possible to give ginger tea. He will only do them good. It is especially good to offer it half an hour before meals to those children who suffer from reduced appetite. After all, it speeds up the digestion process and causes appetite. This tea relieves manifestations of nausea. And he is an excellent bactericidal drink. Tea is also useful for children with colds and inflammation of the upper respiratory tract.
