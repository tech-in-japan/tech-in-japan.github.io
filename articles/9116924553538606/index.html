13-day trial: Angel diet

This is the name of one of the most popular and effective diets of our time - the Angel diet. Every woman knows: if she wants to get rid of extra pounds of excess weight for a rather short time, strengthen her health, improve her general well-being, she should turn to this method.





The so-called Angel diet gives the most amazing results - in a period of two weeks (without one day) you can get rid of 7, and sometimes 8 excess kilograms, and the very construction of nutrition is organized so successfully that it allows you to radically rebuild the metabolism. Which in turn leads to successful results - the weight not only decreases, but also remains stable for a long time.





A feature of such a regimen and method of nutrition is its separability, which implies the alternation of vegetable and protein products. This is not difficult to do, since food, according to the diet, should be taken fractionally, up to six times a day, at intervals of two to two and a half hours. Following all the recommendations, you can achieve an amazing result within a week - ten days after the start of the diet, especially since it is often recommended by nutritionists and has no contraindications. Also, experts recommend during this period to do daily exercise and massage, so that weight loss is more successful and painless.
