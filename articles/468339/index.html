Introducing the Dark Theme for Visual Studio App Center

We strive to make Visual Studio App Center the most convenient for you. Thanks to taking the time to share your wishes with us through our Github repository , we can create features for you, such as this dark theme for the App Center.

How to get started with a dark theme



It has never been so easy! For fans of dark themes, App Center will automatically enable a dark theme by default. However, you can change this in the App Center at any time. Just go to your account settings and click on User Interface. On the page that opens, you can choose your preferred type of theme - dark or light.
