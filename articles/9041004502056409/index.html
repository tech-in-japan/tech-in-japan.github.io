Sauerkraut for weight loss: reviews of doctors

Dietary vegetables are necessary, they are rich in fiber and contain few calories, saturate the body with vitamins and minerals, promote good digestion. Most often they are consumed fresh, but you should especially pay attention to a product such as sauerkraut. For weight loss (reviews indicate the wide popularity of such an application), it is used both as part of a complex and as a mono-diet. The product is extremely healthy, which gives the body a lot of useful substances. Even without striving for severe restrictions on food, it is very useful to add it to lunch and dinner, thereby reducing the portion of less healthy pasta or potatoes.













What you need to know about sauerkraut

This product has been used by our ancestors for centuries. Both Slavs and many other nationalities believe that they are the ancestors of this method of harvesting cabbage. Unlike traditional salting, such a product is much more useful, as it contains less salt. In this case, not only sauerkraut is used for weight loss. Reviews speak of the high efficiency of cabbage soup or just a salad of fresh heads of cabbage.





What is the peculiarity of the preparation of this vegetable by the method of fermentation? After salting, cabbage is kept for 1-2 days indoors, that is, fermented. During this time, lactic fermentation is formed in it , it is this that ensures the conservation of the product and the preservation of its beneficial properties. Not all varieties are suitable for pickling, it is late white and red cabbage that contain a large amount of carbohydrates, which is necessary for pickling. A cool autumn season favorably affects how long sauerkraut will last. For weight loss (reviews suggest that it finds such an application for a long time) it is used in an independent form, seasoned with vegetable oil, or as an additive to other dietary products.

















Composition of the product

There is no doubt in the benefits of cabbage. This vegetable should be on the table of each person daily. No time to plan salads? Then immediately prepare a large jar and put in the refrigerator. But why is sauerkraut so popular for weight loss so popular? Reviews very accurately indicate how useful it is for the body. This method of harvesting allows not only to preserve all the useful properties of the vegetable, but also to enrich it with lactic acid fermentation products.





It is primarily a source of vitamin C. Like other leafy vegetables, cabbage contains vitamin K, choline, and inositol. From this product, the body receives a vitamin B group - carotene. Mineral substances are also present in it: potassium, calcium, iron and zinc. Of the organic acids, malic acid is most found, which is known for its beneficial properties and beneficial effects on the body.





The nutritional value of the product per 100 g: protein - 2%, fat - 0.1%, carbohydrates - 7% (glucose, fructose, fiber, pectin). It is this composition that provides huge popularity, which deservedly enjoys sauerkraut for weight loss. Reviews of doctors fully confirm the beneficial properties of this product, warning only against the use of mono-diets. Even the most wonderful product can not contain the entire set of substances necessary for the body. But the best addition to chicken breast or low-fat fish is difficult to find.

  • K normalizes the activity of the digestive tract.

  • U is an essential amino acid that supports the normal functioning of the stomach and protects against peptic ulcer.

Experts in this field say that sauerkraut is a negative calorie product, that is, your body will spend more energy on digesting it than it will receive. Contraindications

Unfortunately, such a product as sauerkraut is not suitable for everyone to lose weight. The benefit (contraindications do not cancel it), of course, will not be the same, but patients with high acidity, acute and chronic kidney diseases, high blood pressure, peptic ulcer and tachycardia need to reduce its intake to a minimum in order to avoid deterioration of health.





Side effects

Like any other product, they also exist. This does not mean that sauerkraut is not suitable for weight loss. Doctors' reviews, prescriptions - all this warns us against product abuse out of a desire to lose weight more quickly. Unlimited consumption of sauerkraut can lead to flatulence. This is especially true for those who consumed little fiber and his intestines were not ready for such a load. Edema and mild stomach pain can also develop.
