What to drink with and after poisoning

After the mandatory cleansing of the body (often for this purpose they use drinking a weak solution of potassium permanganate), one should carefully approach the question of what to drink in case of poisoning. Strong dehydration of the body occurs at this time , so the restoration of water balance is a priority. But here's what to drink in case of poisoning in each case, it is worthwhile to carefully understand.





So what to drink in case of poisoning? For all types of food poisoning, you need to drink a large amount of still water. The main thing is that it should be of very good quality. Water helps the body cleanse itself of toxins. After cleansing the body, you can give the patient a little unsweetened black tea. When the urge to vomit stops, you can drink chicken stock (preferably non-greasy). Sparkling water should not be drunk at this time, since it strongly irritates the walls of the inflamed gastrointestinal tract, which further aggravates the patient‚Äôs situation.





All fluids must be drunk in small sips and very slowly, since a large amount of drink can again cause vomiting. Such a remedy is very effective for rehydrating the body: fruit juice is mixed with half a teaspoon of honey, after which a pinch of table salt is added to it. An almost universal remedy that helps with various poisonings is milk. A common cure for all poisonings is peppermint and turnip seeds, mixed with wine in equal proportions.





In case of serious poisoning with various toxic substances, medical help is required. Before the arrival of a specialist, it is necessary to completely empty the stomach with vomiting. Everyone can help themselves and their loved ones, knowing what to drink in case of poisoning with the most common chemicals. Consider the most common types of poisoning:









- for poisoning with plant poisons, camphor, wine vinegar and coffee helps;





- in case of poisoning with vinegar essence, the patient is given milk to drink; lime water; decoctions of rice, barley, flaxseed; a lot of cold water;





- in case of poisoning with hydrocyanic acid, it is necessary to consume a lot of coffee or water with ammonia;





- fish poisons are neutralized by drinking sweet water with charcoal or vodka;





- for poisoning with chlorine vapor, sweet water, wine or vodka is used;





- poisoning by vapors of pesticides is neutralized by coffee;





- with drug poisoning, water with vinegar and coffee is most often used;





- for poisoning with metal salts they use raw egg white and water with sugar ;





- in case of drug poisoning, give ghee or fat cream, and after a while - pomegranate juice;





- poisoning with sausage poison is eliminated with vinegar diluted in water, and after improvement, you can give warm black tea;





- when poisoning with mushrooms, you need to drink water with charcoal or wine;





- in case of poisoning with caustic acids, the use of vegetable oil helps;





- wine helps with poisoning by harmful fumes of chemicals;





- for alkali poisoning, glauber salt is used;





- poisoning with iodine preparations is neutralized by starch water.





Very often people are tormented by the question of what to drink in case of alcohol poisoning. Wine alcohol poisoning is usually treated with milk, kefir, mucous drink. After alcohol poisoning, drink more strong tea with lemon or honey. Coffee should not be abused, so you can drink 1-2 cups of a not too strong drink.





What to drink with alcohol poisoning with severe consequences? A good remedy is: a glass of water, five drops of ammonia and one teaspoon of sugar. An ancient and effective treatment for severe alcohol poisoning is the use of brine. It accelerates the alkalization of blood, which after alcohol poisoning becomes acidic, which in fact causes the unpleasant consequences of a hangover.
