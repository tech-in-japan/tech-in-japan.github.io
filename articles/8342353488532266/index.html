Vegetable diet for weight loss - weekly menu, features and effectiveness

If your numerous attempts to lose weight did not lead to anything, then you should pay attention to vegetable diet for weight loss. One of the most important advantages of such diets is that you will feel full longer, and the tendency to overeat will gradually disappear.





The benefits of vegetables

Vegetables often remain underestimated components during cooking. They also pay little attention to the preparation of nutrition systems and a wide variety of diets. But vegetables are the most important component contributing to the improvement of the work not only of internal organs, but also of the whole organism.









Vegetables contain important nutrients, contribute to the absorption of vitamins and minerals. Unlike meat products, they have a significantly lower protein content, but in return a person receives a large amount of fiber, pectin substances and other important components. Also, vegetables contain a small amount of carbohydrates.





In vegetables, there are substances that have a beneficial effect on the cardiovascular system and peripherals. They have an antioxidant effect and cleanse the body of toxins and toxins. According to some reports, about 500 grams of vegetables should be present daily in an adult's diet.









Diets

Vegetable diets for weight loss differ from the usual ones in that high saturation with this particular type of food makes the dish more satisfying and healthy. Due to this, a person is saturated with a smaller portion of food, and the calorie content of his diet decreases.





There is a huge variety of such diets and nutrition systems, but some are worth paying attention to, as they will not only help to quickly lose weight, but also turn out to be filled with great-tasting culinary dishes.





Milk and Vegetable Diet

For weight loss, you can use this particular diet only if you have no problems digesting lactose. Lactose is called milk sugar, which is found even in pasteurized and ultra-pasteurized milk.





But if, despite this, you decide to use this diet, having problems with the digestion of milk, then you can purchase an enzyme called lactase in specialized stores. He will just take on all the work of digesting lactose.









For this diet you will need:





  • Milk - 1 liter.
  • Cottage cheese with a fat content of 0% -1.8% - 200 grams.
  • Coffee, tea, compote - without sugar in any quantities.
  • Coarse Bread - 200 grams.
  • Turnip, potatoes - 200 grams.
  • Fruits, berries - 300 grams.
  • Butter - no more than 30 grams.

With this diet, your daily diet will be approximately 1000 kilocalories. It is not recommended to fry potatoes and turnips, it is better to boil them or steam, then more nutrients will remain in the product.









Cooking recommendations:





  • For better assimilation of dishes, mix, for example, 100 grams of cottage cheese with 50-100 ml of milk, this will quickly absorb the cottage cheese, as it will be soaked and the stomach will be able to digest it faster.
  • If you decide to cook potatoes, then after cooking, remove it, use a blender to mashed it and add a small amount of milk and 15 grams of butter. In this case, you will get a very tasty and hearty dish.
  • You can safely add berries to the cottage cheese for a variety of taste sensations.

Do not forget that in this diet absolutely no additional sugar and salt are used, as they will provoke changes in the composition of the body. Salt helps retain excess fluid, and sugar enhances appetite.





The diet is effective because it contains low amounts of proteins, fats and carbohydrates. On average, a person receives about 2,000 kilocalories per day, and with this diet you will receive half the amount. For a week, weight loss will be approximately 3-5 kilograms. After this, it is necessary to increase the calorie intake to 1,500 kilocalories and remain in this diet.

Soups while dieting

Soups are a great option to create a more varied diet. They are even less caloric, but saturate better due to the large volume of the portion.









Diet for weight loss on vegetable soup will help to lose weight even faster than previous options.





The advantages of this diet:





  • Variety of diet.
  • Satiety.
  • Improving the condition of the body due to fluid.
  • Fast cooking.

Basically, this diet is suitable for those who do not want to count calories, acquire fat burners and do fitness.





In order not to spend a lot of time preparing soup every day, it is recommended to prepare the vegetable broth a few days in advance. To do this, add to the boiling water, in the amount of 5-7 liters, the peel from two large clean potatoes. The whole potato is not used in the recipe, since it is not suitable for weight loss. It can be prepared for other family members.





Also in boiling water add about 10 medium carrots, 300 grams of turnip, half the celery root and a little coriander for taste. All vegetables should be finely chopped and the broth cooked for at least two hours. Then cool it and strain it with a fine colander or gauze. Now you can use this broth for making soups for 3-4 days.





Using various components, you can get soups of various tastes, and your vegetable diet for weight loss will not bother with its monotony.





Slimming Cabbage Soup Recipe

This recipe is notable for its simplicity and cheapness. Cabbage is cooked very quickly, and even costs less than potatoes. In addition, cabbage contains many vitamins, minerals and fiber that are beneficial to human health. Fiber is useful for the gastrointestinal tract, as it improves digestion due to the fibers contained in it and helps to eliminate toxins.





The calorie content of the resulting dish is approximately 50 kilocalories, so you should not be afraid that a large amount of soup eaten can affect your weight.





List of ingredients for making soup:





  • White cabbage - 500 grams.
  • Carrots - 5 pcs.
  • Bell pepper - 1 pc.
  • String beans (fresh or frozen) - 250 grams.
  • Tomatoes - 4 pcs.

Stages of cooking:





  • All that is on the list, except for tomatoes, should be thoroughly washed, processed and finely chopped.
  • Take about two liters of the previously prepared broth, which was described above, and add the chopped vegetables there.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil and cook for about half an hour.
  • As soon as half an hour comes to an end, you need to add finely chopped tomatoes and leave to cook together for about another ten minutes.
  • Let the soup brew for about 20 minutes.

When using this soup in your diet, you will begin a quick process of losing weight. The recipe for a vegetable diet contains a small amount of calories, so during the week weight loss can be up to ten kilograms.
