Retro train with a steam locomotive





While you are sitting here, Russian Railways sells tickets for retro-train very well. These are, naturally, with a steam locomotive. Yes, steam engines will survive a lot, including nuclear war. Yes, you can take a ride on our strategic reserve.







So, we have the Ruskealsky express train that runs in Karelia from the city of Sortavala to the mountain park of Ruskeala. It is 5 hours from Petersburg to an important tourist point.



In addition to the retro-train, retro-passengers traveled with us. More precisely, theater college students who staged an immersive production of communists carrying party gold. What brought the brain to a considerable number of pensioners.





How to get on this retro train



First you need to go on this page of the Russian Railways website and select a route. This is 921CH (the return is 922CH), a ticket costs a maximum of 500 rubles per hour of travel (you can take for 300 if you catch it much in advance). He departs from the Sortavala station in Karelia, but he still needs to be reached. Previously, this was an so-so idea, because it was necessary to do it with an ordinary electric train or by bus, which did not bring any joy and required an overnight stay somewhere in Karelia, so as not to get tired of fatigue. Now the "Swallow" walks in about 4 hours, which dramatically changes the situation - you can and should put the whole trip in one day.



So, you have to wake up at 5 in the morning to leave at 6:15 from Finland Station by train 821. It is better to take food with you or buy at the station.







In an hour the Internet will begin to end, and in two - electricity. At 44 kilometers from Vyborg in Kamennogorsk, the 3000 Volt network will finally run out. And the "Swallow" she really needed. But we live in a country where engineers are able to overcome reality, so a special diesel locomotive is hooked to the train, which can distribute these same 3000 V.
