The process of heat treatment of metals is the basis of the entire metal industry. It is this industry that makes it possible to create various alloys with unique properties. The heat treatment of metals changes the structure of steel and metal alloys due to the influence of temperature on them. This process includes several stages:



  • heating the material at a certain speed to a certain temperature;
  • holding the necessary amount of time;
  • cooling at a specific speed to a predetermined temperature.


These stages give hardening, softening and stabilization of the material.



It often happens that during the metal processing process excessive warping is generated, due to which cracks form. The reason for this marriage is uneven or improper heating and cooling.



Winnum TRP is a unique solution for data monitoring and programming of heat treatment modes, which allows you to reduce costs and increase the quality of products, parts and blanks.
