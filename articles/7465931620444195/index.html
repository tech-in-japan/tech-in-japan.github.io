Product Collection Rules

The optimal period when you can collect birch sap is the beginning of March. It is worth considering that one tree can produce about 3 liters of juice, in rare cases - about 7 liters.

















First you need to determine if there is juice in a particular tree. To do this, using an awl, it is enough to make a small hole in the cortex. If after these actions you see a drop that stands out, you can safely begin collecting nectar.





You need to choose only mature trees, the crown of which is quite well developed. It is advisable to drill a hole on the south side at a distance of 50 cm from the ground. The drill must be pointing down. After that, the groove must be inserted into the hole. It is on it that the juice will flow into the left container. To maintain the vital activity of the plant, it is recommended to collect no more than 1 liter of fluid per day.
