Google translator



20 years ago, Yuri Yartsev became an employee of Google, sitting at home. Then it was a completely different company, which was just looking for commercial routes. There was a line ‚Äúneed volunteers‚Äù on the company's website, he joined and began translating Google‚Äôs interfaces into Russian and Ukrainian. ‚ÄúThey had a very old and inconvenient system for translations.
