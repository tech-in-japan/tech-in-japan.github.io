Composition of Slavyanovskaya mineral water and its storage

What does Slavyanovskaya (mineral water) consist of? Its composition is rich in bicarbonates, sulfates, chlorides, sodium, potassium, magnesium and calcium cations. At the same time, it has a low level of mineralization and has a mild taste, perfectly quenching thirst. Keep the water bottle in a prone position in a cool, dark place. In this case, a small precipitate in the form of mineral salts is allowed.





"Slavyanovskaya", mineral water: indications and contraindications for use

As mentioned above, the products of ZAO "MVZH" have healing properties. And this means that there are both special indications for its use, and certain contraindications.













Mineral water "Slavyanovskaya" is recommended for use if:





  • esophageal diseases;
  • chronic gastritis;
  • ulcers of the duodenum or stomach;
  • bowel disease;
  • problems with the liver, gall bladder and biliary tract;
  • pancreatic diseases;
  • diabetes mellitus, obesity and other metabolic diseases;
  • urinary tract diseases;
  • cystitis, pyelitis or pyelonephritis.

In particular, the intake of this product is indicated in the presence of a chronic form of the above diseases. Mineral water "Slavyanovskaya" can also be used to prevent the occurrence of gastrointestinal problems.

















Contraindication to use is the occurrence of an exacerbation of a disease, as well as the pronounced intolerance of a similar treatment method in the form of heartburn, diarrhea, etc. By the way, the same applies to another no less well-known healing mineral water - Essentuki 17.
