Choosing an alternative to Mailchimp: why switch and which product to choose





Mailchimp is perhaps the most famous and iconic tool in Internet marketing, and email marketing in particular. At the time of its appearance, Chimp made a revolution: at that time there were no inexpensive email marketing tools for small and medium-sized companies. Mailchimp developers managed to create such a product and make it very convenient, which allowed it to gain popularity around the world.



However, today for Russian companies the use of this service is becoming less and less profitable. Today we‚Äôll talk about the difficulties you will encounter when working with Mailchimp, and how to overcome them.



Why today Mailchimp is not suitable for Russian companies



To begin, consider the main disadvantages of Mailchimp from the point of view of Russian business.



English interface



The most obvious: this product is American, created by English-speaking developers and mainly for customers from English-speaking countries. Russia has never been a priority market for Mailchimp; it is not today.



Therefore, it is not necessary to expect the appearance of localization of the interface in Russian. In situations where you need to configure complex conversations, use many different functions of the service, a fully English-language interface can seriously slow down the work.



No Russian language support



From the aforesaid, problem number two also follows: if you encounter any difficulties in the process of interacting with the product, they will also need to be solved in English. Mailchimp does not have Russian-language support; the customer service is located in the United States.



Therefore, it will be necessary not only to conduct correspondence in English, but also to accept that communication will go with a long delay due to the time difference with the USA.



High prices and inconvenient billing



An even more important point is the cost of services. Mailchimp will have to pay in dollars, but that‚Äôs half the trouble. Recently, the company began the transformation and moves away from the image of ‚Äúa convenient and inexpensive email service for small and medium-sized companies.‚Äù Now it is a tool for CRM marketing, and the focus is already big business. Accordingly, pricing has changed.



Since the spring of 2019, Mailchimp billing is built not on the basis of sent letters, but according to the number of contacts. Moreover, those users who have unsubscribed from the newsletter are taken into account. Yes, there is a free version, but it is so "slaughtered" in terms of functionality that it is impossible to solve many business problems of even a small company.







Mailchimp Rates



As a result, using the service becomes extremely disadvantageous in a number of cases:



  • if you have a small base (up to 5 thousand people);
  • if you do not send letters often (for example, only stock alerts are sent);
  • if you need a large number of features: testing, message chains, etc. - all this immediately costs from $ 299 per month.


No Russian specifics and compliance with laws



Since Chimp is an American service, it is imprisoned for companies from the USA. This means that you can pay for a service from Russia, in fact, only by credit card. This is not always convenient for domestic companies.



In addition, Mailchimp does not comply with Russian law, in particular, the law on the storage of personal data of Russians in the country (152-FZ). It turns out that if a company with Russian customers uses Mailchimp, then personal data will be stored on servers in the USA - and this violates the law and can in theory lead to trouble.
