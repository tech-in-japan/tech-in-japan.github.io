Mapping



  • Facebook said it plans to make the Intelligent Recognition service available to OSM mappers in its RapiD editor. According to media reports , Facebook plans to improve the data that it uses for its own purposes. Recall that the experiments of this company in mapping using artificial intelligence not so long ago caused a lot of questions and complaints .
  • Joseph Eisenberg tried to improve the process of preparing new tagging schemes. Especially for this, he created a page in WikiOSM where he collected 13 properties (characteristics) that certain tagging schemes may possess.
  • The waterway=tidal_channel



    , which was proposed by Joseph Eisenberg, is approved . They are encouraged to mark natural tidal waterways. Recall that the discussion on this tag took place in September 2018. A corresponding article has already appeared in WikiOSM describing this tag. The information was taken from the page on which the proposal for the new tagging scheme was drawn up.
