International Team Coding Party







  • November 20 (Wednesday)
  • Leningradsky prospekt 39s79
  • is free
  • We invite you to the International Team Coding Party by JavaScript. In the evening of November 20, developers from England, France, Spain and Russia will meet in a friendly battle.

    Participants will gather in London, Madrid, Paris and Moscow. The Moscow team gathers in the office of Mail.ru Group. Registration is required!

    Each team (city) will have to complete several tasks on JS, of varying degrees of difficulty: from Beginner to Expert. Tasks are performed on the Kodiri platform, therefore, for best results, it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with their interface and rules.

    At the end of the game, we compare the results of the teams. The result of each team is calculated as the arithmetic average of the results of its participants. For each difficulty level, the results are independently reported.
