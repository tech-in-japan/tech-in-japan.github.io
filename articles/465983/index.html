Intel Comet Lake: a little 14 nm in the tenth generation





According to the strategy of launching Intel processors on the market already in recent years, the next tenth generation includes several heterogeneous groups: along with the already presented 10-nm Intel Ice Lake , the 14-nm Comet Lake will also live here in their various forms . In late August, the official announcement of the Comet Lake mobile line for portable devices took place.



Mobile Comet Lake on the Legacy rights received model indexes of the "old model", not the same as those used in Ice Lake. In general, the picture is as follows (clickable so that you can read the small text).







Unlike Ice Lake, Comet Lake uses 9+ generation graphics cores - they debuted in 2017 at Kaby Lake. Core i3 has 23 actuators, the rest - 24, their operating frequency is 1.0-1.1 GHz. Other main characteristics are given in the table.
