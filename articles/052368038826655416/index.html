How to get rid of PMS: methods, medicines and folk remedies

Almost every girl and woman of childbearing age every month faces a condition such as premenstrual syndrome. This condition is really very difficult and terrible for every member of the fair sex. Therefore, it is very important to learn how to help yourself. In this article, we will look at how to get rid of PMS and regain good health. Carefully read the information provided in order to arm yourself and protect yourself as much as possible. So let's get started.





Symptoms of PMS in women

In each girl, premenstrual syndrome manifests itself in different ways. But most often the fair sex complain of a bad mood, depression, lethargy and fatigue. In addition, the occurrence of headaches, as well as pains in the lower abdomen, nausea and diarrhea is not excluded. Also very often representatives of the weak half of humanity complain about the swelling of the mammary glands and the presence of pain in them. For someone, this condition begins a week before the onset of menstruation, and for someone - a few days before it.









According to gynecologists, the symptoms of PMS in women can be alleviated or even completely eliminated. The main thing is to monitor your health and follow the recommendations of a gynecologist.









Many are interested in the answer to the question of what is PMS in girls. The interpretation of this concept is as follows: premenstrual syndrome.





The main causes

More than half of the fair sex are faced with pain and bad mood before the onset of menstruation. At the same time, men who live next to them know firsthand what PMS is for girls (a transcript of this concept is indicated in this article). During the entire menstrual cycle, hormonal changes occur in the female body. In the second half of the cycle, the amount of estrogen in the body increases significantly with respect to progesterone. At the same time, the amount of such hormones as serotonin and dopamine is also reduced. It is for this reason that a bad mood and a depressed state arise. In addition, the second phase of the cycle is also characterized by a decrease in the production of natural painkillers - endorphins. That is why during menstruation a woman feels a pain that seems simply unbearable for her.









How to get rid of PMS is a question that worries almost all representatives of the fair half of humanity. It is not possible to completely eliminate this condition, since it depends on the activity of the hormonal system, but you can still improve your well-being, overcome nervousness and reduce the level of pain. The main thing is to know how to do this.









Gynecologist visit

If you are interested in the question of how to get rid of PMS, first you will have to find out the reason for this condition. To do this, it is recommended to consult a gynecologist. You may have impaired production of female sex hormones. If necessary, the doctor will also refer you to an endocrinologist in order to determine if there are problems with the production of other types of hormones. According to experts, improper thyroid function enhances PMS. How to get rid of it in this case? Take thyroid medications.





Power Features

Your condition will largely depend on what you eat. If you do not know how to get rid of PMS, pay attention to your nutrition. Experts recommend a week before menstruation to drink as little tea and coffee drinks as possible, as well as generally reduce fluid intake. Also, do not abuse foods that contain a large amount of potassium. These include dried apricots, baked potatoes, bananas and raisins.









But products with a lot of calcium, on the contrary, are welcome. Prefer greens, cabbage and green turnips. Gynecologists also advise during the PMS and during menstruation to additionally take vitamin complexes.





Many women suffer from depression and low mood. Therefore, the question naturally arises of how to get rid of nervousness in PMS. This can be done using foods containing large amounts of magnesium. You can find such a trace element in cereals, seafood, and nuts.

Make the right break

What helps from PMS? Another remedy is a sound and healthy sleep. An insufficient amount of night rest negatively affects the condition of a woman in the premenstrual period. However, spending all day in bed is also not worth it. You can work, walk in the park and do your usual household chores. The main thing - do not load yourself heavily.
