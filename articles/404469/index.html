How did one of the most anticipated events in Russian telecom go?





That was the last annual conference of Russian Telecom Operators. The second visit to Sochi turned out to be impressive, but only the participants can objectively judge the past event. This year, one of the most significant events in the Russian telecom gathered 618 participants from 274 companies representing 138 cities of Russia and the countries of the post-Soviet space. Arrival of participants is a responsible stage, because it is here that participants receive first impressions of the conference and unwittingly assess the level of organization. At CROS 2017, our entire team worked smoothly, like an engine in a German premium car.
