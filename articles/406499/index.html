IFA 2017: Lenovo & Motorola New Product Guide

The International Consumer Electronics Show, or Internationale Funkausstellung Berlin, and briefly the IFA, annually brings together the best manufacturers of appliances and electronics. Moto Z2 Force smartphone with Moto Mod support



The flagship Moto Z 2 Force is interesting from all sides, but its main feature is the display, protected from breaking and cracks. Proprietary technology ShatterShield will save the smartphone screen when falling even on tile or asphalt. The device itself is not shockproof, so throwing it from the 9th floor is still not recommended.







The device supports all existing modules - from a simple Moto Style Shell lid to a Hasselblad True Zoom camera. Recall that the mod range is quite wide - it has a projector, extra batteries, speakers, and a 360 camera, GamePad Moto Mod and a cover with support for wireless charging will soon appear.







The main camera is dual - it has two modules with a resolution of 12 megapixels, the first is color, the second is monochrome. The camera makes excellent shots in almost any conditions, it is possible to blur the background or foreground already on the finished photo directly from the smartphone.



Branded chips on the spot - the newest Moto Experiences include a night display, voice control of Moto Voice and everything we are used to on Moto devices, gestures along the scanner, gestures to turn on the flashlight and camera, Active Display showing notifications without unlocking.



Moto X4 Smartphone







Without exaggeration, the novelty has a great appearance. The smartphone is available in two colors - black and blue, the back panel received a 3D volumetric design. The device is protected on both sides with Gorilla Glass, which is not afraid of scratches and other mechanical effects. The smartphone has received protection against water and dust according to the IP68 standard, which means it is able to withstand immersion in water to a depth of 1 m.







The main camera is double, right in the camera application there is a mode for measuring depth and color correction. Sensors with a resolution of 12 megapixel and 8 megapixel stretch the frame even in the worst lighting conditions, and the proprietary function Landmark Detection recognizes business cards and other important information.







The front camera received a resolution of 16 megapixel, panoramic mode and the ability to shoot in low light conditions.



Moto Mod: Camera 360



The Camera 360 module is compatible with all Moto Z Series smartphones and, as the name implies, turns the smartphone into a panoramic camera. Interactive panoramic video can be recorded in 4K format, and 4 microphones are immediately responsible for recording spatial sound.







The software allows you to edit the footage directly without leaving your smartphone, and you can view and share the finished videos through Google Photos.







Lenovo Yoga 920 laptop



High technologies are not just words in relation to the new Lenovo Yoga 920 laptop. It is not just powerful and modern, it has absorbed everything that the user may need in the world of the upcoming virtual reality.







Yoga 920 runs on quad-core Intel Core i7 processors of the eighth generation, equipped with two Thunderbolt 3 ports, a fingerprint scanner and an almost frameless anti-glare IPS touch screen of 4K with a diagonal of 13.9 inches. Supported and stylus - Lenovo Active Pen 2 with a set of applications for touch input Windows Ink recognizes 4096 levels of pressure for drawing and creating notes.



Lenovo MiiX 520 Tablet



The Lenovo Miix 520 transforming tablet retains the main features of the series - a keyboard case with real keys is attached to it, and there is a swivel stand on the case that makes it easy to place the tablet on the table.







Miix 520 is based on the latest Intel Quad Core i7 processor of the eighth generation and works under Windows 10. The device is equipped with DDR4 RAM up to 16 GB and PCIe SSD drives with capacities up to 1 TB. Tablet weight - only 880 grams, thickness - 9.9 mm.







The tablet supports work with the digital assistant Cortana and stylus Lenovo Active Pen 2.
