Cauliflower in the oven with cheese

Structure:





  • 1 head of cabbage;
  • 1 egg
  • 100 gr. hard cheese;
  • 3 tbsp. l mayonnaise or sour cream;
  • greens, salt and pepper to taste.

Divide the cabbage into inflorescences, wash thoroughly and place in boiling and salted water. Cook for 5‚Äì7 minutes over medium heat under a lid. Drain the water, cool the cabbage and put on a greased baking sheet with inflorescences upwards in one layer. Beat the egg, add sour cream, grated cheese, finely chopped greens. Pour the cabbage with the mixture and put in the oven for 10-15 minutes. The dish is ready when the cheese has melted, and the cabbage has acquired a beautiful golden crust.





Light soup

You will need:





  • 1 head of cauliflower;
  • 3 potatoes;
  • 4 tbsp. l rice;
  • carrot;
  • onion;
  • vegetable oil;
  • salt, pepper, herbs.













Wash and peel vegetables. Dice potatoes and onions, carrots - in strips, sort cabbage into inflorescences. Rice and rinse with cold water to leave excess starch. In boiling water, throw potatoes, half a carrot and onion, rice and cook for 15 minutes. Pour oil into a pan, put the remaining half of onions and carrots, stirring, stir for 10 minutes. Pour the fry into the broth, salt, pepper, put greens and cauliflower. After boiling, cook for another 10 minutes over low heat under a lid. Serve hot with chopped herbs.





Braised inflorescences

Ingredients:





  • 1 head of cabbage;
  • 1-2 onion heads;
  • vegetable oil;
  • greens, salt and spices.













Boiled cabbage inflorescences boil for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, lightly fry the onion in vegetable oil. Put cabbage in a pan, stirring, fry for another 5 minutes. After this, salt the dish, add spices and 300 ml of boiling water, cover. Simmer until cabbage is soft.
