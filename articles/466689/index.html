How to create character classes in mobile MMO Action

We continue to talk with the development team of the MMO action Pixel Wars from DatCroft Games. This time, representatives of the game design department Roman Davydov and Ekaterina Sterlikova tell how the new Druid character class was created, why it turned out that way and what is its uniqueness for the project.













What classes are already in the game?



The Pixel Wars project began with four base character classes. The developers started off from classic fantasy RPGs, and, of course, the first three available heroes included the Warrior, Mage and Archer. The fourth was the general favorite of the Gunners - the only girl at the time of the launch of the game who brought drive and a bit of steampunk style to the game.







The fifth one was added the Shaman - in the basic set of characters there was a free position of a buffer who could heal allies and weaken enemies. The shaman is one with nature, he puts totems on the battlefield, charged with the power of ancient spirits.







Which class will be next?



Sixth grade Druid is now at the final stage of development and will appear in the game in the foreseeable future. The main feature of the Druid is reincarnation. In the guise of a fragile girl, the Druid heals allies, and becoming a huge bear, crushes enemies.







With the choice of a new class, everything is simple. The new character should be unique both visually and gameplay, but at the same time harmoniously integrate with team tactics and the balance of the game as a whole. For example, at the time of the appearance of the druid in the game there is only one mile class, causing great damage in close combat (Warrior). This means that the Druid in the form of a wild beast will also be a formidable enemy in close combat. Is there only one character girl in the game? So, you need to add one more.







It is important to note that with the advent of the Druid, the team begins to practice a completely new approach to visual stylistics. If earlier the characters were similar to each other in proportion and size, then starting with the Druid, the situation will radically change. Abilities and skills



The next stage of development is the creation of abilities and skills, which the new class will most fully reveal. In addition, it is very important to enter these skills into the ENT and the style of the game and think over the visual component - even on the small screen of the smartphone, the player must clearly distinguish between the characters and understand which one is attacking. Therefore, the team immediately prescribes both mechanics and animation skills.







The developers decided that the Druid was born in the north of the Empire of the Faces, so the character received ice magic. By the way, several ice effects were originally attributed to the Shaman. To separate these classes visually and in meaning, all the ‚Äúice‚Äù is sent to the druid, plants and a ‚Äútropical‚Äù color remain to the Shaman.

The druid in Pixel Wars is somewhat different from other classes - for him a special, additional set of skills has been created that is used when the character takes the form of an animal.







  • Furious jerk. A powerful blow with two paws.

  • Frantic roar. The bear growls at the enemy in front of him, causing damage to it.


What happened as a result









The druid, like the Shaman, is a natural class. In adolescence, Druid children go alone to Bear Island to bow to the animal protector and gain the ability to turn into a beast.







A druid is a long-range character, not the most tenacious, but can for a while become a strong melee.







A key class feature is the switching between faces (forms) in which the character has different abilities and types of attack.







The appearance of the girl is a weak unit with a weak range attack and point support (heal). Her weapon is a ritual tambourine and a short rod.

The developers hope that the new class will appeal to players, and are looking forward to the appearance of druids on the battlefields of the Pixel Wars MMO. Meanwhile, the team is already approaching the next hero.
