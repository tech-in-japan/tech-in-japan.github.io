Raw food diet. Harm or benefit?

There are several reasons why people come to raw food diet. One of them is a strong desire to lose weight. The new diet does not contain a lot of calories or preservatives. In addition, there is no excess salt, which means that water in the human body does not stay for long. Hence the possibility of gradual weight loss.

Despite its merits, there are many facts that indicate the harm of raw food diet. It is worth noting that in some cases this regimen is completely contraindicated.









Raw food diet. Disease Harmful





Doctors do not recommend sticking to a raw food diet if there is an urolithiasis. It is especially important to get nutritious food if there is a violation of the metabolism of phosphorus and calcium. A raw food diet also adversely affects chronic or acute pancreatitis. Health damage is caused by eating only raw foods with gout.





Cholecystitis, peptic ulcer of the intestines and stomach also do not accept raw food diet. Finally, after some surgical interventions, a similar diet is also not worth a long time.





Raw food diet. Harm to the body





Each plant in itself accumulates many harmful substances. All of them adversely affect human health. Of course, with raw food, they all get inside. Constantly accumulating, they can suddenly turn into an unpleasant disease. Of course, all these substances are destroyed if they are thermally processed.





Some raw foods can cause allergies. And indeed, even in a person who simply eats fruits and berries, it can manifest itself. And raw foodists this risk increases.





One of the reasons for refusing to heat treat foods is the loss of valuable vitamins. It is worth reassuring raw foodists. The main thing is to properly cook. However, there is such provitamin A, which can be obtained only by performing heat treatment with the addition of fats for better absorption.





It is very important that raw foodists receive the full range of nutrients. Therefore, knowledge is needed to help balance nutrition. For a person who does not know, a raw food diet is harm.





Bacteria and parasites that come along with raw foods do very much harm to the body. In raw meat and fish, there are also worms that disappear after heat treatment. This means that raw foodists are more susceptible to trichinosis, anisacidosis, opisthorchiasis and other serious and dangerous diseases.
