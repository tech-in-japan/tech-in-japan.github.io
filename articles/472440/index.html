One day at Joker 2019

October 25-26, literally two days later, in St. Petersburg on the territory of the Expoforum will be held Joker 2019 - the international Java-conference for hardcore Java-developers.



This time, the interest in it turned out to be such that the last tickets remain now - so if you are also interested, now is the time to hop on this train. But if you cannot attend the conference for some reason, we have partial consolation: an open broadcast of the first hall on the first day. About why bother watching the broadcast, we have a separate post .



So on October 25 at 9:30 a.m. (Moscow time) it will be possible to follow the link under the cut (owls, of course, will have to wake up a little earlier than usual). Under the cut, we will discuss what is there at the conference, which is not broadcast.

That which cannot be



What will be in the broadcast, we examined above, and what is available only to those who come personally?



At the conference, dissenting speakers will be attacked in discussion areas. This is one of the most important actions at the conference. The speaker has already set the stage, created the mood and is ready to answer many questions, argue and explain his position. The juice itself, you can dive deeper into the topic with an expert. There are frequent cases of fierce battles between opponents of different approaches to the implementation of various tasks.



At the end of the first day (and this will not be broadcast), after the main program, mini-presentations will be held during the party. Every ten minutes a speaker enters the stage with slides, and this is his finest hour: perhaps we will observe the birth of new Joker stars. If you are one of these, and you have something to say, feel free to send an application - you still have time.



Four BOF sessions will also not be broadcast. BOF is now the traditional format for JUG Ru Group conferences. Improvised discussion groups or something like round tables in which everyone can participate. There is no division by speaker and participant: everyone participates on an equal footing.



In addition to the BOF sessions, full-time conference participants can communicate in an informal setting, discuss everything during the party, and also participate in the Mozgoboyne - a bar quiz of questions and answers.



And of course, behind the scenes there will be three rooms completely during the first day, as well as the second day of the conference.



Summarizing



After reading, you have three options for participation:



  • If you want to see the reports of the first day from the first hall - your choice is a free broadcast .
  • If in addition to broadcasting you need all the reports and videos, then online tickets are available .
  • For the most desperate, who wanted to personally participate in the conference, regular tickets are still available.


We look forward to seeing some of you offline, and with others online.
