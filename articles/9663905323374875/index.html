How to reduce the calorie content of fried potatoes

Does high calorie fried potatoes stop you before you cook a whole pan of this delicious dish? If so, then let's figure out how many calories are in fried potatoes, and what to do to make them fewer.





The most high-calorie is the classic recipe for frying: in such potatoes from 280 to 320 kcal per 100 g, depending on the amount of oil that you poured into the pan. It is logical that the less oil, the less calories. Boiled potatoes in general have an energy value of only 80 kcal per 100g, all the rest falls on additional products.

How to cook delicious french fries at home





  1. Peel the potatoes, wash them thoroughly and towel-dry each tuber. If it remains wet, the oil in the pan may flare up.
  2. Cut each potato into thin strips.
  3. Pour 150-200 g into the dishes. Vegetable oil and wait until it warms up well.
  4. Pour potato sticks in small portions of hot oil, stir them occasionally, and take out the finished ones with a slotted spoon.
