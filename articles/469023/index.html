How to find a job with relocation to Europe: a practical guide for IT professionals

In itself, finding a job in IT is quite simple. As for the search for vacancies with the possibility of relocation, here it is already more difficult. Despite the fact that technology companies around the world are now actively hiring developers and other IT specialists from abroad, the search for such vacancies takes a lot of time and effort.



In this article I decided to collect the most popular sites / resources on which it is really possible to find vacancies with moving to European countries.



Job boards for IT professionals and niche sites



Angellist



Today AngelList is one of the most popular startup job sites around the world. The process of finding vacancies with relocation on this site is very simple - in the ‚Äú Compensation ‚Äù tab, select the ‚Äú Company Will Sponsor Visa ‚Äù option. To narrow the list of vacancies to the most relevant, you can use the filters " Role " and " Location ".







Glassdoor



Many of you have probably come across the Glassdoor website at least once. First of all, Glassdoor is known as a resource on which you can read insider reviews about a particular employer, as well as about the interview process and salaries in the company of interest. In addition, the site has a huge number of vacancies in different countries of the world, and not only for IT-specialists.



To find vacancies that offer relocate, write in the search bar relocation developer or relocation and the name of the position you are interested in . To narrow the search results, specify the city / country where you would like to move.







Since there is no special filter on the Glassdoor website for finding work with moving, when searching for vacancies using the method described above, irrelevant ones may appear. In general, the relocation keyword in the search bar does its job.



Relocate.me



In short, all the companies that place vacancies on this site are open to hiring IT specialists from abroad and provide at least a basic relocate package (visa + air flight) when moving. In addition, the site has a lot of useful information about the process of moving to different countries, calculators for calculating the wages ‚Äúon hand‚Äù, as well as the history of those who moved.

The site is dedicated to finding work in the field of IT in Europe. Most of the vacancies presented on the site are in Portugal , but there are also vacancies in other EU countries. There is a ‚Äú Visa & Work permit ‚Äù filter on the job search page. Using it, we get these 2 lists:

JobsInTech



JobsInTech is a small site dedicated to finding work in the IT field. To view all the vacancies that offer a relocate package / visa assistance, you can simply click on this link . The site contains vacancies both in Europe and abroad.



TechMeAbroad



Like Relocate.me, only those companies looking for / willing to hire IT specialists from abroad publish vacancies on the TechMeAbroad website.



Github jobs



Another well-known site in IT circles that works great in terms of finding vacancies with relocation. In the search bar, simply enter our keywords:



  • Relocation support
  • Relocation assistance

As the name suggests, the site is dedicated to finding work in startups. The site presents both onsite and remote positions in startups around the world.

Firstly, in most cases, the recruiter will be able to provide you with a lot of useful information about the company (projects, team, interview process, etc.). Secondly, it will tell you in detail about the relocate package that the company offers, as well as about the process of moving.

Search Sites and Aggregator Sites



Indeed

Indeed is the largest of all job aggregators to date. Searching for vacancies with relocation should begin by choosing a country to relocate to . At the request of " relocation developer " or " relocation " + any other name for the position that interests you, the site gives a large number of vacancies, where, among other "goodies", help is indicated during the move (relocate package).







Here are some links to get you started:



  • Dev jobs with relocation to Germany
  • Dev jobs with relocation in Netherlands
  • Dev jobs with relocation to the Czech Republic
  • Dev jobs with relocation to Spain


Indeed also has the option of subscribing to e-mail notifications of new vacancies similar to your search query.



Monster

Like Indeed, Monster leads the way in job search resources. By clicking on this link , we get to a page with a list of Monster sites in various countries. We select the country we plan to move to, and in the search bar we enter relocation and the position of interest, for example, developer .







Since Monster sites do not have a special filter for finding work with relocation, when searching for vacancies using the method described above, irrelevant ones may appear. In general, the relocation keyword helps to filter out the vacancies we need, especially in countries such as Germany and the Netherlands , which are famous for the active involvement of foreign IT specialists.



Karriere.at

Karriere.at is the largest job search site in Austria . In order to view all vacancies with the possibility of relocation, enter relocation support or visa sponsorship in the search bar. To narrow the list of vacancies to the most relevant, try also adding the name of the position that interests you.

Other options



Vanhack



Jobs on this platform are primarily worth looking for those who would like to find work in Canada . Positions in European countries are also presented on the site, but there are not many of them. All companies whose vacancies are posted on the VanHack website are ready to consider candidates from abroad.



Techloop



On the Techloop website you can create an anonymous resume (without a name and other data identifying you) and solely thanks to your knowledge and ability to receive an offer. If the company contacts you, that is, is clearly interested in your candidacy, then most likely it will be ready to help you with relocate.
