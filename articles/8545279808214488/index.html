Caloric benefits of the product. Structure





So, asparagus is a low-calorie product. There are only 30 kcal per 100 g of vegetable. In most vegetables, calories are an order of magnitude higher. The main component of the product is water (over 90%). The plant is almost fat free. The concentration of proteins and carbohydrates is 1.9 and 4.5%, respectively. Asparagus abounds with such beneficial substances as vitamins (A, C, B1, B2), potassium, magnesium, selenium and calcium. Also in its composition is folic acid.
