Advantech AIoT InnoWorks Student Program



As part of the AIoT Developer InnoWorks program, we work with universities to support the training of specialists in industrial automation, smart manufacturing, energy, and healthcare. The program is designed to bring together the best students and developers around the world in the field of IoT and, in addition to Russia, is held in Japan, South Korea, China, Vietnam and Taiwan.



To participate in the project competition, 20 teams of 5 people were selected from various universities in Russia, whose task was to develop a technological project based on the Advantech WISE-PaaS platform. Finalists of the competition took part in the September conference in Moscow, where they presented their projects to specialists and company executives.







Meteolab Project



The winner of the InnoWorks contest was the Meteolab project, which the guys themselves described in our blog in a separate article: Monitoring air quality in a cottage village.



The jury praised the project for its relevance and relevance in modern conditions, when air pollution is becoming an increasing problem. The potential of the LoRaWAN wireless protocol and the Advantech WISE-6610 gateway are well revealed in the project.







Summary



We are pleased that the conference attracted a large number of specialists from various industries. The discussions turned out to be lively and informative, manufacturers were able to share their experience and listen to suggestions from colleagues and developers who are just exploring new products and planning to implement.



Conference attendees could familiarize themselves with the solutions in the field of the Internet of Things in the demo zone, where demo stands were installed with solutions of partners who use Advantech products in their work.





We thank everyone who participated in the conference and will be glad to see you at our future events.
