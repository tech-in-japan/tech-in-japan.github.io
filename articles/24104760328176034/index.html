Beverage Recipes

The healing elixir from the Chinese sea mushroom, the benefits and harms of which this article is devoted to, is prepared in an elementary way. Here is the first way:





  • Take 1 liter of cold purified water.
  • Add to it sea rice (2 tablespoons).
  • Pour sugar (2 tbsp) previously diluted in a small amount of water. It is important! If sugar crystals get into rice, then he will get sick.
  • Add 5 large fresh raisins to the mass.

After 1-2 days, the drink will be ready. It needs to be filtered, and the raisins thrown away. Rice should be washed with boiled water at room temperature and reused.









The second method is also elementary:





  • Take 1 tbsp. rice.
  • Pour it with settled cold water in an amount of 500 ml.
  • Add dissolved sugar (1 tbsp) and 10-15 raisins. They can be replaced with dried apricots, but if you take dried apricot, then it will be enough in the amount of 3-4 pieces.
  • Add dry kvass (1 tsp). As an alternative, a piece of dried gray bread is suitable.
  • Place the mass in a jar of glass and cover with gauze, then put the container in a cool, bright place, but in no case in the sun.
  • Give 48 hours to brew.
  • After time, also strain and refrigerate.

How to take such a drink? 100 ml is enough 10 minutes before each meal. But it is not forbidden to use it at will. Such an elixir of health perfectly quenches thirst.
