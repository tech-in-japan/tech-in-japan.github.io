Mi Notebook Pro - Maximum Performance







In addition to two smartphones, at an event in Beijing, Xiaomi showed an updated Mi Notebook Pro laptop. This time the manufacturer focused all his efforts on performance, but did not forget about compactness: thanks to the thin frames around the screen, this computer is considered one of the smallest in its segment. The weight in the magnesium alloy body is less than 2 kilograms.



It is based on the eighth generation Intel Core processor. If the core version comes with a dual-core Core i5 8250U, then the quad-core Core i7 8550U chip is already installed in the advanced model. The processor is complemented by 8 GB of RAM and a 256 GB solid state drive. The graphics rendering on the Full HD display provides the NVIDIA GeForce MX150 with 2 GB of memory. Speaking of the screen - here it is protected by Gorilla Glass, the display gives 72% of the NTSC color range. Symmetrical cooling system consists of two fans for maximum efficiency. The laptop is equipped with speakers Harman Infinity.







Separately, it is worth noting the keyboard. Besides the fact that it is almost 20% more than the MacBook Pro, the keyboard is equipped with a backlight. The touchpad, located under the keyboard, boasts the presence of a fingerprint scanner.







Unlike Apple laptops, Mi Notebook Pro is pleased with the variety of ports. Here and two ports USB-C, and two ports USB 3.0, and even full HDMI. In addition, there was a place for a memory card slot and a 3.5-millimeter headphone jack. Charging is also carried out using USB-C, and fast charging allows you to fill up the charge level up to 50% a little more than half an hour.
