Nuts - an indispensable storehouse of protein

The best vegetable protein found in nuts. They are great for vegetarians as well as athletes.





Pine nut





The champion among nuts is cedar. Unfortunately, the price of this product is quite high, and for this reason it is not available to everyone. The composition of this nut includes a large number of nutrients and fatty acids, and the protein content in it exceeds 60%. This product is quite suitable for use as a separate meal.





Peanut





This nut is available to everyone and has about 40% protein. Eating it is best raw.





Almond





The product is inferior to the first two in the protein content in its composition, its amount is in the range of 35%, but it has an advantage, it is nutritious and it contains a lot of polyunsaturated fatty acids. Just a hundred grams of such a nut is enough for a normal snack.













Protein-rich cereals

Many people think that cereals contain only complex carbohydrates, and few people know about the protein content in them. Some cereals have a lot of vegetable protein in their composition.





Buckwheat





Among cereals, it contains the largest amount of vegetable protein - 18%. For what reason, buckwheat is not very common abroad, it is not known, but in the open spaces of the former Soviet Union it is a very popular product.





Oatmeal





This cereal contains a lot of complex carbohydrates, and it is rich in fiber, it is known to many, and most love to eat it for breakfast. But oatmeal also contains quite a lot of vegetable protein - 12%.





Wheat groats





Wheat groats are popular only in the CIS, nothing is known about it abroad, but in fact vegetable protein is quite enough in it, a little less than buckwheat - 16%.





Lentils





Many people heard about it, but not everyone tried it. The content of vegetable protein in lentils is simply huge - 25%. And also it contains such beneficial substances as potassium, magnesium, zinc and B vitamins, it is very useful for those who follow a diet.
