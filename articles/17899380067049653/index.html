Can there be a thrush without secretions?

In the article, we will examine whether there can be a thrush without curdled discharge.





Vaginal candidiasis (thrush) appears due to the multiplication of such pathogenic fungi of the genus Candida. In a healthy body, these fungi are on the surfaces of mucous membranes, their amount is regulated, since their growth is constantly inhibited. If conditions arise under which active reproduction of fungi can occur, then pathology will not be long in coming.





Can there be a thrush without secretions? The answer is yes, and such situations are common. The danger of thrush without secretions is that it is harder to recognize than a disease with characteristic symptoms caused by fungi.

  • pads and tampons;

  • dramatic climate change;
  • pathological desire for perfect cleanliness.

The probability of reproduction of pathogenic microflora increases with the influence of several negative factors at the same time.
