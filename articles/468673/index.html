Workshop "Ensuring the security of personal data" - October 3, St. Petersburg





Good afternoon! Linxdatacenter is pleased to invite you to a seminar on the protection of personal data!



Date: October 3.

Location: Linxdatacenter Data Center, St. Petersburg, ul. Repishcheva, d.20A.



What will be discussed:



  • Maintenance of FFPA: what is important to know?
  • Awareness of business needs and selection of a contractor.
  • Audit of business processes: how to establish interaction for fruitful work.
  • The main risks in the field of information security today.
  • Data security in cloud services.
  • Requests for digitalization of internal processes, taking into account security requirements.
  • Features of the legislation on personal data and electronic signatures.


The program of the event:



  • 9: 30-10: 00 Welcome coffee.
  • 10: 00-10: 30 Personal data protection system: from implementation requirements. Olga Ermakova, Senior Legal Advisor and Compliance Specialist, Linxdatacenter.
  • 10: 30-10: 45 Break.
  • 10: 45-11: 15 The main problems of ensuring information security. Boris Merkulov, Linxdatacenter Cloud and Information Security Engineer.
  • 11: 15-11: 45 Prospects for obtaining consent to the processing of biometric personal data in the form of an electronic document. Daria Prokhorova, Head of Project Legal Support, GAZPROMNEFT STC LLC.
  • 11: 45-12: 30 Coffee break | Tour of the data center.


The event is free, registration on the Linxdatacenter website .
