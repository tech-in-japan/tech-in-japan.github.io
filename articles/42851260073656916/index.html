What should not be eaten while losing weight? Proper nutrition for weight loss

Currently, almost every representative of the fairer sex is making every effort to ensure that her figure is slim. It is very fashionable to meet the parameters of 90 x 60 x 90 today, so the question of what should not be eaten while losing weight is haunting young people (and not only).





Of course, the desire to lose weight is commendable, but one should not go too far in this effort, as they say. Very many girls, having reviewed their diet and losing 5-6 "extra" pounds, do not stop there and continue to lose weight and lose weight. As a result, they "fall into the network" of an insidious pathology called "anorexia". Remember that everything should be a measure.





Of course, those who repeatedly sat on all kinds of diets are well aware that you can not eat with weight loss. However, even they do not understand that if the body requires "Sprite" or fried potatoes, then they should not be deprived of these dishes. The main thing is not to use them often and in large quantities. You must understand that a person should enjoy eating.

















At the same time, if the situation is extremely neglected, and the young lady "carries" an extra twenty pounds, then, of course, she should at first strictly monitor what should not be eaten while losing weight, and there should be no talk of any sandwich with butter can not.

Prohibited Food List

It should be noted that the list of food products that are banned for those who want to lose weight is very impressive. We list only the main ones.

















Carbonated drinks

First of all, you should abandon any carbonated drinks, among which, of course, Coca-Cola is in the first place.





They contain sugar in their composition, so when you use them, it will be difficult for you to lose weight.





Fast food

Fast food enthusiasts should also review their diet. What foods can not be eaten while losing weight? Naturally, cheeseburgers, hamburgers, french fries and other similar dishes. Such food is extremely harmful because it contains a huge amount of cholesterol.

















Meat

What other foods can not be eaten while losing weight? Taboo applies primarily to smoked, cooked sausages, ham, sausages, as this food contains a lot of fat and very little meat. However, this does not mean at all that nutrition during weight loss excludes meat consumption. On the contrary, nutritionists strongly recommend that it be present in the diet. Remember that meat is a source of protein, which is the main building material in our body. You can eat boiled chicken or cook, for example, steamed turkey fillet. In no case should you eat fried meat, such as meatballs or pasties.









Bread and Bakery

White wheat bread, as well as donuts, buns, cakes, custard, cookies and gingerbread should also be discarded. Flour immediately adds extra pounds. As for bread, it is better to prefer a loaf of rye flour to a white loaf.





Sweet desserts

It should be noted that sweets when losing weight should also be excluded. Girls who like chocolate, jam, jams, condensed milk and other goodies will have to demonstrate great willpower, adhering to a particular diet. All of the above products are classified as high-calorie.

















Potatoes and cereals

Often they write that proper nutrition during weight loss excludes the use of foods such as cereals and potatoes.





However, such a point of view cannot be considered one hundred percent correct. The above dishes are present in a variety of nutrition systems with the effect of weight loss. Well, which of us has not heard about oat, buckwheat or rice diet? It should be noted that with the help of them people get rid of a huge amount of extra pounds.





As for potatoes, we can say the following: you should not eat it in fried form, especially when it is cooked in sunflower oil. But in a boiled or baked version, this food product is allowed, but with the reservation: not at night and not three times a day.

Fruits

Naturally, fruits must be eaten regularly, because they abound in vitamins and useful trace elements that the body needs for a full life.





However, people who control their own weight should remember that pears or grapes should be eaten in strictly dosed quantities. Why? This is due to the fact that most natural foods contain an incredible amount of fructose - a monosaccharide that is slowly absorbed by the body, and a significant part of it is deposited in the form of fat reserves. Moreover, it should be remembered that fructose cannot cause a person to feel full, on the contrary, after bananas and raisins, appetite becomes even greater. It is not recommended to eat fruit as a main course and have a snack between lunch and dinner. This is also not a good idea.





So, if you are determined to lose weight, you should give up products such as figs, bananas, dried fruits, pomegranates, mangoes, grapes.





Know the measure in food

Nutritionists recommend eating at least five times a day, while servings should be small. Before breakfast (in 30 minutes) make it a rule to drink a glass of water, so you can activate the gastrointestinal tract. Try to eat just cooked food.
