Blood-thinning and blood clotting foods: a list of the most effective

Sometimes to strengthen blood vessels in the human body, blood thickens, namely, the amount of fibrin increases. Because of this, when veins are damaged, blood clots form. If the blood is too thick, then calcium salts, fats and clots settle on the walls of the arteries, thereby provoking atherosclerosis. With improper nutrition, the situation only worsens. If blood-thinning products are not consumed, blood clots condense and, if they enter the heart, cause a heart attack, stroke in the brain, and pulmonary embolism in the lung.

All processes occurring in the body depend on the quality of the blood. If her condition worsens, then health problems may arise. The main reasons why it thickens are the following factors:

  • general oxidation of the body;

Of course, it is impossible to prevent the emergence of certain circumstances, because it is so important to try to reduce any risks, therefore it is recommended to use products that thin the blood and blood clots.

Despite the fact that this problem is very common and can occur in anyone, there are cases when patients are more susceptible to the onset of the disease.





Increased blood coagulation is often found with:





  • any forms of coronary heart disease ;

It must be remembered that all people after 50 years of age need to consume blood thinning foods. This is due to the fact that during this period the likelihood of cardiovascular problems increases due to internal changes in the composition of the blood.
