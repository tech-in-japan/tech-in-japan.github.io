Blockchain and smart contract



In the last quarter of 2017, on the CarTaxi platform, we planned to activate the blockchain technology based on the Ethereum smart contract. We need blockchain in order to simplify, speed up and make as much as possible controlled all orders when entering the global market. With the help of a smart contract we are going to:



  • register performers and partners;
  • register customers (car owners);
  • create orders and manage their life cycle;
  • make calculations with performers and partners;
  • make the company transparent to investors.


At the same time, each participant will be able to see all the data he needs through the interface of the smart contract, for example, the start and end of the vehicle transportation or the transfer of rewards. In each order, the smart contract will take into account the coordinates of the client, the parameters of his car, the estimated time of arrival of the tow truck.



If the tow truck could not arrive within the specified time, it is subject to sanctions. At the place where you need to pick up the car, the performer with the help of the system checks the order data and fixes the damage to the car. If the data does not match, the smart contract will not allow to proceed to the next stage - loading the car. Upon completion of the order, the program analyzes the final parameters and calculates the contractor.



An important point is the control of order fulfillment and car insurance in all orders. CarTaxi plans to provide long-distance car transportation services, between cities and countries, in complex logistics chains. For example, from Stavropol today you can bring a car to St. Petersburg with the help of CarTaxi.
