After the entry into force of the new Federal Law dated 01.05.2019 No. 90- "On Amendments to the Federal Law" On Communications "and the Federal Law" On Information, Information Technologies and the Protection of Information ".







A new wave of indignation has appeared over a large number of Runet users, and especially IT specialists, but we must understand that the law has already been adopted and entered into legal force. What does this mean?







It is assumed that an independent infrastructure will be created for the smooth functioning of the Internet in Russia.







Indeed, most root DNS servers are located outside the territory of the Russian Federation (root DNS servers respond to requests from other DNS servers during translation of domain names into IP addresses and allow you to get a list of DNS servers for any top-level domain), and the Internet itself is built on the principles of ARPANET (created in 1969 by order of the American agency DARPA).







And with the global "militia" in our country, we must have the Internet resources operational if it is impossible to connect Russian telecom operators to foreign Internet root servers.
