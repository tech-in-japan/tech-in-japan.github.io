Crisis in the Kremlin



A slightly belated ideological sabotage - in the year of the release of the game, the USSR was already ruined. And so - the game begins in 1985, the reins of the USSR, budget management engines and crises raining one after another are awarded. Hunger, Chernobyl, strikes, the collapse of the country ... At the same time, the message of famine in the USSR is accompanied by news that "hunger was defeated forever in the USA." Until 1991, the crises are historical, and then begins the fantasy and the repetition of the old. Chernobyl exploded several times. The collapse of the country here is inevitable, you can only slightly delay. And with the separation of the republics, the budget is reduced, it is necessary to cut spending, new crises - and again, someone is separated. My record - somewhere up to 2040 reached, remaining with one Moscow region in the end.

Paperboy 2



The goal is to deliver the newspaper to subscribers in a couple of blocks. Difficulties - the newspaper must be accurately thrown into the box.
