From the beginnings to the present day













Goji berry (wolf berry, Goji, common dereza, Tibetan barberry) is an evergreen shrub that grows in the subtropical climate of China, Tibet and Mongolia. Belongs to the family of nightshade plants, reaches a height of 2.5 - 3 meters.





Dereza vulgaris was discovered by Chinese monks about 5,000 years ago. Ancient people experimented with Tibetan barberry: they used it fresh and understood how to brew goji berries.





The positive properties of ordinary dereza, such as the normalization of the liver, kidneys and endocrine glands, have been tested by time. The fruits of the tree were used in Taoist, Tibetan, Chinese and other oriental medicine.









Soon the miraculous berry became known not only to monks, and ordinary Chinese. Further, it spread throughout the Asian continent and around the world. In Europe, the first Goji was sold in specialized stores. A certificate of quality and instructions on how to brew goji berries were attached to a small package.

How to brew goji berries?

From dried fruits, you can get both aromatic tea and healing broth. It all depends on how to brew goji berries.





For losing weight people, a decoction is ideal. In this section, we will tell you how to brew goji berries. But this information is not enough. How much to brew goji berries and how to get fragrant tea from dried fruits?

















The easiest way is to brew a medicinal product in boiling water. You will need a small 250 ml teapot and one tablespoon of berries. Pour dried fruits into a container and pour them with boiling water. After 20 minutes, the healing broth is ready for use! Such a drink should be taken three times a day in half a glass.





The second way is to add goji berries to the boiling water. How to make tea? Very simple - you need to mix a pinch of dried fruit with your favorite tea. A great idea is to brew goji berries in a thermos. So you will not part with your favorite tonic drink even at work!

How to distinguish an original berry from a fake?

Unfortunately, recently, cases of selling fake products have become more frequent. Instead of the Tibetan berry, dried barberry or tinted cranberries are put in the package. In order not to become a victim of scammers, you need to know what a real dried fruit looks like. We list the signs that make it possible to say with accuracy that this is a real Tibetan barberry:





  • Natural color.
  • Size from 1 to 1.5 cm.
  • Sweet and sour taste.
  • Dry and hard consistency, towards the end the fruit of the ordinary dereza is sharpened.
  • When brewing, the dried fruits float up, and the water turns light yellow.
  • The resulting broth is sweet and tastes good.




If you order goji berries online, then you should pay attention to the resource offering the product.





Signs of a suspicious site

  • Obsessive presentation of information.
  • The absurdity of the results. It is clear that the promise to lose weight in three days by 12 kg is a clear exaggeration.
  • The price of the goods is too high.
  • Too low product price. Chances are good to get a fake.
  • The exact number of dried berries per package is not indicated.

Sometimes the fruits of dereza can be seen in ordinary stores. Most often, such a product of medium quality and produced in Russia. The best option is to order a small portion of goji berries online. It is necessary to give preference to foreign online stores with a clear site interface.





Follow these simple rules, beware of fakes!
