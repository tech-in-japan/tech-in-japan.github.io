Telegram bot showing the front pages of the 10 most popular world periodicals

Now you can often hear what the Russian media are forming, or someone is forcing them to form a limited and essentially propaganda information agenda. I decided to test to the best of my abilities and desires is this so? Why wrote Telegram bot, which every day can send the first pages of the top 10 most popular world newspapers, such as:







  1. The new york times
  2. The guardian
  3. China Daily
  4. USA Today
  5. Bild
  6. The washington post
  7. The asian age
  8. The wall street journal
  9. The japan times
  10. Los angeles times


The bot daily determines the 20 most frequently used words. In addition, you can search by tag and subscribe to specific tags. As soon as the selected tag appears in the newspaper, a notification will come.







After exploring the world media with the help of this bot, I can say one thing - not everything is as bad with us as it seemed to me before.







If you are interested in trying the bot in action, please Main press pages @press_pages_bot .
