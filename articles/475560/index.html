KotlinConf 2019 Live: Watch Live December 5-6

KotlinConf broadcasts are becoming a tradition. This year, the global community of Kotlin users will be able to see the conference live - all the speeches, not just announcements and news. A pleasant bonus to live broadcasting will be 12 interviews with speakers, which will be conducted by the host of the popular YouTube channel Android Dialogues, Huyen Tue Dao , over two days.



The broadcast will begin with a presentation by the head of the Kotlin development team Andrei Breslav, then two full days of reports and at the end the final panel, during which the speakers together with the Kotlin development team will answer community questions. You can also take part in the discussion - ask questions on Twitter with the hashtag # kc19ask right now, and watch live to hear the answers.



There is another way to join KotlinConf 2019 - organize a meeting within the framework of the KotlinConf 2019 Global program to view the conference remotely with your community, and JetBrains will support your undertaking. Read our blog post , apply and get support.









What how when



The broadcast will begin on December 5 at 11:00 Moscow time with the cinema out of Andrei Breslav. After that, you will be able to watch speeches in any of the four conference streams, with the exception of the Hands-on Labs practical training section. The schedule of 12 interviews that Huyen Tue Dao will conduct will be announced shortly, so stay tuned on @kotlinconf. The final panel will begin broadcasting on December 6 at 18:15 Moscow time .



You can connect to the broadcast on the kotlinconf.com homepage at any time. Subscribe to notifications not to miss the beginning and the final panel. We will send you a reminder 24 hours before the movieout and one more thing as soon as it starts.



If you are unable to watch the broadcast of a specific performance or it is too difficult to choose between four streams, do not worry - all reports will be recorded and uploaded to the KotlinConf website. As soon as these entries are published, we will notify you by email.



Enjoy your KotlinConf 2019!
