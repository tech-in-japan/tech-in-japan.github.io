How to solve current IT problems in the management of equipment repairs

The more information you have about the condition of the equipment, the more predictable for you the work of the entire enterprise. To cope with the increasing flow of data, highlight the main thing and suggest what needs to be done, modern IT repair management systems will help.



To learn all about equipment repairs, from accounting to predictive analytics, and to see the domestic TURBO TOPO system in action, we invite everyone to the free webinar on October 17 at 11:00 Moscow time.









Technical renovation of production is gradually gaining momentum - factories are actively buying new equipment and get additional economic benefits from its use. And this is true, because modern equipment not only fulfills its main production function, but also opens up new opportunities for control over the general technical structure of the enterprise.



Such opportunities include obtaining relevant and timely information about the condition of the equipment. The high price of failure and unexpected equipment failure is not only the cost of restoration, but also the risk of disruption of production plans and, as a result, falling profits. Failure of only one of the links in the chain can lead to consequences, the losses from which will not be comparable with the cost of repair.



At the webinar, we will talk about a modern approach to managing equipment repairs, share our practical experience and the experience of partners in building integrated solutions and optimizing the costs of building up spare parts and replacement stocks, and, of course, we will address the issues of expanding the capabilities of the system through IoT, predictive analytics and cloud resources.
