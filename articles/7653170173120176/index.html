Is it possible to lose weight if there is little to eat: serving size, calories, nutritional value and weight loss

Many people who want to get rid of extra pounds believe that it is enough to put a lock on the refrigerator and not eat at all. But this opinion is completely erroneous. The body understands complete hunger as a distress signal and begins to strenuously guard the available savings in case of an unforeseen situation. This is a stressful situation that should not be allowed in any case.





Then a no less urgent question arises: "If there is little to eat, is it possible to lose weight?" The answer here is given by nutritionists unambiguously positive. It is even advised to purchase a smaller plate. In overweight people, the stomach is stretched from plentiful food, and at first there will be a feeling of hunger, as a small portion of the final satiety will not work out.





In the article, we consider whether it is possible to lose weight if there is not enough. How to gradually adjust the body that much less food is needed for saturation than was previously absorbed. How to compensate for the decrease in the amount of food consumed for the stomach, so as to feel as calm as possible. Consider the useful tips of people who have coped with the task and without much effort reduced their weight to normal.





Mini portions

Overeating is an enemy for any organism. A heavily filled stomach can not always keep such an amount of food in itself, sometimes it is thrown back into the esophagus. It threatens with reflux disease and esophagitis. This is an inflammation of the mucous membrane of the esophagus, accompanied by pain and heartburn. Overweight people often suffer from both.









Can I lose weight if I eat in small portions? Nutritionists advise this method to all their clients, however, like gastroenterologists. You need to eat in smaller portions, but more often. Meals should be 5-6 times a day. This is breakfast, lunch and dinner, and in between take small snacks to fill the stomach with something.









According to nutritionists, for weight loss, the first thing you need to do is change the plate to a much smaller size. I automatically want to put more on a large dish, add a salad or side dish. A small plate will not give you such an opportunity, thus there will be no overeating.





We only eat homemade food

If you are interested in the question of whether it is possible to lose weight, if there is little, then pay attention to the reviews of people who have cope with the problem of excess weight in the following simple method. Stop eating fast foods and fast food. Speaking of snacks, we did not mean hamburgers or hot dogs. Unlearn such food forever.





If you want to eat between the main meals, then have a snack with dried fruits, nuts, a fresh apple or carrot. This will fill the stomach but not overload it. A glass of low-fat kefir will help satisfy your hunger.









Do not eat on the go, set aside time for this, sit back, eat slowly, slowly and chewing food carefully. Stretch out the pleasure and give the stomach time to deal with food without overcrowding.

More vegetables

Vegetables contain fiber and many healthy vitamins needed by the body. This gives a feeling of satiety, in addition, fiber is processed for a long time by the stomach, which will increase the gap between meals. It is advisable to consult with a gastroenterologist or nutritionist before such a diet. They can say for sure whether it is possible to lose weight, if there is little to eat, they will advise whether weight loss on vegetables is suitable for your body. If you have gastrointestinal diseases, then exacerbation can occur from a large amount of fiber.









If there are no contraindications, then fresh salads, greens, beets, lettuce, zucchini and pumpkin, and eggplant will help you reduce weight. Of particular note is celery. It helps to get rid of extracellular fat. The diet contains not only dishes with celery, but also freshly squeezed juice.





Increase your intake of fresh vegetables. Season salads with vegetable or olive oil, low-fat sour cream or yogurt. Pamper yourself with variety, and then the diet will not be a burden.





From vegetables, you can prepare a side dish for meat dishes, making them steamed or baked in the oven. For lunch, cook yourself a bean vegetable soup without adding potatoes.





Only delicious food

Can I lose weight if I eat a little? Reviews of people who have cut their servings, confirm that this method is quite suitable for losing weight. But it is advised to cook tasty and eat, savoring every bite. It is very important psychologically to set yourself up for eating less. Rebuild your body in such a way that absorbing less food is more fun.





Before a delicious dinner, enjoy the appearance of the dish, then inhale its pleasant aroma, take a small piece in your mouth and slowly sip it. Then, chewing thoroughly, hold as long as possible in your mouth.









Do not frustrate yourself with a tasteless oatmeal or something that you don't like at all. Pamper yourself with delicious food, because food that brings joy makes us happy. If you yourself are not able to come up with tasty and healthy dishes, consult a nutritionist. They will write you a delicious diet for a week, which people who are not interested in the question of whether you can lose weight while eating less will envy.





Refrigerator cleaning

If you decide to pay utmost attention to your figure and proper nutrition, then first conduct a thorough cleaning of your refrigerator. Remove all greasy, sugary and smoked foods. Get rid of mayonnaise and spicy sauces, pickled foods and canned foods.









Make a list of prohibited foods and put it right in front of your eyes on the refrigerator, if you do not live separately, and your family members do not participate in weight loss activities.

Physical activity

If you are interested in the question of whether it is possible to lose weight, if there is very little to eat, then judging by the reviews of people and experts, we can conclude that fasting and a strong reduction in the amount of food consumed will not lead you to the cherished goal, but only affect your health.
