Telefuel - an improved Telegram client, similar to Slack



With the growing popularity of Telegram and using it to get a wide variety of information, the problem of flooding and sorting notifications has appeared. How often have you not noticed an important message in their total mass?



The main feature of the application is that you have a vertical navigation bar on the left. In this panel you can group chats, and already within these groups sort them by categories. It works like this: the user, for example, creates thematic groups ‚Äúbuyers‚Äù and ‚Äúfamily‚Äù, adds chats there and creates the categories ‚Äúclosed the deal‚Äù, ‚Äúwaiting for payment‚Äù, ‚Äúnegotiation stage‚Äù, ‚Äúpotential buyer‚Äù.
