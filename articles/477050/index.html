Bridge to the cloud







The next model for which we give a discount, Ivideon Bridge is a device that allows you to connect up to 16 cameras (or DVR channels) to our service. Its main purpose is to give users of local systems access to the cloud at minimal cost and without replacing installed equipment.



In terms of price / channel ratio, Bridge is becoming the most profitable way to connect to Ivideon: one channel with Bridge with paid basic archive storage will cost 375 rubles (in prices before Black Friday).



Devices are needed in four cases:



  1. You decided to scale the video surveillance system, bought cameras with Internet access and connected them to the cloud service. Ivideon Bridge will combine old cameras with new ones and allow you to control the entire system through a single interface.
  2. The object is equipped with old-style cameras without the possibility of remote access via the Internet. In this case, Bridge will help you get access to the live broadcast, archive, as well as to configure the cameras.
  3. At several points, CCTV systems from different manufacturers are installed with different management applications, but you want to combine all the systems and control them through one interface.
  4. You want to reduce system maintenance costs. Bridge connects cameras to the cloud, opening up the ability to remotely control devices. You can add cameras through your personal account - the client only connects the devices, and the rest of the actions are performed by a specialist of the service organization remotely.
