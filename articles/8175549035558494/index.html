The list of what you can eat a nursing mother is much wider than the forbidden. This diet is by no means a diet, it is just a system that you should adhere to, so as not to harm yourself and your baby.

Vegetables and fruits are good for everyone, not just nursing mothers. It is important that these are only natural and fresh products, preferably grown independently. Potatoes, asparagus, cucumbers, cabbage, greens, apples, bananas, prunes, pineapple - all this is not only very tasty, but also useful.





Foods that often cause allergies, such as honey or nuts, should be eaten in very limited quantities. They have many vitamins, but overeating can cause itching in the baby.
