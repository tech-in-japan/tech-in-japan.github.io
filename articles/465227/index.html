Augmented Reality in Online Retail

Before we begin the basic study of the material, it is necessary to understand what is online retail?



Retail refers to the retail sale of goods to consumers. Accordingly, any retailer can be called a retailer. It can be either a small store or an international online platform.



Online retail - trade organized with the help of online stores in conjunction with delivery services.



Augmented reality has become an innovative tool that allows brands to interact with consumers on their mobile devices. AR creates a new digital experience that enriches the relationship between the consumer and the brand and can be used anywhere using a mobile device.



In 2018, many companies experimented with augmented reality and implemented advanced technologies in their products and services. Consider ways to promote retail products using augmented reality in mobile applications.

IKEA Place



If you encounter a problem when the furniture did not fit or fit in your room, then this application is for you!



IKEA Place was first published back in 2011, but has been updated several times since its initial release.



The application includes 3D models of all furniture in the company catalog.



To try on furniture, point your camera at the space you want to decorate and start arranging the furniture. Select any IKEA piece of furniture from the IKEA Place main menu, and then move it to the screen to insert it wherever you want. Turn and move it around the room until you find a place where it fits perfectly. Keep adding furniture by combining different objects together to create the perfect home decor.



Another interesting feature of IKEA Place is the ability to search for furniture in a catalog using photography. Just take a picture of any piece of furniture to see information about it in the IKEA catalog, and if it is not IKEA furniture, the application will show you similar options available in IKEA.
