What is mangosteen

Reviews of those who have visited Southeast Asia and tasted this fruit, indicate that it is very unusual, but tasty. Distributed mangosteen in Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand. It contains a persistent dye, which is often used for tanning the skin. The peel is very dense and thick - about a centimeter. Under it is a delicate white pulp in the form of 4-8 cloves, similar to garlic. A very pleasant and unusual taste has mangosteen. Despite the large number of useful properties, it is not recommended to eat these exotic fruits during pregnancy, as well as to people with allergies or with serious chronic diseases of the heart, liver and kidneys. Reviews say that eating it is simple:













  1. Carefully tear off the stalk, as ants often hide under it.
  2. Using a knife or hands, split the fruit in half.
  3. Pull out the pulp slices with your hands or better with a spoon, because they are very juicy.
  4. You need to eat them carefully, since there are seeds inside.

When choosing fruits on the market in Asian countries, you need to squeeze the fruits a little by hand. They should not be too hard, but not soft. Ripe good mangosteen is dense and slightly springy when pressed.
