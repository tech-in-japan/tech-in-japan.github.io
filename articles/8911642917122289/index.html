Juicy and fragrant melon: calories and benefits

Most people think that melon is a fruit, but they are mistaken. Melon comes from the pumpkin family and is a vegetable. The melon ripens from August to September, and it blooms once a year. The length of its stem reaches three meters, depending on the variety, the leaves can be of different shapes and lengths. The fruits of a vegetable are round and oval, orange or light green, inside with a white fragrant flesh.





These delicious vegetables began to grow more than 4000 years ago. Her homeland is Asia Minor, East Indies and Africa. This gourd tolerates drought and heat. It came to Russia from Asia in the 17th century, it began to be grown everywhere - the most favorable climate for this vegetable is considered to be the south of Russia and the Astrakhan region.





In other regions of Russia, melon began to be cultivated in greenhouses, as well as watermelons. Currently, there are practically no places where wild melon grows.
