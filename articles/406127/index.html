6 ideas by Andy Rubin



Essential is designed to make the use of technology as convenient as possible. Although Android has achieved a lot over the past 14 years, this OS is also responsible for making new smartphones more difficult, instead of being simpler. Each manufacturer adds its own applications to the system, extra programs, themes. Many products refuse to work with each other or perform the same function, and users have to put up with it. According to Rubin, he feels responsible for this, and Essential should be one of the first steps towards solving this problem.







In his blog, Rubin published 6 ideas that he took as the basis for working on Essential Products:



1. Gadgets - your personal property. We will not force you to use what you do not want.

2. We will cooperate with others. Closed ecosystems are hopelessly outdated.

3. Access to premium materials and high quality work should not be restricted.

4. Gadgets should not become obsolete every year. They must develop with you.

5. Technology should help you enjoy life.

6. Easier - always better.
