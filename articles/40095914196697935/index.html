Proper nutrition for weight loss - features, menus and recommendations

A beautiful, slender figure is the dream of any girl. But with all this, the number of overweight people is increasing every year. A variety of newfangled diets that promise a cool result, help for a short while. And in some cases, they do not even contribute to a decrease, but rather to an increase in body weight. But there is one diet that helps not only to cope with excess weight, but also improve your health. So what is proper nutrition for weight loss?





Metabolism

The body is a whole system that functions 24 hours a day. All this happens due to the fact that in the process of metabolism a certain amount of energy is generated. If we stopped this process (for example, did not eat for more than a day), the reserves would gradually decrease, a person would feel a breakdown, drowsiness, dizziness.









So, the main function of nutrition is to obtain energy for the life support of the whole organism. If an imbalance occurs in this process, this leads to weight loss or weight gain. In the latter case, the amount of energy consumed is much more than the body needs. And where to go to all superfluous? Of course, it remains on the waist and hips in the form of body fat. So, first of all, proper nutrition for weight loss is based on the fact that you need to consume a certain amount of calories. How to calculate them?

Daily calorie intake

The energy value of a product is important information for those who want to lose weight. On each product that we see on the shelf of the store, there is information about how much in 100 grams contains:





  • calories
  • proteins;
  • carbohydrates;
  • fats.

For those dishes that you cook at home, the calculation can also be done. The daily calorie intake depends on many factors: weight, height, gender, age, degree of physical activity. At the same time, if you want to lose weight, 15% of the total amount should be subtracted from the calculated number of calories. Consider an example. Let's say we have a girl with parameters:





  • weight - 89 kg;
  • height - 158 cm;
  • age - 28 years.

Degree of physical loads - a sedentary lifestyle.





The formula for the calculation is as follows:





655 + 9.6 * (body weight, kg) + 1.8 * (height, cm) - 4.7 * (age).





In our case:





655+ 9.6 * (89) + 1.8 * (158) - 4.7 * (28) = 1662 kcal.





This figure shows the minimum number of calories needed. It must be multiplied by the coefficient of physical activity.





  • A sedentary lifestyle - 1.3.
  • Normal physical activity is 1.5.
  • Intensive training - 1.7.

The resulting value (2160) will be the maximum daily calorie intake; eating above it is not recommended. Of course, in general, everything depends on the human body, and sometimes such formulas do not work. If after a month of proper nutrition for weight loss, you do not feel the result, then the number of calories should be reduced.













Food quality

Since the main function of nutrition is to provide the body with the necessary energy, the quality of the products themselves in this case plays an important role. The daily diet should consist of carbohydrates, proteins and fats in the proportion of 50%, 30% and 20%, respectively. Proper nutrition for weight loss is based only on healthy foods that contain all the necessary elements and minerals. Many people mistakenly believe that in order to lose weight, you need to exclude fats. However, this is a misconception. Lipids are very important for any organism. Their deficiency can cause excessive dryness of the skin, the appearance of wrinkles. Best of all, if 20% of the fat in your diet will be included in such products: extra virgin olive and corn oil, red fish, nuts. But trans fats (cakes, pastries, fast food, food cooked in fried oil) should be removed from your diet.

What and when to eat?

So what is proper nutrition for weight loss? The menu for every day can look very diverse, but at the same time, the person will not feel hungry. So, the daily calorie intake, which we have already calculated, should be divided into 5 meals. There will be three main ones (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and two snacks. Servings should be small, not more than 200 grams.





The first meal should be sufficiently nutritious and contain a large amount of carbohydrates. So, porridge is perfect: oatmeal, buckwheat, bulgur. To them you can cook a vegetable salad or boiled lean meat. Lunch should be full and hearty. It may include liquid (soup, borsch), as well as the second - porridge, potatoes.
