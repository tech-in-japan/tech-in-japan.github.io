Vegetable harm

It is important to consider all contraindications to beets. And also follow the rules and recommendations so that it brings maximum benefits to the body.





Despite all the useful properties of red beets, there are still contraindications and harm from it. That is why you need to remember the restrictions on the use of this root crop in fresh and boiled form. Among the contraindications for beets, it is necessary to highlight:





  • osteoporosis;
  • urolithiasis;
  • gastritis;
  • some heart disease;
  • an allergy.

With urolithiasis, it is strictly forbidden to use beets and drink the juice of this vegetable, since it contains oxalic acid. If a person has osteoporosis or is predisposed to the development of this disease, then he is recommended to limit himself in the use of beets and beetroot juice. This is due to the fact that this vegetable prevents the full absorption of calcium.





With gastritis, experts also do not recommend including beet dishes in the menu. This vegetable in raw and boiled form increases the acidity of the stomach, even despite its natural sweetish taste.









Do not include beetroot foods in your diet if you have a tendency to heartburn, colitis, and internal bleeding. Among the contraindications to beets, even minor bowel problems should be noted. When combining root vegetables with garlic, there is a risk that this can overload the heart muscle.





It is worth noting that this root crop has the ability to accumulate harmful and toxic substances from the soil, therefore, in order to avoid poisoning, it is not recommended to eat vegetables grown in ecologically unfavorable areas.
