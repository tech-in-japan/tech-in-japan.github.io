September 3 - CTO Day in St. Petersburg

September 3 in St. Petersburg, in the conference room Selectel spend CTO Day!







The celebration is organized by Dmitry Simonov, creator of the CTDEC channel CTORECORDS













Slurm takes care of organizational matters, Selectel provides premises, SimbirSoft pays for beer and pizza for guests.







We invite service stations, IT managers and everyone who wants to become one!







An open broadcast, and then the recording will be on the site https://cto-day.ru







We are going to gather at 18:30, at 19:00 short reports begin.

We will analyze what CTO is doing, what resources it manages, what skills, solutions and actions are required from it, what problems it faces.







Admission is free, by pre-registration here: https://cto-day.ru
