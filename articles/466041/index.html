The first offline event of the Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow community - Launch Event





Starting a new career can be difficult, especially when moving from a specialty with a humanitarian bias to a technical one. Where to start, how can you develop your skills? How and where can a job be found very quickly? Although there are a lot of online resources now, conversations and meetings with others like you from the offline community will become more useful.







Already in more than 100 cities around the world launched the program Developer Circle from Facebook. And finally, in Russia, in Moscow, this program is also being launched. In this community, developers will be able to learn from each other and share best practices, find new friends.







On September 10, Developer Circle: Moscow from Facebook officially opens. The speakers from the Facebook office in London, as well as ours, Russian, will share their knowledge and best practices. After technical presentations, there will be food and drinks, where you can socialize in an informal setting.







This mitap is the first of many speaker events, hackathons and mentor sessions that will be regularly held on Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow. To stay updated, join the group here .







You can attend the event by registering here .









Event Schedule:







  • 19: 15-19: 45 - Registration of visitors. Say hello to each other :)
  • 19: 45-20: 00 - Opening of the mitap. Learn more about why the Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow / Vicky-Marie Gibbons community was created.
  • 20:00 - 21:30 - Tech Talks. The speakers will tell you very significant and useful in practice techniques, techniques and methods in the design and development of the Web.
  • 21:30 - 23:00 - Networking. Let's discuss all the reports together and ask our experts questions over a slice of pizza!
