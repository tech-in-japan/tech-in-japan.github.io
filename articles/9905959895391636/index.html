How to choose a kiwi

The benefits and harms to the body of any product depend on its quality. To use kiwi as useful as possible, you need to choose it correctly. Inspect the fetus carefully. Vitamin Salad

This healthy dish can be consumed during lactation. Have to take:





  • leaf salad;
  • small zucchini;
  • half a kiwi;
  • cottage cheese - 40 g;
  • hard cheese with a low fat content;
  • olive oil - 1 tsp;
  • lemon juice.




How to cook:





  1. Leave the lettuce leaves.
  2. Steam the zucchini. Cut it and kiwi into small cubes.
  3. Add cottage cheese.
  4. Season with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Garnish with grated cheese.

Salad "Africa"

Another salad that will help diversify the menu of a nursing mother.





You will need:





  • 250 g chicken;
  • 100 g of hard cheese;
  • 2 boiled eggs;
  • 1 apple
  • 1 kiwi
  • 100 g Korean carrots;
  • 100 ml of sour cream.




How to cook:





  1. Boiled chicken fillet, kiwi is cut into small cubes.
  2. Grind eggs, apple and cheese on a grater.
  3. Spread the components in layers on a dish: fillet, kiwi, egg, apple, cheese, Korean carrots. Each layer needs to be smeared with sour cream.
  4. They put in the refrigerator for an hour and a half.

Fruit ice

This recipe is useful for young mothers in the heat. The treat will quench your thirst and saturate your body with vitamins.





Have to take:





  • 3 kiwi;
  • 150 ml of water;
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar;
  • lemon juice to taste.

Cooking:





  1. Lemon juice, sugar are added to the water, heated until the latter is dissolved.
  2. The peeled fruit is ground on a grater or using a blender.
  3. Combine the resulting mass with syrup.
  4. Poured into forms that are designed for ice cream.
  5. Sent to the freezer for three hours.
