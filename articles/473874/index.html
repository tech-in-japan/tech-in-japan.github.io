Digest of IT events in November (part one)





Autumn is running out, developers are starting to pack up and move away from chamber meetings to major conferences with an eye to practical use. Our first November bulletin includes large-scale events from the Python, Go, JavaScript communities, hackathons with unexpected topics, DBMS administration and information security.



Huawei Developers Day



When: November 1

Where: Moscow, Novinsky Boulevard, 8, p. 2, Hotel Lotte

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Initiative from the manufacturer: introducing mobile developers to the new open platform Huawei Mobile Services. The mini-conference will be held in two stages - first, Huawei representatives will talk about new tools for creating, testing, promoting and monetizing applications and highlight the ecosystem development strategy, then developers will be able to integrate HMS into their own applications right on the site and consult with experts about any emerging difficulties.



PiterPy 2019



When: November 1

Where: St. Petersburg, st. Starting, 6

Terms of participation: 14,000 rubles.



The annual international Python development summit is characterized by practical focus and a high level of presentations. The program consists of two general tracks and one for machine learning in Python. GoLang Piter 2019



When: November 1

Where: St. Petersburg, st. Starting, 6

Terms of participation: 14,000 rubles.



A conference on the Go programming language will be held in parallel and on the same platform as PiterPy 2019. An international team of speakers and a high concentration of technical reports are also expected here. Building functions, debugging problems, data compression, distribution of certificates - this is just a small fraction of what the speakers want to cover for the audience.



START IT



When and where:

Krasnodar - November 2 (Uralskaya St., 87)

Belgorod - November 9 (Koroleva St., 2a, building 2)

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Humanitarian aid to novice programmers continues to tour Russian cities - Krasnodar and Belgorod are next in line. Those who visit the site will receive recommendations on how to find a niche, write resumes and conduct interviews, learn many interesting stories about retraining as IT specialists and expand (or start) a circle of professional contacts.

Aug meeting at Modulbank



When: November 5th

Where: Ufa, Verkhnetorgovaya Ploshchad, 6, floor 3, Modulbank

Terms of participation: free, registration required



This time the meeting will be held in a trial format for lightning talks with pre-agreed topics. On the agenda: an exchange of concise (5 minutes) stories about your experience with Jira (are there enough statuses, are there custom schemes, and how much does the workflow fit into the system-defined framework), then there will be a general discussion about a major update to Confluence and, possibly, voluminous Atlassian Solutions Report.



Sber X - RamblerFront & Meet Up



When: November 5th

Where: Moscow, Kutuzovsky Prospect, 32

Terms of participation: free, registration required



This meeting is carried out by the staff of the Rambler Group and the Sberbank Ecosystem, who have prepared several reports for everyone interested based on their own cases. The first will be devoted to designing a banking application that will stand out among others, the second to problems and methods of creating diversity in page elements, the third to ITP and difficulties with cross-domain cookies.



Open meeting with Peter Zaitsev



When and where:

Ryazan - November 5 (Pervomaisky Avenue, 54)

Nizhny Novgorod - November 9 (Rodionova St., 192/2)

Terms of participation: free, registration required



A few insights from the head of Percon, the company that produces enterprise solutions for MySQL, MongoDB and PostgreSQL. Petr Zaitsev will give an audience with two reports. The first will deal with techniques for working with popular databases; the second will focus on the use of the Percona Monitoring and Management 2.0 system to optimize and debug the functioning of the DBMS with minimal cost.



DotNext 2019



When: November 6-7

Where: Moscow, Krasnopresnenskaya nab., 12, WTC Congress Center

Terms of participation: from 21,000 rubles.



Traditional global conference for .NET developers. Everything will be here: experts from large companies (Google, Microsoft, JetBrains), living authors of books, speeches covering various aspects of development and training levels of developers, workshops and entertainment. A detailed program with explanations and tags can be found on the event website.



C ++ Evening # 2



When: November 7th

Where: St. Petersburg, nab. Bypass Channel, 136

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Another portion of useful talk about the vicissitudes of development in C ++. This time, the official part will be reserved for talking about configuration file formats and organizing CMake projects.



DUMP Kazan 2019



When: November 8th

Where: Kazan, ul.Fatykha Amirkhan, 1, hotel "Riviera"

Terms of participation: 4900 rub.



As in previous years, the large-scale and wide-profile conference in Kazan brings together all those who are familiar with the process of developing IT products - a total of more than 400 people. The reports will be divided into four sections: Frontend (machine learning in the frontend, creating a video player, anatomy of image formats, using a single repository), Backend (microservices, code review, cloud technology, subject model), DevOps (static code analysis, post-mortem procedure, PostgreSQL fault tolerance, multi-cluster balancing) and Management (performance metrics, new products in mature teams, remote team formation). A separate track will host a number of diverse workshops.



HolyJS 2019 Moscow



When: November 8-9

Where: Moscow, Krasnopresnenskaya nab., 12, WTC Congress Center,

Terms of participation: from 21 000 rubles.



The next meeting of a great number of JS-specialists from different countries to discuss accumulated news and eternal front-end issues (with backend elements). The event will be attended by representatives of the TC39 committee, with whom everyone will be able to chat directly during the group discussion. Also, participants are waiting for BOF sessions, lightning talks, for which it is not too late to apply, the premiere of a documentary about GraphQL. Well, the reports, of course.



Platform Developers Conf



When: November 9th

Where: Perm, Reshetnikovsky descent, 1, Miro office

Terms of participation: free of charge according to the selection results



Miro is organizing a conference where you can learn a lot about creating and supporting platforms for developers from techlides and engineers involved in these processes. Programmers from Stripe, Intercom Miro, Wrike will make presentations. The program is still being formed, however, participants can count on a solid list of performances, joint discussions and afterparties.



Baltic Sea Hack



When: November 9-10

Where: St. Petersburg, Petersburg sh., 64, bldg. 1, the territory of Pulkovo, pos. Shushary

Terms of participation: free, registration required



36-hour marine dive and development. Developers, designers and just generators of ideas are invited. The team can choose a topic for their liking from an extensive list: from tourism and leisure activities to solving environmental problems, from navigation systems to creating an accessible environment. Not only the general appearance and functionality of the prototype will be evaluated, but also its compliance with the declared case. For the award of winners allocated 500,000 rubles.



Photohack



When: November 9-10

Where: Veliky Novgorod, Antonovo, 1

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The goal of the hackathon is to work together to develop a solution for visualizing text in the messenger. The program should generate a contextual and attractive image based on the user's selfie and a short message entered by him; accordingly, programmers and designers have to work together. From a technical point of view, both a mobile application for iOS or Android, and a server solution are suitable. The organizers, PhotoLab, provide participants with an API with a template designer. Prize fund - 300,000 rubles.



#ITsubbotnik



When: November 9th

Where: Ryazan, Pervomaisky Avenue, 54, Congress Hotel Amaks

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Semi-working day off for everyone to talk on professional topics and listen about interesting problems. Among the problems stated: parsing complex fragments of Java code in a puzzle format, switching to SAFE, implementing a library for managing state in ReactJS, fundamental difficulties of mobile development, cloud providers and much more. Those who catch the finals of the program have a chance to take the prize with them.



PgConf.Sibir 2019



When: November 11-12

Where: Krasnoyarsk, etc. newspaper "Krasnoyarsk worker", 31, p. 7, building L

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The PostgreSQL Development and Administration Conference invites database administrators, PostgreSQL kernel and extension developers, architects, and other programmers familiar with the topic to participate in a discussion of critical topics. For example: how to make the transition to PostgreSQL, how to create a system toolkit, whether the DBMS is suitable for highly loaded systems, what are the specifics of administration and applied benefits in different areas. The program consists mainly of substantive reports in which practicing developers propose their solution to real problems.



ZeroNights 2019



When: November 12-13

Where: St. Petersburg, 3 Medikov Ave.

Terms of participation: from 10 900 rubles.



A conference on information security issues, especially valuable due to its practical bias and high requirements for reports and participants. The list of topics this year is announced as follows: cryptography problems, security of various devices, IoT hacking, research of the Windows registry for attacking purposes, trends and forecasts of dangerous hacker attacks; Alexander Matrosov and Matt Swish were identified as key participants. The reports in the program are interspersed with contests with a real search and neutralization of vulnerabilities, and for those who are interested, first of all, in applied knowledge that are useful for protecting systems, a separate track is highlighted.



Smart City Forum 2019



When: November 13th

Where: Moscow, Bolshaya Yakimanka, 24

Terms of participation: from 2000 rub.



Presentation of solutions for digitalization of the urban environment and utilities. The exhibition will feature a wide range of technologies (robotics, the Internet of things, big data, biometrics, AR / VR, 3D, artificial intelligence) as applied to the tasks of various fields - transport, communications, ecology, energy and others. In parallel, a number of plenary sessions will be held on the site, dedicated to the modernization of these sectors, and a pitch session for startups.



Big Monitoring Meetup 4



When: November 14th

Where: St. Petersburg, st. Flower, 19, data center Selectel

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The color of the domestic IT monitoring market will gather at the site to communicate with the benefits of professional growth. The total number of participants is expected to be about 150 specialists involved in the implementation of decisions; Among them are speakers from Coms, VDSina.ru, Grafana, IT-GRAD, Selectel, LUKOIL Technologies with fresh cases and tool reviews.
