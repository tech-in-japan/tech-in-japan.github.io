Design and Management



The hybrid is made in the classic form factor for horn devices. The case is stretched forward, and in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe screen is the most massive (due to the large screen) part of the device.

Button Functions:







Users of DVRs may notice the absence of two popular quick access functions - turning on / off the screen and microphone.











They were removed so as not to overload the device with keys. Instead, the settings can be set to automatically turn off the screen after 30 seconds or 1/3/5 minutes. As for the microphone control, on / off is available through the settings. However, the question of whether it is necessary to urgently disable it remains open.



The body of the device is made of matte plastic and in black. Only the overlay around the screen is a little glossy and gray. As a result, fingerprints are practically invisible on the body.

On the highway, the hybrid confidently detects at a distance of up to 1,600 meters all relevant radar detectors on Russian roads in 2019, including Avtouragan, Amata, Binar, Vizir, Iskra, Cordon, Krechet, Chris-P, LISD, Radis, Robot, Skat and others.







Of course, a sore point, especially in the city - false positives. To minimize their number, Playme uses the SAFP (Superior Anti-falsing Platform) system in the P600SG. It eliminates false signals from motion sensors, automatic doors, vehicle blind spot control devices and other devices operating in ranges similar to those of police radars. The hybrid really works very quietly. Therefore, even in the city, it is not necessary to turn off the Playme P600SG radar detector, leaving alerts only on GPS points.



The model refers to signature devices. The memory contains samples of radar signals, and by name.



The radar part can work in one of five modes:



Track. Signals of all radar ranges are detected, and the device also alerts about cameras from the GPS base.

City 1. Reception of radar signals in the bands K, Laser, signature recognition, as well as notification of cameras from the GPS base.

City 2. Reception of laser radar signals and signature recognition, as well as notification of cameras from the GPS base.

GPS DB. Alerts only on the GPS base.

Auto Automatic mode selection based on speed. Up to 40 km / h - City 2, 40-70 km / h - City 1, over 70 km / h - Route.



In the settings, you can set a certain speed (from 10 to 70 km / h), below which radar detection will not work. It‚Äôs logical - why notify if you do not violate the speed limit.
