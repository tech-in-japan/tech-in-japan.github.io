Zabbix Summit 2019: what to expect this year

From October 11 to 12 this year, the annual, ninth, Zabbix company summit will be held in Riga. As usual, the conference program will be as diverse as possible - not only reports, but also many other interesting events. Let's take a closer look.









Presentations



Of course, the most important part of the conference is its informational component, therefore we tried to make the selection of topics as diverse as possible. There will be several technical topics from the Zabbix team - the executive director and founder of Zabbix Alexey Vladyshev will tell you in detail and with practical examples about the new version of program 4.4, and technical support engineers will share knowledge and examples of successful use of Zabbix from their practice. Many interesting reports were prepared by invited speakers. You can familiarize yourself with the detailed schedule of performances here .



Entertainment



Let's be honest - this is a point that brings the community together like no other. As usual, we have prepared three entertaining events for you: before, during and after the summit. Stay tuned - soon we will publish a detailed program every evening.



Workshops



At the beginning of the second day of the summit, October 12, several practical workshops from technical support engineers and Zabbix partners will be held for visitors. This year we have planned six workshops. You can sign up for a workshop of interest on the spot a day earlier. Please do not forget that to participate in the workshop you need to have your own portable computer with a pre-installed Zabbix.



Trainings and exams



Each year, we provide the opportunity for visitors to take the Zabbix exam and get a certificate without taking a course at a reduced price. This year will not be an exception - if you are confident in your knowledge, feel free to sign up for the exam. We also took care of those participants who would like during one trip not only to attend the summit, but also to pass our training. Please read the schedule of training courses and register in advance in order to secure a place for the training.







Open Day



For the first time during the summit, we decided to invite all interested Zabbix users to visit us. On Thursday, October 10th, we will open the doors of our head office in Riga, tell and show how we work, answer many questions and treat ourselves to coffee. If you intend to visit us, please let us know in advance at conference@zabbix.com so that we can send you all the details.



Lots of cool new merch



We are extremely pleased that Zabbix users from all over the world love our merch. This year we have prepared many new products - interesting and practical.



A special program for the "second half"



We know that many guests travel to the summit, accompanied by a loved one. Let's not let your soulmate get bored! Especially for the accompanying parties, we have prepared an interesting excursion program in Riga on Friday, October 11. Let the trip be interesting and useful for everyone.



Exhibition stands



Starting this year, we have been providing our sponsors with the opportunity to represent their company at separate exhibition stands. This is a great opportunity for visitors to get acquainted with the services of our partners, and for partners - to chat with Zabbix customers, find out about their needs and offer solutions.



Registration for Zabbix Summit 2019 is open until September 30th. Less than a month is left, which means you should not delay the decision. See you!
