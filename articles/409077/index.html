Unpacking



The i3 Mini comes in a tight cardboard box.







Everything is securely packaged to avoid damage in transit.















Included are the 3D printer itself, the power cable, a USB cable for connecting to a computer, tools and accessories, a small test coil of filament, and even glue stick for the platform.

Advantages and disadvantages



Pros:



  • Easy to run
  • Massive hard body
  • Rigid Z axis, no wobling
  • E3D V6 Hot End
  • High-quality printing PLA
  • Convenient screen and control
  • Convenient and thoughtful calibration process printer table


Minuses:



  • No heated platform, this is the main disadvantage
  • Plastic Feed System - Bowden
  • Open case
  • Small print area


findings







Wanhao i3 Mini will be useful to anyone who does not intend to print parts larger than 12x13.5x10 centimeters, and who will have enough plastic that does not require a heated platform for printing.



All of its shortcomings stem from the design features: a compact form factor and the desire of the manufacturer to reduce the price due to these restrictions.



Apart from these features, the printer is very good - it is built with high quality and works with dignity.



Given its low price, we can say that this printer will be an excellent New Year's gift to a teenager - as the first 3D printer, or to an enthusiastic homemadera - as an unpretentious ‚Äúworkhorse‚Äù for printing small objects from PLA.
