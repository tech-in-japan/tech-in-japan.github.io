September IT Events Digest (Part Two)





September continues on a wave of enthusiasm after Knowledge Day. In the second half of the month we will see a whole bunch of events of different sizes dedicated to specific languages, frameworks and platforms, the balance of mobile and web development, as well as unexpectedly close attention to the problems of beginning developers and team leaders.



Microsoft IoT / Embedded



When: September 19

Where: St. Petersburg, st. Mayakovsky, 3A, hotel "Novotel"

Terms of participation: free, registration required



A series of seminars for manufacturers of smart devices from Microsoft and Intel will start in St. Petersburg. Speakers will talk about the capabilities of the Microsoft IoT / Embedded product line and the benefits that they provide in the performance and reliability of intelligent systems. The following solutions will be discussed in detail: Windows 10 IoT Enterprise, Azure IoT Central, and Azure IoT Edge. Some private technical issues will be touched upon, for example, what to do after discontinuing support for Windows 7 and how to configure using the Embedded Configuration Manager.

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Java, pizza, communication. Two speeches are planned: the first will focus on how to painlessly pay off technical debt in critical parts of the project (debug the process, eliminate errors), from the second, students will learn how to analyze the response time, with examples from life.



Mobile Forensics Day 2019



When: September 19

Where: Moscow, st. Forest, 7. BC "White Gardens"

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The traditional gathering of experts in mobile forensics is a discussion of trends, new tools and threats. The speakers will cover topics such as incident investigation, data extraction methods, encryption, passwords as a means of protecting devices, attack models, types of digital traces and others. An exhibition area, master classes will be organized on the site, and in the end - a prize draw for participants.
