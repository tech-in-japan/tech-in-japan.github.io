What you cannot demand and why you cannot praise employees if you want them to work well

Please note that this is not only about "do not praise", but also about "do not demand."



I was recently surprised to find that so far a large number of companies require a strict dress code for all employees (even those who do not get out of the office and do not see customers) and make sure that people arrive at 9:00.
