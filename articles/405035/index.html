Telegram allows for censorship; client update 4.1 protects against blocking



Pavel Durov



The head of Roskomnadzor, Alexander Zharov, promised that Telegram did not need anything, only to send information about the company for inclusion in the register of distributors of information distributors (ARI). Pavel Durov agreed to send information and agreed to be included in the register of ARI. Thus, on June 28, 2017, the telegram.org, web.telegram.org and t.me services are entered in the registry .



Nevertheless, the creator of the Telegram messenger perfectly understands that now the authorities have every reason to proceed to the next stage. According to the law, every ORI is obliged right now to comply with the law 97-FZ (Law on bloggers and organizers of information dissemination):



  • Start collecting all metadata about user actions and interactions. All attributes of correspondence, calls, interactions with Telegram services;
  • store all this metadata for 6 months;
  • provide this metadata to the Russian authorities at their request.
