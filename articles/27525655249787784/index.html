Which variety is considered the most useful?

Firstly, rice itself varies in shape (grains are long, medium, round). Secondly, it may be subjected to heat treatment. Due to different processing methods, rice is divided into:





  1. Brown. This species is not thoroughly cleaned. Flower peel is removed from it. He is most useful. It is in it that all the useful properties described above are stored.
  2. Steamed rice (it is yellowish). Purified from the original shell and scales. After cooking, it retains its structure, and also up to 80% of useful substances are stored in it.
  3. Classic white rice. It is completely cleaned. Because of this, few useful substances are stored in it. Also changed the natural taste of grain. It is undesirable to use for people with diabetes. Of the advantages: the most popular in stores and has a low price.

Which is better?





Is it possible to breastfeed rice and which is better to eat? Of these varieties, brown and steamed are more recommended. But no matter what kind of rice is chosen, it is recommended to soak it for a while in cold water before cooking, so that all harmful substances come out and dirt is removed.

Recipe for Rice Soup

You will need:





  • rice groats (steamed) - 50 g;
  • potatoes - 2 medium tubers;
  • carrots - 1 medium;
  • onion - 1 head;
  • water - 1.5 l;
  • cooking oil.

Wash vegetables, peel, chop. Rinse and boil the rice (five minutes). Run the potatoes into boiling water. When the water boils again, start rice. At the same time, fry the carrots with onions (until the onions become golden brown). Add them to the pan when the rice is almost ready (10 minutes before the dish is ready). After cooking, the soup should stand for a while.





Rice cereal porridge recipe

Would need:





  • rice (white, round-grain) - 130 g;
  • cow's milk - 180 ml;
  • water (settled or boiled) - 250 ml.

Rinse and let stand in water for 15-20 minutes. Gently drain the water. Thus, excess starch, nitrates and dirt are washed off. Also, the cooking time for porridge will be reduced. Add cereal to boiling water (sugar or salt depends on taste preferences), cook 1/3 hours. Add warmed milk and add porridge. Rice should be completely soft, but not cooked. Often, mothers worry whether it is possible for a nursing mother to eat rice while breastfeeding. If the child behaves as usual and does not bother anything, then there is nothing to worry about. And this dish can be consumed several times a week (especially if a brown variety of rice is used), but in a limited amount, so as not to cause constipation.





Rice casserole

Pre-lubricate the preheated form with oil. Mix all ingredients (except cheese). Put gently on a baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes at 180 degrees. Then sprinkle with cheese and put in the oven for about 5 minutes. Let the finished dish cool.
