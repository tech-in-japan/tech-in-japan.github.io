In iTunes, a song without sound is climbing up the tops of the top.

When you connect your iPhone to some car stereos, there is one small problem - the first song from the playlist starts to play alphabetically. Each time the same song, until the user selects a playlist. It's clear that it bothers to listen to the same song every day for weeks and months. Even if you like it very much, you still get tired of the melody.



A certain user named Samir Mezrahi (Samir Mezrahi) has become a real hero for millions of unfortunate, tortured iPhone owners who are tired of listening to the same thing. He posted a ten-minute silent song in iTunes: A aaaa Very Good Song - Single . Download this song and add to your playlist, you get 10 minutes of reverent silence when you connect your phone to the stereo system. There is a time to calmly select albums for listening.



Judging by the thousands of thanks on Twitter , such a simple focus helped a huge number of people who could not solve the problem on their own.
