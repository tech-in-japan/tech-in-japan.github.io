First recipe

There are many recipes for making jelly from flax. The beneficial properties of each drink remain unchanged, and they differ only in taste and texture. Moreover, if you add certain ingredients, you can increase the beneficial properties of the drink.

















The first flaxseed jelly is prepared on the basis of any berry. It is very tasty and healthy. It combines the beneficial properties of flaxseed and berry vitamins.









For him you will need:





  1. Ready jelly from sweet berries.
  2. Flaxseed flour.













The cooking process is very simple. In one glass of berry ready-made jelly, you need to add one tablespoon of flour. Then wait until the drink has cooled.





Linseed kissel, prepared according to this recipe, heals wounds on the mucous membrane and removes toxins and poisons due to laxative properties. Berries make the taste more pleasant and cheer up all day.





Second recipe

The following flax jelly, the recipe of which will be discussed below, is suitable for those who are allergic to potato starch or intolerant or poor digestibility. It is prepared simply, but long enough.





For him you will need:





  1. Grains of flax.
  2. Water.
  3. Flaxseed flour.

Two tablespoons of flaxseed are added to one cup of hot boiled water. The mixture must be infused in a thermos overnight. In the morning, the infusion is filtered. 1 tablespoon of flour is added to it. Kissel is thoroughly mixed. Now it is ready to eat.





Drinking such jelly is better chilled and on an empty stomach. Thus, it will bring more benefits.





Third recipe

This option is suitable for lovers of coffee and coffee drinks. It improves digestion, helps with weight loss and invigorates without overloading the nervous system.









For him you will need:





  1. Flaxseeds.
  2. Chicory.
  3. Water.













First, three tablespoons of flaxseed are crushed in a blender and poured with a liter of water. Then the mixture is brought to a boil and removed from the heat. Beat the cooled mixture in a blender for ten minutes, gradually adding one teaspoon of dried chicory. Then the kissel must be filtered. It is used in the same way as the previous one. Having drunk a mug of jelly in the morning instead of coffee, you will increase strength and performance for the whole day. Fatigue will not appear in the evening.





The fourth recipe. Kissel for weight loss

There is also flax jelly specially for weight loss. It has a very low calorie content and has a fat burning effect. At the same time, such jelly improves digestion and metabolism. After losing weight with jelly from flax, kilograms do not return.





For cooking you will need:





  1. Water.
  2. Flour from flax.

It is prepared very simply. In half a liter of water, one and a half tablespoons of flax flour is added. Constantly stirring, it is necessary to bring the composition to a boil and immediately remove from the stove. For taste, you can add any dried fruit and honey to it. Sugar cannot be added to this jelly.





Jelly is drunk at the beginning of the day, a few hours for food. It works by reducing appetite. Hunger does not torment. Because flax in the stomach swells, fills it completely and is digested for a long time.





The fifth recipe. Kissel for weight loss with kefir

You can also cook a similar jelly from kefir. It has more pronounced laxative and cleansing effects and contains calcium, which is usually so lacking when losing weight.





To prepare it you need:





  1. Kefir.
  2. Flaxseed or flaxseed flour.

In one hundred grams of kefir, one teaspoon of flour or flax seeds is added, then the mixture is infused for one hour.





Such jelly is drunk for three weeks. Moreover, each subsequent amount of flax is increased by a teaspoon. After the course, a break is taken per week.





This jelly has improved taste. You can also add low-calorie fruits, berries and dried fruits to it. Sugar will only ruin the flax jelly.
