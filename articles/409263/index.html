In 2017, 5,509 materials were created, of which 5,291 were published. In 2016, there were slightly more publications - 7,374 were created, but only 7,078 were published. For various reasons (zaminusovannye, randomly posted, with violations, etc.) there were 218 publications in drafts , and last year - 296. That is, the percentage of "marriage" on the site was about 4%, which, in fact, is also quite a lot. But in general, users have become kinder - the number of pluses has increased, and the number of minuses has decreased.



Of all the published materials, 124 publications received the mark ‚ÄúTutorial‚Äù (125 in 2016).



Despite numerous requests to add markdown markup (which we added at the beginning of 2016), only 214 publications were rolled up in 2017, that is, only 1.8% of the total number (114 in 2016). We hope that in 2018 we will correct this situation in favor of the WYSIWYG editor, for which we have big plans.



With a decrease in the number of publications in 2017, the total length of records was 59,041,562 characters (in 2016, the figure was 58,856,586). This is approximately 32,801 A4 sheets, that is, almost 66 packs (500 sheets each) or a volume of 3.41 meters thick (based on the thickness of a sheet of A4 paper 0.104 mm). You have probably noticed that there are fewer articles to write in 2017, but their volume has increased. In fact, the amount of content has not decreased - just readers received more voluminous, thoughtful materials. And this trend is well seen as Hiktimes, and on Habr√©.



If we calculate the average number of characters per publication, then in 2017 the volume of material averaged 10,711 characters, and in 2016 - 7,982 characters, that is, 6 and 4.4 sheets, respectively. It's nice that neither Twitter nor Instagram have spoiled Hiktimes' readers - they still love Longrid and read even sooo long articles (which is confirmed by the results of our survey). And they are so fond that materials with only one video are mercilessly minus. Read useful, do not argue.
