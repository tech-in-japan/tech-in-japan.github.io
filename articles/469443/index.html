Technological progress is merciless: materials used everywhere today will become obsolete tomorrow. Where to go next if everything possible has already been done? In the experimental part of the study, rhenium was placed in a diamond anvil and nitrogen was supplied. Usually, materials modified at ultrahigh pressures are not able to retain their properties after extraction from the diamond anvil, but in this case, fellow experimenters expected success.
