Is radish useful in pregnancy?

A healthy woman who is expecting a baby needs healthy, fortified food; this is no secret to anyone. Therefore, the future mother's diet should include fruits and vegetables that contain vitamins, fiber, acids and mineral salts. Dishes from fruits and vegetables help to saturate the body with all the necessary substances, and also produce a diuretic and laxative effect.













All this suggests that the key to the health of the mother and the unborn baby is proper nutrition during the period of gestation. But it should be noted that not all fruits and vegetables can beneficially affect the body. In order not to worsen your well-being and not harm the baby, it is necessary to divide the food into mandatory and undesirable. This article will focus on such a delicious, long-awaited and spring vegetable, like radish.

















Radish composition

Many women in an interesting position are concerned about the question of whether it is possible to eat radishes during pregnancy. Why not? After all, there are so many useful substances in it! The composition of radishes includes fiber, sugar, fats, salicylic acid, enzymes, as well as various vitamins, such as A, K, C, B2, B1, B6, PP. In addition, the components of radish are salts of magnesium, potassium, sodium, phosphorus, calcium, and iron. A low percentage of fluoride is also part of this vegetable. Radish also contains protein, which is half as much as squash, zucchini, eggplant and tomatoes. A slightly pungent taste of this vegetable is obtained due to mustard essential oil.









Radish Properties

Radish during pregnancy is useful to use, since it provides a moderate decongestant and choleretic effect. Appetite with the use of this product increases, in addition, the emptying and peristalsis of the stomach improves. Due to radish juice, metabolism and digestion are activated. In addition to its basic properties, this vegetable prevents the development of cancer. This product is useful not only for pregnant women, but also for patients who are overweight, who have a sick liver or problems with the gall bladder, it is also useful for gout or diabetes.





Benefits of Tops





It should be noted that not only radish during pregnancy is useful, but also its tops. The tops of this vegetable contain the same vitamins as in the root. The leaves also have a bitterness, but despite this, they have a rather pleasant taste. They can be used as a seasoning for any dish, for example, meat, potatoes, soup, fish and various side dishes. The tops of this vegetable combine with such herbs as parsley, onions, dill. Radish during pregnancy can be used both in fresh and in cooked form, but you should not forget that the fresh product preserves all vitamins and nutrients.









The benefits of radishes during pregnancy

Spring is a period in which the body simply needs vitamins. And not to buy a radish at such a time is simply stupid not only for a pregnant woman, but also for an ordinary person. But for a future mother, a fresh, juicy, young root crop is indispensable in the spring. As already mentioned, this vegetable contains calcium, without which the formation of bone and muscle tissue of the fetus is simply impossible. Folic acid is also a component of radish. Due to this vitamin, the risk of developing congenital malformations in the fetus is reduced.













Potassium, phosphorus, manganese, sodium, iron, zinc, copper - all this is part of this vegetable, namely, the body of a pregnant woman really needs these components. Due to the vitamins K and C that this product contains, bleeding during the birth can be prevented. Radish during pregnancy is also useful because it contains natural fiber in large quantities, and due to this, stool is normalized, constipation is prevented, intestinal problems that occur during pregnancy quite often disappear. All this suggests that the radish is not just needed, but even necessary for a pregnant woman.





Harm





But still in some cases, the radish can be harmful. This vegetable is undesirable to eat in raw form, despite the large amount of vitamins that will disappear when cooked. If the radish is used raw during pregnancy, it should be washed very well or scalded with boiling water, as otherwise you can catch salmonella or E. coli. It all lies in the pathogens found in raw vegetables. Because of them, the development of some infections is possible. Best of all, in order not to harm the body, use radish in a stew, this will help reduce gas formation.





Radish during pregnancy. Contraindications

In addition to useful properties, there are obvious contraindications in which the use of this root crop must be reduced to nothing. Radish is undesirable for patients who suffer from diseases of the intestines and stomach, for example, ulcers, gastritis, flatulence. To completely exclude this product from the diet is necessary for people who have had a stroke or heart attack, since radishes can contribute to the formation of thrombosis.





Healing properties

Traditional medicine allows the use of radishes for headaches and radiculitis. Also, the vegetable is used in the preparation of cosmetic masks. You can eat radishes during pregnancy to avoid constipation, which very often accompany the bearing of the baby.





We can conclude that the benefits of this root crop are very great, so it can be safely added to the daily diet of a pregnant woman, just need to follow simple rules.
