Who is recommended to eat oats





Children, as well as their parents, youth and the elderly, are all advised to eat oats. It can be infused, brewed, boiled. With the exception of individual intolerance, there are practically no contraindications.





To the beneficial properties of oats as much as possible, it should be insisted or brewed, and not cooked. Therefore, you need to know how to brew oats in order to get the most benefit.









Decoction to stabilize the metabolism





  • Wash a glass of oat grains well.
  • The washed raw material is poured with a liter of distillate.
  • We insist for eight to ten hours.
  • Slowly bring to a boil.
  • Cover, cook for about 30 minutes.
  • After removing from the stove, we wrap it in a warm blanket and insist about 12 hours.
  • We filter and add distilled water so much that the total mixture leaves one liter.

The resulting broth for a month is applied one hundred grams in 25 - 30 minutes. before eating.





With diathesis





  • Grind oats with a coffee grinder.
  • Two tbsp. tablespoons of flour obtained pour 0.5 liters of boiling water.
  • Infused for three hours.

Method of use: 150 ml four times a day.





With a cold
