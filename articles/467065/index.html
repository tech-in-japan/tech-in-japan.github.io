Archive of olympiad problems in physics for schoolchildren

For a long time at school, I formed a bank of problems in physics to prepare for the Olympics. Tasks can be searched for by topics, level, class. Then send to print, or as a link to students. And although I no longer work at school, I decided that it was a pity for the good to disappear. A site without ads and other monetization. If you are a physics teacher, or a parent, welcome to cat.













For a long time, in preparation for the lesson, I had to process a lot of files to find the necessary tasks, and then also recollect from the pictures. I was tired of it, and I decided to create a website for myself where the tasks would lie in a convenient form. No sooner said than done. I managed to do the tasks only until the 9th grade. Taken from such Olympiads as the All-Russian Olympiad for schoolchildren, the Moscow Olympiad of schoolchildren and the St. Petersburg City Olympiad. Spaced by topic, class, year. For some tasks, the average score of children during the Olympiad (difficulty) was added. There are clues (part of the solution).







For teachers, it is possible to create collections of tasks (+ icon on the left), and then clicking on the task name will add it to the collection (up to five tasks). After that, you need to save the selection at the bottom left. Further, the task can be sent to print ( example ) or as a link to the student with subsequent control.









To add new tasks there is a special form. Tasks are stored in the SQL table as rows with TEX markup. The site is displayed using Katex . Printing is on the mpdf module.







We kindly request, if you are not the target audience, do not go to the site . It is located on a cheap hosting and will not withstand the influx. If someone wants to add tasks and develop a resource - write to me.
