Payment systems



To a first approximation, we sorted out the tax base. Now about the legalization of income from abroad. Since they come in foreign currency, they are subject to articles 14 (parts 2 and 3) and 19 of the Federal Law on Currency Regulation and Currency Control and articles 7 (parts 8.1 and 9) of the Federal Law on the National Payment System. That is, foreign money intended to pay for my services should be credited to my bank account when it comes to receiving funds from foreign legal entities. Or to another organization authorized in the territory of the Russian Federation, if the funds come from a foreign individual.

Why not payoneer



Payoneer is not a bank, but a corporation that opens an account for you on which it deposits funds transferred to it by another user of the corporation's services for you. Nothing criminal, but since, unlike PayPal and Upwork, the company did not pass accreditation in our country and does not provide information about the accounts of citizens to authorized state bodies, such transfers to the accounts of individual entrepreneurs and legal entities are not legal in the Russian Federation. However, individuals do not have any legal restrictions on the use of Payoneer. It is worth noting a number of interesting facts related to this:







  • funds that are on Payoneer accounts are not visible to our fiscal authorities
  • purchases made with a Payoneer plastic card are not tracked in any way in our country


You can even pay personal income tax from some part of this money, for example, from what you turn into cash at an ATM, I don‚Äôt know why (:-)), but you have to remember about 115-FZ , according to Article 6, part 1.5, which , a Payoneer plastic card may be blocked by an ATM, as it falls within the definition.

Subtleties of revenue accounting



When working with exchanges and payment systems, according to the Tax Code of the Russian Federation , it is imperative to record in the ACS (Revenue Accounting Book) the receipts of the amounts that your counterparty transferred to you, and not those that settle in your pocket after paying the commissions. If you are on the simplified tax system, this is necessary for calculating the tax, which is taken exactly from the amount that the counterparty transfers to you. Also, do not forget to enter this amount in KUD in rubles at the exchange rate of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation on the day of receipt of funds to your account in the payment system or on the exchange. This is extremely important, and will save you from huge hemorrhoids and fines on the day of the desk check, which inevitably occurs three years after the opening of the IP. Costs, however, are of no interest to anyone. On STS 6% - because the tax is calculated on income, on STS - because KUD is important only to show that you secretly did not earn more than 60,000,000 a year.
