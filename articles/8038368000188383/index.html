Rules of use and advice from doctors

In the presence of pancreatitis, doctors are advised to eat goat dairy products in limited quantities. Most often, they recommend drinking no more than one glass per day. Dosage is strictly individual and directly depends on various factors, for example, on the form of the disease, the presence of other chronic ailments, and so on.









This drink is introduced into the diet gradually and begins with a quarter glass. At the initial stage, it is better to dilute this product with water in a ratio of one to one. It is recommended to drink goat milk in the morning on an empty stomach, and in addition, every time a person has a desire to have a bite.





Before taking goat's milk with pancreatic pancreatitis , it is necessary to boil it. Thermal processing of milk leads to the destruction of bacteria that cause fermentation processes in the digestive system. The optimal duration for boiling should be a minute. During this time, all harmful bacteria die, and vitamins with other valuable components are not destroyed. Whole milk of goats is not recommended for use in the presence of severe forms of pancreatic inflammation.





In the presence of chronic pancreatitis, doctors advise drinking 100 or 150 milliliters of the product for medicinal purposes half an hour before each meal. The course of therapy is at least two months. But this is only part of a comprehensive treatment.





Drinking goat milk with pancreatic pancreatitis in an acute form is not recommended. In this case, you will need to follow a diet, and in addition, all medical prescriptions. Sometimes gastroenterologists advise drinking this healing drink as part of various cereals, casseroles and other dietary dishes. And in order to always stay fresh, it is necessary to store them in the refrigerator, because the nutrient components that make up their composition serve as a beneficial environment for the development of pathogenic bacteria.
