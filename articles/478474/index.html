Brother Pablo Escobar also released a flexible smartphone





It is just a theater. The brother of the richest drug lord, rats in whose walls they ate $ 2.1 billion a year, will now sell smartphones in competition with Samsung and Apple. Moreover, his first device, Escobar Fold 1, released a day ago, costs $ 350 and in some ways looks better than the last failed Galaxy Fold.









The new Fold 1 is the brainchild of Roberto de Jesus Escobar, brother and former accountant of Pablo. He was a co-founder of the Medellin Cartel, responsible for over 80% of drug trafficking in the United States. Pablo was shot dead by special forces in 1993, and his legacy is still being raked by Colombia. For example, after his death, tame hippos escaped and filled the surrounding rivers, causing a local crisis.









Two Acrobat Brothers



But Roberto Escobar escaped easily. He was caught and imprisoned for ten years. He was released in 2004, and in 2014 founded Escobar Inc, a patent trolling company. For example, he demanded from Netflix $ 1 billion for the story about him and his brother in the series Narcos. And in July 2019, he accused Ilon Mask of copying his design for the Non-Flamethrower .







Finally, on December 2, the former drug lord accountant presented his first gadget of his own. Fold 1 is a bendable smartphone on Android 9 with a Snapdragon 8150 processor, 6/8 GB of RAM and two 16 and 20 MPix cameras. A rather large device includes two 7.8-inch AMOLED screens. Dimensions - 13.4 x 19 x 0.76 centimeters, weight - 320 grams.

The Escobar website says that the product is shipped to markets around the world, but is mainly designed for the United States. Comes with a free case. So far, only 100,000 units have been produced.

Roberto also says that much attention is paid to security in his smartphone:







A bending screen is good, but we also have special protection. It complicates access to data both when scanning via Bluetooth, and for the state that wants to access the device. But what Escobar does not mention is that his Fold 1 is very similar, just a rebranding of the Royole FlexPai model. Up to marketing images, design, size and all the characteristics.
