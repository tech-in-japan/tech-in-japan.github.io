Investors Putting Down Tesla Shares Lost Over $ 1.4 Billion Per Day After Rising





Financial analysts estimate that investors who put Tesla, the electric car manufacturer, in the fall, lost a total of more than $ 1.4 billion. This happened against the backdrop of the company's stock growth - Thursday October 24 was the best day for Tesla securities on the stock exchange since 2013.



Note : any operations with shares on the exchange are associated with a certain risk, this must be taken into account (this is also confirmed by the experience of Tesla investors). What happened



Tesla shares rose 16.5% at the end of the week, approaching $ 300 apiece. As early as Wednesday, Tesla shares were trading at $ 254.68 - in 2019, the price reduction was 23.4%.



Why investors play against Tesla



Tesla shares are considered one of the most popular among investors playing for a fall. The total volume of transactions with shares in the hope of a further drop in their value exceeds $ 9 billion.



This situation is facilitated by the fact that Tesla is one of the most talked about companies in the world. Any statements by Tesla representatives are immediately circulated to the media around the world, and any mismatch of reality with the announced plans gives investors reason to hope for a further drop in prices. So in 2018, the fact of whether the company will be able to produce a sufficient number of new Model 3 electric vehicles was widely discussed.



Among the supporters of Tesla's short sale of shares, there are also top managers of large funds and organizations. Elon Musk argued actively with some of them on Twitter.
