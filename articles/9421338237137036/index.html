Cleansing the liver with beet kvass

Beet kvass contributes to the effective cleansing of the liver due to its gentle action. It fully removes toxins and waste from the body. In addition, it is advisable to use it for older people, because they are harmed by complex therapeutic techniques.





This method is one of the longest, but its benefits to the whole body are invaluable.





To make kvass, the patient will need:





  • Fresh beets - three pieces.
  • Sugar - one and a half kilograms.
  • 50 grams of flour.
  • 0.5 kg of raisins.
  • One liter of water.

Finely chop the peeled beets, pour them into a three-liter jar, then add flour, 0.5 kg of sugar, close the lid, leave it for two days in a dark place. Shake the jar periodically so that all the ingredients are mixed again. After the specified time, add the remaining water, raisins and sugar, leave all this for another week. After that, kvass is filtered with gauze.





The result should be about a liter of drink. To undergo a liver cleansing course, you will need three liters. Once the kvass is over, you need to take a break for two months, and then resume the course.





Dosage: drink one tablespoon three times a day before meals.
