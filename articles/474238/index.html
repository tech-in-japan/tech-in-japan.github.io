And what is it about?



Conversations is the only event in Russia devoted entirely to colloquial artificial intelligence, where they are also looking at it from all possible angles.



Developers share personal and corporate hacks for creating bots with AI and skills for assistants and a smart home, test interactive platforms and analyze ASR \ TTS technologies, discuss the intricacies of working with customers and ways to make money in a new market.



The business tells about its experience in using conversational AI technologies in communication with customers and employees, about the effectiveness and inefficiency of projects, about working with vendors and self-development, about high expectations and real breakthroughs.
