Is it possible to drink milk to a nursing mother: benefit or harm, doctors' advice

Can I drink milk to a nursing mother? This issue has long been discussed by pediatricians, other specialists, and women themselves. Disputes continue to this day. There is no single answer. Despite the fact that milk is a healthy product, it can lead to allergic reactions in the child. Some children show lactose intolerance. Therefore, we consider all the benefits and harms of milk during breastfeeding.





Positive properties

Can I drink cow's milk to my nursing mother? Before you get the answer to this question, you need to understand what useful properties the product has:





  1. Milk replenishes the reserves of calcium in the body of a woman.
  2. Contains easily digestible protein.
  3. Normalizes the acidity of gastric juice.
  4. Prevents sleep disturbance.
  5. Strengthens the nervous system, which is especially necessary for a woman after childbirth.
  6. Milk contains a large number of amino acids.
  7. Strengthens the immune system.
  8. The product allows vitamin D and many trace elements to be absorbed.




The product has many useful properties, which is a prerequisite for its inclusion in the diet of a nursing mother. However, it is necessary to deal with its negative properties.









Possible harm

An unequivocal answer to the question of whether it is possible to drink milk to a nursing mother does not exist. Separate risks are known that can adversely affect the health of both the mother and the child. These include protein intolerance, which manifests itself in the form of an allergic reaction. It is characterized as follows:





  • Rashes.
  • Violation of the digestive tract in the form of vomiting, loose stools, constipation.
  • The appearance of cough and runny nose. In children, a condition occurs when it becomes difficult to breathe. This is the most dangerous condition contributing to the development of asthma.
  • Nervousness and insomnia.

An allergic reaction can also occur if the baby is premature, has a hereditary predisposition, or if his intestinal microflora is impaired.









A woman may increase the amount of milk produced. As a result, lactostasis develops, because the child does not eat up a completely valuable product and it stagnates in the mammary glands.





Can I drink milk while breastfeeding? A woman should understand that a child can inherit intolerance to a product from her parents. Therefore, when he is breastfeeding, the risk of allergies does not decrease. 10 days after the abolition of milk in a woman‚Äôs diet, all the unpleasant symptoms in the baby should disappear.













How to choose

Can I drink milk while breastfeeding? If an allergic reaction to the product is not found in either the infant or the woman herself, the product can be consumed in the correct amounts. Preference should be given only to fresh milk. What other product requirements are there?





  1. A woman should check the containers in which milk is sold. Buying it in the market, nursing mothers believe that it is tasty and fresh. However, if the can or bottle is dirty, then milk can only do harm. After all, it may contain harmful bacteria. In this case, milk must be boiled to get rid of pathogenic microflora.
  2. Buying a product is best in trusted locations. Many people prefer products presented in supermarkets. The choice is large, which brand should be preferred, there is no single answer.   3. It is not forbidden to purchase milk in the market, but only from sellers you can trust.




Choosing a quality product is an important task that will benefit the body of the baby and woman and will not bring harm.





How to enter the diet

Can I drink milk to a nursing mother? In order not to have a negative effect on the body, you need to follow simple rules:





  • A woman does not need to consume large quantities of milk at a time. To determine the reaction of the baby, 1/2 cup of product is enough. If during the day the child has an allergic reaction, then during lactation it is best to refuse milk. If it is absent, then the next day you can increase the amount of milk.
  • You can add a little product to tea or cocoa and watch the baby react.
  • It is allowed to cook omelets and other dishes with it.
  • Breastfeeding experts recommend drinking goat milk rather than cow milk. Features of the reception will be discussed later in the article. If this type of product is not perceived by the body, then it must be replaced.

It is necessary to introduce milk into the diet gradually so as not to harm the body of the newborn.

Goat milk

The product can become the main substitute for the occurrence of intolerance to cow protein in a child.

Goat milk includes:

  • folic acid, which promotes the growth and development of the child.

Milk provides powerful support to the immune system and prevents the development of rickets.

Dairy products

Can I drink milk to a nursing mother? In addition to it, a woman can include sour cream, yogurt, kefir in the diet. Various dishes can be prepared from them, such as cottage cheese casserole. If there is an allergy to milk during breastfeeding, it is fermented milk products that will become its worthy replacement.
