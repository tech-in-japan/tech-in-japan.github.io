Belarus was the first in the world to legalize smart contracts and abolished income tax for miners

During the last year, the main topic was discussed in the corridors of power and the Belarusian IT-community: the benefits for the High-Tech Park (HTP) can be canceled, more precisely, not extended. Critics said that programmers have the highest wages - several times higher than the average wages in the country. Why should we give fat cats the benefit of paying contributions to the Social Security Fund and provide other tax deductions? The budget and FSZN lose big money.



But supporters of extension of benefits laid the main trump card: HTP has become one of the main sources for attracting currency to the country: as a result, Belarusian IT-companies are well-known all over the world. It is necessary not only to preserve the benefits, but also to expand them to other areas of business. Moreover, it is necessary to maximally support and implement IT in all areas of the economy and society , because this is the future of humanity.



Fortunately, in a clash between the economic and the IT lobby, the president chose IT. It introduces mining and cryptocurrency tax breaks. And most importantly - HTP is exempt from income tax and VAT until January 1, 2049 .



Aleinikov and Partners, Vlasova, Michel and Partners, Baker Tilly Bel and others worked on the text of the document. Preparation of the decree took about nine months, during which representatives of all HTP residents and IT companies that are not part of the HTP were interviewed. The signing of the superliberal decree is the undoubted success of its authors and the entire expert community that participated in the promotion of this project.

Blockchain technology is discussed in Section 6 of the main published provisions. It says that legal entities have the right to create and place their own tokens in Belarus. You can do it abroad, but only through HTP residents. You can acquire, dispose of tokens and perform other operations with them through cryptocurrency exchanges, cryptocurrency exchange operators.



Separately, it is worth mentioning mining by individuals. According to the text of the document, individuals receive the right to own tokens and perform the following operations with them: mining, storage, exchange, acquisition, alienation for Belarusian rubles, foreign currency, electronic money, as well as donations and testaments of tokens.



It has been established that the activity on mining, acquisition, alienation of tokens carried out by individuals is not an entrepreneurial activity. Moreover, participants of relations connected with the use of tokens were granted tax and other benefits and preferences. Economic entities are exempt from taxation of activities related to cryptocurrencies and tokens, including mining, in particular, token exchange (cryptocurrency) transactions. Exemption applies to income tax, VAT, tax on simplified tax, income tax on individuals.



In addition to all of the above, the decree significantly expands the types of activities that make it possible to become a resident of the HTP and receive appropriate tax benefits. There is a list of 38 types of activities, including ICO, mining, cryptocurrency exchanges, securities trading on tokens, etc.



Working conditions for residents of the HTP are significantly simplified: the authorization procedure for opening accounts in non-resident banks is canceled, the procedure for making payments using electronic money is simplified, currency transactions are allowed in the notification procedure, it is allowed to use English as the primary accounting document document language, etc.
