What are female hormones

This condition, in which there was a delay in menstruation and chest pain, can occur for many reasons, and usually they are all associated with hormonal changes in the body of the representative of the fair half of humanity. As you know, the entire menstrual cycle is accompanied by the production of certain hormones that are produced in the pituitary gland. And the work of the reproductive hormonal system of a woman will depend on the presence of these hormones.









As you know, hormones such as prolactin, estrogen and progesterone are considered female. If they are in the right ratio, then the woman feels great and does not notice strong changes during the period of PMS, as well as during the menstruation itself. However, if these hormones are in imbalance, then the appearance of the girl and her health are significantly impaired.









For what physiological reasons can a delay and chest pain occur?

Such a phenomenon can occur both in the presence of physiological reasons, and pathological. Most often, this can be observed provided that the woman has become pregnant. In this case, the hormonal balance is disturbed, as the representative of the weaker sex is preparing to become a mother. However, pregnancy is not the only reason why there may be a delay and chest pain.









There are a large number of physiological reasons for the imbalance of female sex hormones, namely:





  • During acclimatization. When the climate changes, the whole body needs to be rebuilt, including the hormonal system.
  • The cycle may be broken after stopping the use of oral contraceptives or during the use of other medicinal drugs.
  • Also, hormonal disruptions can occur due to a sharp weight gain or because of too rapid weight loss.
  • Many women are frightened when there is a delay in menstruation and chest pain. This phenomenon can occur when the body is in a state of stress or has experienced serious physical exertion.

Pathological causes

However, keep in mind that there may be a delay in menstruation and chest pain if there are more serious and dangerous reasons. There are quite serious pathologies that are the cause of hormonal imbalance in the female body. Consider these reasons:









  • the presence of various oncological diseases;
  • mastopathy
  • the occurrence of infectious and inflammatory processes in the organs of the female reproductive system;
  • Also, the cause may be hiding in the presence of polycystic ovary.




Consider, if there is a delay in menstruation, chest and stomach pain, and also such symptoms are repeated every month, then this means that you need to seriously think about your health and consult a gynecologist. The sooner you do this, the faster you can count on recovery.





The onset of pregnancy

Most often, when the chest hurts, the lower abdomen and the delay, this indicates that the woman became pregnant. Typically, such symptoms indicate that fertilization of the egg has occurred. In the body of the expectant mother, a serious hormonal adjustment occurs. Significantly increases the number of hormones responsible for the construction of the placenta, inhibition of ovulation, and other processes. Since the level of hormones is subject to change, then there is a delay, pulls the stomach and hurts the chest.





Pregnancy definition

Today, determining the presence or absence of pregnancy is very simple. This can be done using a special test, which in most cases shows the correct results from the very first days after the onset of conception. Sometimes such a test may be wrong, so if you notice a delay, it is best to consult a gynecologist for a comprehensive examination.





And if the test still showed a negative result

If the stomach is pulled, chest pain and delay, then pregnancy is not always the reason for this. There are also many other factors. Let us consider each of them in more detail, and also learn how to eliminate such an unpleasant phenomenon in a particular case.









The presence of a stress factor, as well as the effect of excessive physical exertion.





If a woman is under stress, then her body produces a large number of stress hormones, but the endorphins responsible for a good mood are much less. In this case, there is a hormonal imbalance, which leads to the fact that there is a delay in menstruation, chest and stomach pain. In order to restore it, you will have to pay attention to your psychological health. If necessary, take a course of treatment with antidepressants and be sure to seek help from a specialist.





Of course, every woman should do physical exercises to maintain beauty and good health. However, excessive enthusiasm for physical exercise can adversely affect the state of health and upset the hormonal balance. In order to establish a cycle, you will have to reduce the level of physical activity, and after a couple of months you will notice positive changes.





Sudden changes in readings on the scales

Please note that sudden fluctuations in weight in any direction are very dangerous for the body. Both sharp weight loss and significant weight gain lead to significant changes in metabolic processes, and this directly affects the state of the hormonal system. In addition, a sharp change in weight is very often accompanied by stress, depression and other disorders of the nervous system, which makes the situation even worse.









If the chest hurts and the delay is observed due to the fact that the woman has experienced significant weight jumps, then in this case it is very important to normalize the diet. Only in this case can we hope for positive changes, however, the treatment process will not be as fast as we would like. If necessary, the doctor may also recommend the use of hormonal drugs.





The use of hormonal birth control pills

Quite often there is a delay, chest pain and discharge in women using hormonal methods of contraception. Especially often this occurs when hormonal pills are canceled. As you know, during their use, the natural work of the ovaries is suspended, and after they are canceled, it gradually improves. Therefore, changes in the hormonal background may occur, affecting the delay.





The presence of mastopathy

Do not forget that breast pain and a delay in menstruation can also occur due to the occurrence of mastopathy. This disease is characterized by a rapid increase in connective tissue in the mammary glands.









If there is a delay and prolonged pain in the chest area, be sure to consult a doctor. In order to alleviate their condition, in the presence of this disease, doctors recommend taking medications containing iodine, as well as diuretics. In addition, the doctor will select individually for you medications that will significantly improve your health. However, any medications can be taken only after you have passed all the necessary tests, and an accurate diagnosis will be established.





Reproductive system diseases

Inflammatory diseases of the female genital organs, as well as procedures such as cauterizing erosion and installing a spiral, can lead to delay and pain. The presence of inflammatory processes can be determined by measuring body temperature (it becomes high), as well as by pain in the lower abdomen.





Polycystic ovary is another reason for the delay in menstruation and pain in the lower abdomen. Such a disease is accompanied by a deterioration in the appearance of a woman, as well as a rapid gain in excess weight. Despite the fact that the pathology is very serious and dangerous, it can be cured. For this, it is very important to undergo the necessary examinations and choose the right medicines. The recovery process is quite lengthy. However, with every month the condition of the woman begins to improve. She becomes attractive again, and her cycle is restored.





The presence of cancer

If a woman has tumors in the genital area or thyroid gland very often there is a delay in menstruation and other hormonal disruptions. At the same time, the nature of menstrual flow and their volume can also change. Please note that the sooner you seek medical help, the greater the likelihood that you can continue to live a full normal life.





How to overcome pain

If you still have a delay, and your chest stopped hurting, then most likely this indicates the onset of pregnancy. However, do not forget that most often painful sensations signal the presence of rather dangerous pathologies.





Of course, if there is pain in the chest, be sure to consult a gynecologist. To relieve pain at home, you can take painkillers and medicines that can improve blood circulation (No-Shpa, Ketorol, Analgin). However, such procedures are contraindicated in pregnant women.





If pain occurs occasionally, then there are no serious reasons for concern. However, if nevertheless pain in the chest area and delayed menstruation occur regularly, immediately go to the hospital. After all, eliminating pain does not mean curing the disease.





findings

Delayed menstruation and pain in the lower abdomen and chest are clear signs of pregnancy. But this is far from always the case. Such symptoms can occur for a number of other reasons. In order to get rid of such an unpleasant condition, it is not enough just to take painkillers, it is very important to eliminate the cause of the phenomenon.









If the delay arose due to the presence of physiological reasons, then in this case it is very important to establish a diet, correctly alternate the mode of rest and work and understand your psychological state.





But if the causes are pathological in nature, then you can not do without medical assistance. In this case, you need to establish a diagnosis as soon as possible and find the optimal solution.





Do not forget that health should always come first for you. Keep track of your cycle, record any changes, and if necessary, do not delay the trip to the gynecologist.
