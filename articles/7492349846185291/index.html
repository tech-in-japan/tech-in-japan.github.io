Step-by-step instruction

Is it possible to lie on my stomach after cesarean? The opinions of doctors are unanimous, this can and should be done. But everything should be done correctly and gradually.





Here's what doctors advise women to do:





  1. For the first time, it is allowed to roll over within a few hours after the operation. This can only be done if everything went well and there are no complications. You can lie on your stomach only with the permission of a doctor.
  2. At first, the woman will feel severe pain, so she can not withstand more than 10-15 minutes in this position. But she should not tolerate, this can negatively affect lactation. A woman needs to roll over several times during the day, and she will soon notice that the pain gradually goes away.
  3. Do not sleep on your stomach all night, especially if the pose is unusual. So she can create problems with falling asleep. And for a nursing mother, sleep is very important.
  4. If a woman cannot sleep on her stomach neither day nor night, then these efforts must be abandoned. You can simply periodically briefly occupy this position when such an opportunity arises.

Recovery in the postoperative period for each woman is an individual process and dependent on many factors. Therefore, you need to focus on your own health and the advice of a doctor, and not on the recommendations of other women in labor.
