Can I eat eggs every day? What is the harm of eating eggs daily?

Each person who monitors his diet has repeatedly thought about whether it is possible to eat eggs every day. Some people prefer to have breakfast exclusively with this product. They have up to 20 eggs a week. Others consider each such meal to be very harmful and able to undermine an already fragile health. This article will tell you if you can eat eggs every day. The effect of protein on the body

Can I eat eggs every day? Of course, yes. If you are a professional athlete or just visit the gym, then you need to eat more than one egg a day. This statement has a fairly simple explanation.





The egg contains a large amount of protein. It is necessary for building muscle. That is why eating boiled eggs every day is not only possible, but also necessary.

Is it harmful to eat eggs every day for the fairer sex? Many people think that such a diet leads to obesity. This is a big mistake. Egg yolk copes with aging skin and maintains its elasticity. All these components not only have a beneficial effect on bones, skin, hair and nails, but also partly regulate the reproductive system. Scientists conducted an experiment and found that women who consumed several eggs a day for several years are less likely to suffer from infertility, benign and malignant tumors of the uterus, appendages and mammary glands. Eggs for children after a year

Can I have eggs every day for children? Pediatricians and nutritionists answer this question in the affirmative. A growing body simply needs vitamins and protein that are contained in the product.





As you already know, an egg contains vitamin D. This element is necessary for children for the normal absorption of calcium. A lack of substance can lead to stunted growth and the development of rickets. That is why children not only can, but also need to eat eggs every day.

Why can't you eat eggs every day?

Despite the great benefits of this product, some people should not abuse it. What is the harm of eggs, and why can not they be eaten daily?

















Allergic reaction





If a person has a tendency to allergies, then doctors strongly do not recommend eating eggs every day. If you really want to get such a dish, then you should give preference to a quail product. Unlike chicken eggs, it does not cause allergies.





Infant nutrition





Babies in their first year of life are not recommended to give eggs every day. Even if your child already eats almost all adult food, the daily portion of eggs for him should be no more than a quarter of the yolk. Protein is allowed for use only after 12 months.

















Bad cholesterol





As you already know, eggs contain cholesterol that is good for the body. However, if the product is used improperly, it can become bad and harm your blood vessels and heart.





If you like to eat eggs fried in butter and add bacon or sausage to the dish, then do not expect anything good from such a meal. Such food should not be consumed every day. This can lead to a deterioration in well-being, the appearance of plaques in the vessels and the development of heart disease.

















The effect of heredity





There are people whose liver works in such a way that it processes all the good cholesterol received into bad one. Remember if you had relatives who had a heart attack or stroke at a young age. If so, then you should not eat eggs daily. Limit the amount of this product to 3-4 pieces per week.





With a hereditary disease of the veins of the lower extremities, it is also worth limiting the number of eggs consumed.
