Cooking broth

The process of making oat broth is not complicated. You should take unrefined grains in an amount of 200 grams, pour them with boiling water in a volume of 1 liter. Use of milk is allowed. After this, the mixture is cooked for about two minutes over low heat, after which the container with the broth is set aside from the fire and left to infuse for half an hour. Then the broth should be filtered, and the grains should be squeezed.

It is necessary to take such a drug for at least a month, preferably two. After this, it is important to take a monthly break and continue taking it. Drinking oatmeal broth is recommended three times a day, half an hour before a meal.

Contraindications and harm

Oatmeal broth has not only powerful healing properties, but also certain contraindications, which are important to consider.





Not recommended for use in such cases:





  1. Renal function impaired.
  2. Heart failure.
  3. Serious violations of the liver.
  4. Increased acidity in the stomach.
  5. Lack of a gall bladder.
  6. The presence of stones in the gall bladder.
  7. Individual intolerance to the product.

If a person has these problems, it is recommended to refrain from using oat broth until a visit to a specialist. Otherwise, fluid intake can cause disastrous consequences.





It should also be borne in mind that oats contain phytin, which is a growth inhibitor that inhibits the germination of grain. Phytic acid and phytates (salts of phytic acid) can harm the human body, which can be as follows:





  1. Phytin interferes with the absorption of phosphorus, which is of no small importance for the human skeletal system.
  2. The work of enzymes is slowed down, as a result of which digestion of food occurs slowly or ceases altogether.
  3. From the body, useful minerals begin to be excreted - zinc, iron, calcium, and other important trace elements.

Therefore, it is important to adhere to a certain rule during the preparation of oat broth: oat grain can be used only after preliminary soaking, subsequent washing, pouring with clean water.

Thermos application

The easiest and most convenient way to cook oatmeal broth in a thermos. The drink contributes to the normalization of metabolism, weight loss.





So, how to make oats in a thermos?





To prepare the product, you should put in a thermos 10 grams of pre-washed grain, pour it with boiled water in an amount of 1 liter. Insist oat broth in a thermos need 10 hours, after which it is filtered and consumed before eating 1 glass.





To prepare a drink in a thermos, you can use ground oats. In this case, you need a tablespoon of grains and a liter of boiling water. The duration of use of the drug is 2 months.





Next, find out how to drink brewed oats in a thermos.





Body cleansing

The rules for taking oat broth brewed in a thermos to cleanse the body are individual for each patient and should be discussed with a doctor.





To prepare it, you will need honey, lemon juice, oats, water.





The cooking procedure is as follows:





  1. Rinse a glass of grains in cold water.
  2. Boil a liter of water.
  3. Add oats to boiling water, cook for 1 hour.
  4. Pour the resulting broth into a thermos, insist for 3 hours.
  5. Strain the drink, add the juice of half a lemon, a teaspoon of honey.

Take the resulting broth in a glass, half an hour before eating. The duration of therapy is 1 month.









Cold Oat Drink

To properly brew oats in a thermos for the treatment of colds, you will need honey (2 teaspoons), a glass of oats, a liter of water.





Cooking is carried out according to the following recipe:





  1. Rinse oat grains.
  2. Pour them with boiled water.
  3. Boil the mixture for half an hour in a water bath.
  4. Filter the broth.
  5. Pour the broth into a thermos, add honey to it.

Drink the resulting drink should be four times a day in a glass. The tool will alleviate the symptoms of colds, improve expectoration, reduce temperature.

Oat broth for gastritis

If you need to prepare a decoction to eliminate hepatitis or gastritis, you need half a liter of water, half a glass of oats.

Oat broth for the liver

To prepare an oat broth that improves liver activity, you will need three liters of water, one and a half glasses of oats.





Prepare the product according to the following recipe:





  1. Rinse oat grains.
  2. Transfer them to an enameled container, add water, boil.
  3. Preheat the oven, place a boiling broth in it.
  4. Stewing the broth must be continued for 2 hours, sometimes stirring, but not bringing to a boiling state.
  5. Pour the resulting drink into a thermos, insist for 12 hours, strain.

Take the product should be warm, half a glass in the morning and half a glass in the evening 30 minutes before eating.
