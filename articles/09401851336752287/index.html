What foods contain proteins - proper sports nutrition





Proteins, as mentioned earlier, are a source of protein. This means that they should dominate in food products. So:





1) The first place among protein products is salmon. This fish is an excellent source of proteins and fats, which favorably affect the cardiovascular system.





2) The second place is occupied by rabbit meat. This meat is lean, but optimally supplies the body with proteins, iron and fat. In addition, nicotinic acid is present in rabbit meat, which is so necessary for a healthy person!





3) Third place - beef. In such meat there is a huge amount of protein, zinc and vitamins, which together help not only build muscle, but also strengthen the immune system!









4) Chicken and quail eggs. These foods contain a lot of proteins and essential amino acids that are essential for muscle growth. Two to three eggs a day in raw (cocktails for weight gain) or boiled form will be an excellent source of protein.





5) Cereals and grains are a good source of proteins and proteins. For example, on the shelves you will need to look for couscous, wheat germ and oatmeal (bran). Such whole grains will be tasty and, most importantly, healthy! They can be added to pastries, flour for baking, cook cereals, etc.





6) Bean products. Beans, soybeans, lentils are traditionally located in the kitchen of every housewife. They are not only nutritious, but also valuable. For those who are gaining weight or building muscle, these products will be indispensable, because in their composition about 25% of the daily intake of vitamins, 17 grams of carbohydrates and 27-30 grams of protein!





7) Vegetables and fruits. Of course, those who welcome the proper sports nutrition pay attention to the natural sources of nutrients! After all, such products are an important source of vital vitamins, antioxidants, phytochemicals! The diet must include broccoli, red pepper, onions, strawberries, asparagus, carrots, pumpkin, tomatoes, cucumbers, kale, lettuce, nuts ... All these products have protein for muscle growth, in addition, vegetables and fruits are very healthy.

Muscle Gain Protein Shake Recipe





It is necessary to add a little cottage cheese (100 g), raw quail eggs (4-5 pieces), banana (1 piece) and honey (1-2 tbsp.) To milk (250 ml). Beat all this in a blender and drink 20-30 minutes before training (or after it). It contains: protein - 50 g, fat - 14 g, calorie content - 530 kcal.
