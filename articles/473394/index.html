Sales and profit growth



The biggest lie in advertising CRM systems is the various statements that the CRM system will do something for you, increase something for you, or that you can increase something thanks to CRM.



No vendor can guarantee you that after implementing their CRM system your sales will grow by 10%, 2 times, by an order of magnitude, etc. Of course, sales automation most often leads to process optimization and, as a result, to sales growth, but for each company these indicators are strictly individual and depend on many factors.
