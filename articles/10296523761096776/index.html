Acesulfame potassium - harm to the body

It was created in Germany in the late 60s. When it was created, everyone was happy, believing that it was possible to refuse harmful sugar. People with diabetes were especially hoping. But in fact, this sweetener turned out to be very harmful. Oddly enough, when people









began to abandon sugar in favor of its substitutes, the number of overweight people increased significantly.





Studies have determined that this substance provokes the development of tumors and adversely affects the cardiovascular system. Although it has a positive property - it does not cause allergies, but, like most food additives, this sweetener is one of the most harmful.





Acesulfame potassium is also the most common nutritional supplement. It is added to carbonated drinks, juices, confectionery, dairy products, chewing gum, and even to medicines and toothpaste.
