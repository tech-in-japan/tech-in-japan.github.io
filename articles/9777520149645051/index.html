How to choose the right kefir

Getting to the system of weight loss, it is necessary with all responsibility to approach the choice of the main component. It is kefir. In order for a sour-milk drink to bring only benefit to the body during the weight loss period, the following should be considered:

  3. A product made three days ago will also not be useful, because it will lose its special qualities.

  5. If kefir, bought in supermarkets, does not cause trust, then you can make it yourself. A special sourdough is sold in pharmacies, which greatly simplifies the cooking process.
