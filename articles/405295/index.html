Jawbone is still being eliminated, but in parallel with this, a new project will be launched at the intersection of the Medgadget niche and the first-aid area for patients


The company became known for its portable acoustics Jambox, Big Jambox and Mini Jambox (a pair of materials on the topic: 1 and 2 ), although it also produced a Jawbone Icon headset.



At the peak of the popularity of the Jambox line, management decided to start working more closely in the IoT niche and wearable gadgets (Jawbone UP series products), but the company was not able to achieve tangible financial results.



Total investment : just under $ 1 billion (951 million according to the PitchBook data ) attracted since the first round in 2000.
