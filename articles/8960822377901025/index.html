Ketone diet for weight loss: menu, description, effectiveness and reviews

Young people who lead an active lifestyle, in recent years, are increasingly choosing a low - carb diet. One of its varieties is the ketone diet. Consider what it is, what contraindications, advantages, disadvantages it has, and decide whether to adhere to such a diet, or whether it is better to find another option for weight loss.

















What is a ketone diet for weight loss?

Its essence lies in the absence of carbohydrates in the diet. Thanks to this diet, you can get rid of subcutaneous fat, but keep your muscles in good shape. The main goal here is the restructuring of your body to lipolysis (breakdown of fats) from glycolysis (breakdown of carbohydrates). In normal mode, lipolysis starts only after glycogen depletion. It takes a few days. Then the fats begin to break down into fatty acids and glycerin, which then go into ketone bodies. Ketosis is the whole process during which ketone bodies are created.





Diet duration

Unlike other protein, the ketone diet is longer and is systemic. In the early days, the body adapts to a new diet. Energy is then taken from the remaining reserves. The preparation of the body occurs in four stages.









  • First, glucose is consumed. Half a day after eating her body wastes.
  • Over the next few days, glycogen stores are consumed.
  • After all the carbohydrate reserves are exhausted, the body tries to obtain the necessary glucose due to fatty acids and protein.













  • From about the seventh day, adaptation begins and ketosis begins. Then the breakdown of the protein becomes slow, and its main source is fat.

Thus, the ketone diet lasts 2-3 weeks, depending on the goals that are set. If you need to reduce the volume only slightly, it is better to find a different diet.





If this diet is not suitable, then you should correctly and slowly return to normal food. Carbohydrates are added to the food a little.





Signs

You can understand that the body has entered ketosis by the following signs.





  1. The appetite of a person decreases.
  2. She feels better.
  3. It smells of acetone.

The last symptom is most characteristic for recognizing the onset of ketosis. The smell is caused by derivatives of fat that are excreted by the body.





It is not always possible to completely remove it. But if you use a large amount of water (from three liters per day or more), then ketone bodies will be excreted with urine and then. The diet that we are discussing, in any case, involves drinking a lot of fluids.









Indications













For the human body to work correctly, it must receive:





  • proteins, which are the building material for the muscle system, joints and the rest of the body;
  • fats, which act as a barrier to internal organs and reserve "just in case";
  • carbohydrates as a source of energy from which vitality is obtained.

All these elements are important for normal life. Therefore, a person leading a healthy and athletic lifestyle, as a rule, does not have problems with being overweight. They can appear in those who move a little and eat improperly. If too many carbohydrates and fats accumulate, they go into the form of subcutaneous fat. Such people are shown a ketone diet.





If you do not like to count calories and limit yourself in food, then such a diet will be to your taste. In addition, it will be simply necessary for athletes during the so-called drying period.





Contraindications

Before you go on a diet, you should definitely consult with a specialist and undergo an appropriate medical examination. Ketone diet for weight loss will give the desired results only if the person is healthy.





It is strictly forbidden to pregnant women, as well as people suffering from thyroid diseases, diabetes mellitus, as well as those who have sick kidneys, liver and gastrointestinal tract.

Main rules

In order for the diet to bring the desired results, nutrition should be based on the following rules.





  1. Exclusion from the diet of fats is unacceptable. With a significant limitation of carbohydrates, another source of energy must be present, which is fats. It is advisable to give preference to its vegetable species.
  2. For many, the permitted consumption of alcohol will be a plus. But it should contain a minimum amount of sugar. One type of such alcohol is, for example, rum.
  3. The general rule for all diets, which applies to this, is to consume water. Every day you need to drink plenty of fluids so that the body is not dehydrated.

First, the weight will go at the expense of water, not fat. But do not despair and stop halfway. Then you will achieve the desired result.

What can and cannot be eaten?

Ketone diet: reviews

The opinions of those who used this diet are different. Some leave rave reviews that in a few months they managed to easily fit into old jeans.





Others claim that it is impossible to do completely without carbohydrates. In addition, there is a risk of damage to such internal organs as the liver, kidneys, and possibly others. In addition, intoxication of the body due to ketones may occur.





Still others confirm that the result has been achieved, but are no longer going to go on a diet, as they have received related problems.





But one way or another, everyone, without exception, assures that compliance with the diet requires some persistence to achieve the cherished goal. And athletes add that, observing such a diet, they still use special sports supplements. Therefore, for them, the regime passes easily and without complications.

















Thus, it can be concluded that the ketone diet, although effective for weight loss, is not suitable for everyone. Therefore, the question of her choice should be approached carefully and deliberately.
