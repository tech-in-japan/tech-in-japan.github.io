Source of iron

Iron is a substance necessary for normal human life, which the body receives from food. In some internal organs, it is stored in reserve in case of extreme shortage, however, during the period of expectation of the baby, one should not rely on "spare" sources. A decrease in the amount of iron supplied to the body leads to a decrease in hemoglobin, which, in turn, is the root cause of the development of anemia. In advanced cases, iron deficiency anemia can lead to a large loss of blood during delivery, as well as to extreme weakness of both a pregnant woman and a woman in childbirth.
