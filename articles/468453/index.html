MBLT19 :: reports, product battle and test

Hello, Habr! This Thursday, September 26, we are gathering a mobile party in Moscow to discuss trends in the development, management and promotion of mobile applications. Under the cut is the lineup of the 7th International Mobile Conference for Business MBLT19 , the battle of products and a test that tells you what kind of application you are.









Program



Learn how to launch, develop and promote mobile products from speakers from the USA, Europe and Russia. We will have the following performances:



  • Manuel Lima, Design Lead, Google
  • Nemanja Djuric, Mobile Product Manager, The Coca-Cola Company
  • Tamy Ribeiro, Chief Mobility Evangelist, Wunder Mobility
  • Lorenzo Rossi, Head of Marketing Free2Move and Co-founder, Marketing Italia
  • Dr. Jaime Gonzalo, Vice President, Mobile Services Europe Group, Huawei
  • Alexander Tolokolnikov, Head of the Department of Digital Interfaces, Tele2, and others.


After each report, you will be able to chat with the speaker in the discussion area, ask questions and discuss your project. The full program is available here .



Battle Product Managers







Application product managers of MTS, Megafon and Tele2 operators will arrange a battle on the MBLT19 stage on September 26.



Battle participants:



  • Ksenia Negoda, product manager, MegaFon
  • Alexander Smerdov, Senior Interface Manager, Tele2
  • Gordey Borodin, Product Manager, MTS

MBLT Talks :: fintech



Experts from ROSBANK, Tinkoff, MKB and Revolut will discuss in a discussion format the trends of mobile fintech, the difficulties their products face and share their experience in managing large projects.



Networking and After-party



Communicate with colleagues, share experiences and exchange ideas during breaks - the lounge zone, coffee and lunch are at your disposal. You will also find cool activities from the conference partners, and in the evening - After-party with quizas, gifts, wine and a DJ set.
