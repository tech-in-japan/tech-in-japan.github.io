New Intraoral Dental 3D Scanner SHINING 3D Intraoral Scanner





The company Shining 3D released intraoral, that is, an intraoral 3D scanner for dentists. With this scanner, you can take a digital cast of the jaws and individual teeth, both for subsequent modeling of the cap and prostheses, and for detailed consideration before treatment, directly from the patient's jaw.



The dentist no longer needs to take a physical impression to scan with his desktop 3D scanner, which saves him time and reduces costs, and the patient no longer needs to squeeze a piece of plastic material with his teeth.

Specifications



  • Interface: USB 2.0, 3.0
  • Camera resolution: 1.3 MP
  • Scan speed: 10 fps
  • Data Output Format: .STL
  • Pre-order available.


Model features



  • Antifogging System
  • Multifunctional indicator and button
  • Ergonomic design
  • Multiple scan modes
  • Aseptic plastic housing, easy to disinfect
  • Realtime Scan Control
  • Software supports touchscreen
  • Fast rescan
  • Can be used as an intraoral camera.
