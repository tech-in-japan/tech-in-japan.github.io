Yandex Science Prize named after Ilya Segalovich


When: October 15 - January 13

Where: online



Senior students, graduate students, as well as academic leaders who are engaged in developments in the field of computer vision, MO, speech recognition and data analysis can apply for the award. The prize for young researchers will be 350 thousand rubles. They will also go to an international conference on AI systems and undergo an internship at Yandex.



Scientific advisers will receive more - 700 thousand rubles.



The winners are selected by a special commission, which includes representatives of Yandex and professors from leading world universities. They will evaluate the quality of available publications, speeches at conferences and the overall contribution of nominees to the development of the scientific community.



The names of the laureates will be announced in the spring of next year, and the application can be submitted before January 13.

It will be held in the format of a kind of accelerator : participants are waiting for a meeting with mentors at the Faculty of Technology Management and Innovation. Together with specialists from Gazprom Neft and other industrial leaders, the teams will model various ways of the company‚Äôs development: from launch to attracting investments.
