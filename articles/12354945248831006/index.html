What antibiotics to drink after an abortion? Tips & Testimonials

Today we will not discuss issues of morality. At least once in a lifetime, every woman has to make such a difficult choice. However, this is not about that now, but about how to recover from the procedure with minimal losses for the body. Such an operation is a big stress for the body. After all, abortion is a very complex and painful process. Moreover, even the best doctor cannot give a 100% guarantee that the operation will take place without consequences. Therefore, in most cases, antibiotics are prescribed after an abortion.

















Safest way

If we are talking about abortion, the word "safety" will not be the most correct. But if you choose the smallest of evils (of course, after timely contraception), then such will still be medical abortion. It is performed at the earliest dates when some women are still not completely sure of their position. Maximum termination of pregnancy with drugs up to 4-5 weeks of delay. If time is lost, then doctors are sent to a vacuum abortion, during which the fetus is sucked out of the uterus using a special device.





However, there are two sides to the coin. In the short term, it is possible that the fetus cannot be removed, and after a few weeks it will be necessary to perform mechanical cleaning. And if the doctors made a little mistake, and the term is already more than seven weeks, then the vacuum installation will only damage the fetus without removing it entirely.





















Rehabilitation treatment

It is imperative to help the body resume its natural work as quickly as possible. Termination of pregnancy is a gross intervention in the hormonal background, which has an extremely negative effect on the physical and mental state. Antibiotics after an abortion should help the body recover and prevent the possibility of possible inflammation.





Of no small importance is the psychological state of the woman. Therefore, if the decision to have an abortion was difficult for you, you need to seek the help of a professional psychologist. Be that as it may, there are circumstances in which it is more humane to interrupt life at the very beginning than then leave the baby in the orphanage.





Compulsory treatment

Any experienced doctor must prescribe antibiotics after an abortion. Infections that cause inflammation can enter the body during termination of pregnancy, as well as after surgery. In addition, in the body, women can doze off infections, which are gaining strength against the background of stress. Therefore, antibiotics after an abortion can save your life or the female reproductive system from further complications that can lead to infertility.









As doctors often emphasize, it is not abortion itself that is terrible, but its consequences. It is in order to try to prevent them that preventive treatment is prescribed from the first day after surgery. The appointment is not always done in a hospital. Then, as soon as possible, you need to visit the local gynecologist and clarify which antibiotics after the abortion are optimal.

Inflammation prevention

The most important question is what antibiotics to drink after an abortion. During this operation, there is an invasion of the uterine cavity, a violation of the integrity of its cover. This opens the way for microbes, and therefore, is not far from inflammation. To avoid endometritis and other disorders, it is recommended to include antifungal and sulfonamide drugs in the course of therapy . Oral contraceptives can normalize the hormonal background. But in this case, you can not really puzzle over and take those that the gynecologist previously prescribed for you.





Under the supervision of a doctor

Speaking about which antibiotics to take after an abortion, it should be noted that the duration and intensity of therapy should be determined by a specialist. In case of any deterioration, urgently seek correction of the treatment regimen. Usually, the duration of admission is no more than 7 days. At the same time, doctors most often prefer the drugs "Gentamicin" and "Netromycin". They are able to prevent and stop the already beginning inflammation, which means, in some cases, save a life. For some purposes, the appointment of "Amoxicillin" is possible, although it began to be used relatively recently. But the dosage is individual, the doctor must weigh the pros and cons and make a decision.

















Exemplary treatment regimens

Take it twice a day, 1 capsule once a day. It is not recommended to take it for more than 7 days, most often five is enough.





In combination with Doxycycline, antimicrobials should often be taken. It can be Metronidazole or Trichopolum. The spectrum of action is very wide, the drugs help to avoid complications during any surgical procedures. The standard dosage is 2 tablets three times a day. After completion of the course, on day 5-7, a single dose of "Fluconazole" or "Flucostat" is recommended. These drugs will help maintain normal vaginal microflora.





You should take one capsule once, and after that do restoration of intestinal microflora. For this, Beef-form capsules are best suited, 2 capsules per day. Now the course is almost over. Do antibiotics harm the body

This question is heard by doctors several times a day. Of course, these are far from vitamins, and they affect our body quite strongly. However, if we are talking about the prevention of inflammatory processes, complications, repeated operations, in the most severe cases leading to final infertility, then taking antibiotics is the least of evils. Therefore, your task is to choose a competent doctor and entrust him with control of rehabilitation therapy. He will decide whether to drink antibiotics after an abortion, which are ideal in your particular case. He will also have to calculate the correct dosage, duration and intensity of treatment. Most of the modern drugs are well tolerated and relatively safe for the body.

















Instead of a conclusion

The need to make such a fateful decision may be faced by every woman. Financial circumstances, age, career, the presence of young children - all this can become good enough circumstances to terminate a pregnancy. But the most important thing in this case is to try to protect your body as much as possible, to conduct high-quality rehabilitation therapy. This is especially true for young women of reproductive age.





Judging by the reviews of women, almost everyone who followed the advice of a doctor and underwent a full course of treatment after an abortion did not feel any consequences of the operation and subsequently could have healthy children. But despite this, doctors strongly recommend using modern methods of contraception to avoid such radical methods.
