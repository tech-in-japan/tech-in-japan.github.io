October IT Events Digest (Part Two)





The second half of October passes under the signs of PHP, Java, C ++ and Vue. Tired of the routine, developers arrange intellectual entertainments, the state organizes hackathons, beginners and leads get a space where you can talk about your specific problems - in general, life is in full swing.



IT Sreda # 6



When: October 16

Where: Moscow, 1st Volokolamsky Avenue, 10, p. 3

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Friendly meeting of JavaScript and QA developers led by Luxoft. Four speakers will present the cases of their teams to the audience: compiling various testing frameworks into a single tool, implementing the sniffer in general, and experience with Charles Proxy in particular, designing a design system for cross-platform development and, finally, organizing code in a large team using the Enterprise React Application example . The audience will be able to develop these topics and offer their own during the time allotted for free communication.



MSK VUE.JS # 4



When: October 17th

Where: Moscow, Andropov Avenue, 18, building 2

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The next meeting of the Vue lovers club in the office of Raiffazenbank. This time, people will come onto the scene who have something to say about the new Composition API and its upcoming impact on the quality, independence and testability of components; audio recording in browsers and the financial prospects of Vue.



Software Development Evening # 1



When: October 17th

Where: St. Petersburg, 136 Obvodny Canal Embankment

Terms of participation: free, registration required



A new series of mitaps with a wide IT theme. The first event of the season is devoted to development problems as a whole and promises to reveal the following topics: the difference between Olympiad programming from industrial programming, the .NET monitoring system in practice, unit testing and the solution of contentious situations in the process of developing and testing software.



Burning Lead Meetup # 7



When: October 17th

Where: St. Petersburg, Seaside Avenue, 70

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Timlids and leading employees of development teams will analyze the next batch of problems that arise when organizing people and processes. This time the questions were especially sore. The first report (with a smooth transition to a general discussion) will discuss the topic of women in programming - how much their involvement in the field is real, necessary and profitable. In the second, the speaker will talk about how difficult it was to solve the problem of estimating time spent on tasks and approving deadlines with other teams, and he will offer the public the final solution.

START IT 2019



When: October 19

Where: Nizhny Novgorod, st. Sovetskaya, 12

Terms of participation: free, registration required



A helping hand to those who are just on the verge of an IT sphere and do not have a specific action plan. The program maintains a healthy balance of employment advice, success stories, a review of promising areas, and self-study recommendations. In total, more than two hundred speakers are expected with rich experience who can help with advice and beyond the stated topic - live communication between the participants is guaranteed.



Hackathon Data Audit



When: October 19-20

Where: Moscow, Kutuzovsky Prospekt 32, Sberbank Agile Home

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The Accounts Chamber of the Russian Federation, Sberbank of Russia and ANO Infokultura organize a data hackathon for analysts, developers, designers and data journalists. Participants are asked to create a prototype of digital solutions for complex cases, which the Accounts Chamber inspectors encounter in various areas - public finance, investment, healthcare, utilities. Specific examples of tasks and a list of sources with open financial data can be found on the event website. Awards - cash prizes and career offers - will be awarded in three categories: software products, media products and visual products.



Meeting for Data Driven Analysts



When: October 19

Where: st. Leo Tolstoy, 16

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The annual tradition will again bring together advanced data specialists in the Yandex office. Analysts and developers of the company will consider in their reports a number of issues related to information processing: unti-economics, analysis of anomalies, a / b testing, data collection through crowdsourcing. The second part of the event will be held in a workshop format: the audience will be able to actively participate in the analysis of unusual cases.



Panda Meetup # 28 Backend (php)



When: October 19

Where: Ulyanovsk, st. Krasnoarmeyskaya, 13V

Terms of participation: free, registration required



Members of the Panda group are preparing a new batch of technical reports on PHP development. The speakers will be practicing developers who want to share their experience in resolving issues such as working with asynchrony, logging and tracing in microservices, and Bitrix rehabilitation.



Quiz IT



When: October 20

Where: Novosibirsk, st. Tereshkova, 12A

Terms of participation: 2000 rub. (from the team)



Inspired by the success of the last game, Novosibirsk developers intend to repeat the experience. A two-hour intellectual competition in the format of a quiz will give participants the opportunity to test their knowledge in various topics related to IT, to enhance general erudition and to practice teamwork in time pressure. Prizes to the winners and a photo shoot for all are attached.



Jocker 2019



When: October 25th

Where: St. Petersburg, Petersburg highway, 64/1, congress and exhibition center "Expoforum"

Terms of participation: 45 000 rub.



Well-known highly specialized conference for Java developers: two days of pure Java in reports from highly qualified specialists from different countries. This year's main topics include performance, concurrency, testing, distributed systems and high loads in the Java world and the future of the platform. In addition to the speakers' speeches, there will be two training sessions on working with Spring Boot and Spring Cloud and profiling, respectively.



BODY



When: October 26

Where: Bryansk, Prospect Stanke Dimitrova, 3

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The Bryansk IT community organizes a conference for those who code (or are engaged in design). The former will hear a lot of interesting technical topics: why Nuxt.js is good for the front-end, what ReactPHP can do to increase productivity, what is the specifics of Single Page Application development, and much more. The second is waiting for reports on how to work with layouts, create a personal brand, make animations on the web and begin the journey of the designer as a whole. After the official part, guests will have time to talk about their projects and make new acquaintances.



Frontday



When: October 26

Where: Togliatti, Southern Highway, 165

Terms of participation: free, registration required



The general gathering of front-end vendors of the Volga region mixed with specialists from other regions and countries. Priority issues this year were selected as follows: development methodologies and tools, automation, interface performance, optimization, modern frameworks, template engines and preprocessors, testing. Speakers will discuss all this plus the problems of teamwork and career growth.



DevWhatYouLove



When: October 26

Where: Moscow, Spartakovsky Lane, 2, p. 1

Terms of participation: 4000 rubles.



A conference dedicated to debugging workflows in a team of programmers will be of interest to all the components of the latter - developers, leads and managers. The reports are divided into three tracks. In the first speech, they will address issues of personal and career growth, the development of professional skills necessary for the development of a specialist. The second deals with various aspects of teamwork, from prudent control and delegation to mentoring and stress monitoring. Finally, the third is given for technological cases from different industries.



PHP Meetup # 1



When: October 26

Where: Rostov-on-Don, Theater Avenue 85

Terms of participation: free, registration required



PHP also comes to Rostov-on-Don: the Rostov community decided to continue and speed up thematic meetings. The program includes four reports from developers from large teams. The first speaker will talk about various types of authentication: what tools they use, how they work and why they are used where they are used. The second will demonstrate how Bitrix uses modern technologies (aravel integration, Symfony components, React SSR, CI, IoC, webpack & ES6 +). The third speaker will present an overview of Laravel 8 - from problems and bottlenecks to trends and prospects. Finally, the final talk will focus on FSM design patterns.



Gig @ office bytes



When: October 30

Where: St. Petersburg, Bolshoi Prospect P.S., 37

Terms of participation: free of charge, registration by application



The opportunity to talk about the most prosaic problems of IT teams: equipping and arranging the workspace. This time we will talk about the pangs of relocation and expansion, as well as new technological solutions for offices. In addition, analytical data on the state of the labor market and the requests of IT specialists today will be presented.



C ++ Russia



When: October 31-November 1

Where: St. Petersburg, pl. Victory 1

Terms of participation: from 19 000 rubles.



A conference for those who write in C ++ and want to know more about it in every way: concurrency, performance, architecture, infrastructure solutions and so on. The venue will bring together representatives of many large players in the international and Russian markets (Adobe, Facebook, Yandex, Kaspersky Lab) with presentations based on personal experience. In addition to the three tracks, the organizers offer participants to continue communication in the discussion areas and at democratic BOF sessions. The program also includes three workshops from expert developers.
