Ivanovo! Mitap in honor of the 10th anniversary of Node.js

Hello, Habr! Involta again invites all advanced IT professionals, as well as those who want to become them, to their new spacious office. This time we are preparing a large-scale immersion in the JavaScript world to talk about the most popular technology in the JS universe - the software platform Node.js.







The first release of Node.js took place in 2009. Then Node.js was more of an experimental technology than the basis for large projects, but times are changing and today Node.js is used everywhere. The main feature of the technology is that it makes it possible to build serious high-loaded systems on a simple and lightweight mechanism.
