What harm can health bring seaweed

Useful products for the thyroid gland, such as seaweed in our case, unfortunately, can cause harm to health. So, there are several nuances:





  • It is important to consider the location of the kelp. After all, algae tend to absorb not only beneficial substances, but also harmful ones from seawater. If kelp grew in waters with a problematic ecological situation, then it can contain various harmful substances, including radioactive elements.
  • It is not recommended to use seaweed for people who may be allergic to one of its components. The reaction of the body can be unpredictable (from skin rashes to attacks of suffocation). Therefore, it is advisable to conduct tests for the presence of such reactions.
