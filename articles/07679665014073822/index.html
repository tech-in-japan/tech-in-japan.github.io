Dried apricots: useful properties and contraindications

The uniqueness of dried fruits lies in the fact that they retain almost all the vitamins that are found in fresh fruits. Dried apricots have long been considered a very healing dried fruit. How do dried apricots

In whole apricots, an incision is made and a stone is pulled out. The most faithful manufacturing method is to dry fruit in direct sunlight in a specialized place. This process continues for a little over a week. During this time, moisture evaporates, and weight is reduced by 4 times. As a result, the finished fruits have a dull copper or brown color with a slight grayish tone.









How to choose

The ability to choose dried apricots will allow you to purchase a natural product. According to generally accepted recommendations, dried fruits are clean, large, resilient, not very transparent and a little harsh. If the color is unnatural and too bright, it means that in the manufacture of chemicals were used to create a beautiful appearance of dried fruit.









How to store

If you store dried fruits properly, their appearance and taste will not disappoint. Dried apricots are dried and dried (the calorie content of both species is approximately the same). Storage is exclusively dried. Due to its low liquid content, it will lie longer than dried.









The best option for preserving sweet fruits is a dry, dark place and room temperature. To avoid oxygen entering the product, it must be kept in a tightly closed glass container. When using paper or cellophane packaging, harmful insects may appear. To save dried apricots for a long time (several months), it can be placed in the freezer.
