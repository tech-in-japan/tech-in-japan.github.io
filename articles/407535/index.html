Testers complained that the battery charge indicator does not work on ARM laptops with Windows 10, but this turned out to be not a bug





Microsoft first announced a collaboration with Qualcomm and porting Windows 10 to ARM laptops and tablets last December . Then Qualcomm announced a new 64-bit Snapdragon 835 processor (ARMv8), on which Windows 10 can work. These processors can be installed in laptops instead of traditional Intel and AMD chips. The exact same mobile processor works on the Samsung Galaxy S8, LG V30 and OnePlus 5 smartphones.



In May 2017, Microsoft engineers showed the first working prototype of a computer with a Qualcomm Snapdragon SDM835 processor at a clock frequency of 1.9 GHz, with 4 GB of RAM. The shown instance of Windows 10 for ARM successfully launched even x86 applications from outside the Windows Store. In appearance, Windows 10 on ARM is no different from the OS on a normal computer.



Soon the official announcement of the laptop motherboard with a Snapdragon 835 processor took place. Executive Vice President of Qualcomm Technologies Cristiano Amon (Cristiano Amon) then said that thanks to the new CPU, manufacturers will be able to produce PCs in the form factor of the ‚Äúnext generation‚Äù with power consumption 50% less.

Microsoft representatives visited the annual 5G Summit in Hong Kong, which suits Qualcomm, and shared new information on ARM devices on Snapdragon 835 mobile chips.



First, they made it clear that laptops will be released without delay. They will be on sale before Christmas, that is, in December 2017. This information was confirmed by Vice President of Global Product Marketing Qualcomm Don Maguire (Don McGuire). At the moment, Microsoft has concluded agreements on the release of ARM-devices under Windows 10 with three manufacturers: HP, Asus and Lenovo. Negotiations are underway with other OEM assemblers. Most likely, there will be a large line of laptops and tablets in a wide price range.







The Snapdragon 835 on-chip system contains a Kryo 280 CPU, Adreno 540 graphics processor and Hexagon 682 DSP. The chip is made on the 10-nm technical process, which means a significant reduction in energy consumption. The Snapdragon 835 Mobile PC Platform computing platform also includes a Snapdragon X16 Gigabit LTE modem with download speeds of up to 1 Gbps and a 2x2 802.11ac MU-MIMO modem for wireless communication at close range.



Windows 10 for ARM is Microsoft's second attempt to release Windows for ARM processors. The previous version of Windows RT was not a success, including due to a lack of compatible software. Now there will be no such problem. Existing x86 win32 applications are installed and run on ARM completely transparently - without the need for additional actions by the user or application developer. There are office applications, Photoshop, video player, etc.



The x86 instructions are translated into ARM64 instructions transparently during program execution, and are also cached on disk for faster use in the future. The kernel, drivers, and all applications work completely natively (ARM code). Thus, application developers will not have to recompile their programs for a new platform: the ARM binary in the system is automatically precompiled. Unless the developer needs to put one tick in UWP.
