Board Games Development Lecture Day (Moscow)

We invite everyone who wants to make their own games to the event, start their journey in the gaming industry, indie developers, students, employees of gaming companies, as well as those who simply love board games!



December 22, 2019 at the Higher School of Business Informatics of the National Research University Higher School of Economics (Moscow, metro station Riga), a lecture day on the development of board games will be held.







From 12:00 to 16:30 four interesting and useful lectures will be held.

From 11:00 to 17:00 you will find a free game library with various board games and Workshop to promote yourself and your projects.



If you are a developer of board games , and you have a finished project, you can apply for free participation in the showcase of game projects !



And if you just want to relax, play board games , find like-minded people and make useful contacts, then these events are for you! Come and bring your friends! :)



At the entrance you will need to show the passport to the building guard and be on his lists. You can get into these lists by registering on the event website. To participate in the showcase, you also need to register.



Entrance to the event is from 11:00.

Location: Moscow, ul. Trifonovskaya, d. 57, p. 1 (metro station Rizhskaya), conference hall (2nd floor).



Age limit 18+.



Please bring an identification document with you.
