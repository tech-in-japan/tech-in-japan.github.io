When a person becomes ill with a cold, his entourage has a desire to feed him. This is one of the mistakes on the way to recovery. The nutritional features of colds and flu include frequent meals in small portions, at least 5-6 times a day.

Dishes prepared during this period must meet the following requirements:





  1. Food should be easily digestible and high-energy.

What to exclude from the diet?

From the diet for influenza and the common cold of an adult, it is necessary to exclude:





  1. Semi-finished products. The more severe the disease, the easier the food should be. Therefore, foods that have an increased load on the digestive tract should be excluded from the diet. Convenience foods and fast foods are poorly digested and create heaviness in the stomach.
  2. Fried and fatty foods. Such food should be completely excluded from the diet. It contains a lot of fat and carcinogens. Forcing the liver to fight these substances, the body is exposed to an increased load that it does not need during the period of the disease.
  3. Juices. It is not recommended to drink freshly prepared and purchased drinks with cough and sore throat. With a cold, sour juice can irritate the inflamed mucosa. Because of this, the throat will hurt more, and the healing process will be delayed.
  4. Alcohol. The medicinal use of such drinks is unacceptable. Alcohol is a diuretic that enhances the dehydration caused by the common cold. In addition, it can speed up digestion, leading to diarrhea.
  5. Sweet confectionery. Sugar weakens the immune system, which slows down the healing process of the patient. Its particles settle on the mucosa, which contributes to the active growth of microorganisms and increases the risk of complications.
  6. Hard food. It includes crackers, cucumbers, shortbread dough. They can injure the irritated mucous membrane of the throat and worsen the condition of the patient.

During the illness, fried, salty and spicy foods should not be included in the diet. Due to the ability to irritate the inflamed mucosa and increase its edema.
