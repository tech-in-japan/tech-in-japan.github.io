Puberty

In girls, this period is usually called puberty. The first menstruation in girls begins already in the middle of this cycle. What happens to the girl in this segment of life? There is a process of transformation from a girl into a mature woman who is able to continue her family. The first day of menstruation occurs approximately one year after the onset of puberty. The first menstruation is called "menarche." This suggests that the ovaries began to function, and now they are able to produce hormones. Right now, ovulation appears and pregnancy is very likely.





The first menstruation should normally begin between twelve and fifteen years. There are cases when they start much earlier or later. It is important to know that there are many factors that affect the time of the onset of the first menstruation:

Early menstruation is between eight and ten years old, and late is between the ages of 15 and more. And so, how should your period go? It is important to know that this question is purely individual, because each organism is special. If there are no health problems, then the cycle should be stable. However, it takes a little time to stabilize the menstrual cycle.





What is the menstrual cycle? This is a kind of reconfiguration of the body. This process involves:

It is important for a girl to know that menstruation is a natural process that occurs during the production of hormones by the ovaries. Control (calendar method)

We figured out what menstruation means. Once again, this is the monthly blood discharge from the vagina of each woman. When the girl began menstruation, she should be accustomed to celebrate these days on the calendar. Why is this needed? Of course, the calendar tracking method helps determine the duration of the cycle and the duration of menstrual flow.

















In addition, the calendar method is a contraceptive. Thanks to the calendar, an unwanted pregnancy can be avoided, since it is possible to calculate the approximate day of ovulation. It is worth noting that this method should be combined with others, because there is a low probability of an unwanted pregnancy even on days unfavorable for conception.
