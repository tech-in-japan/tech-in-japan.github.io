In our turbulent times, dozens, if not hundreds of millions of people suffer from depression. According to the medical definition, it is a mental disorder, which is characterized by a decrease in mood, loss of the ability to experience joy, impaired thinking, and a decrease in performance. In the latter case, the person looks at everything through the prism of the negative and goes into the camp of pessimists. It is worth noting that older people are three times more depressed than young or middle-aged people. In the US, every fifth person suffers from depression.



In addition to the main ones, doctors distinguish additional symptoms of depression:



  • pessimism;
  • guilt, worthlessness, anxiety and (or) fear;
  • low self-esteem;
  • inability to concentrate and make decisions;
  • thoughts of death and (or) suicide;
  • unstable appetite, marked decrease or gain in weight;
  • glycogensia
  • disturbed sleep, the presence of insomnia or sleep.


The World Health Organization claims that depression is one of the main causes of adolescent morbidity and disability. About 5% of children and adolescents in the age group from 10 to 16 years are exposed to it. According to Americans, only every second person tries to treat depression in one way or another , the rest do nothing for it.



How is Google going to identify people suffering from depression and what will the search engine do? If an American (I remind you that this project is relevant only for the United States so far), will enter the corresponding queries into the Google search string, the latter will give information about the disorder itself and how to treat it.



If you wish, you can take a short test that can show whether a person really suffers from depression, or simply seems to him (or else he suffers from something else). This is a questionnaire PHQ-9 . The results will not be shared, this is private information, which, according to the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe corporation representatives, will help people get rid of their ailments.

After the first symptoms of depression appear. Clinical depression (major depressive disorder) also has variations. One of them is known as dysthymia . This is a chronic mood disorder, when a person complains of his bad mood every day for several years.



It is quite difficult for doctors to diagnose depression, since its symptoms are similar to the symptoms of other mental problems. Some patients simply fear antidepressants and the fact that they are prescribed something potent. Others believe that they themselves will cope with the problem, and talk about it is considered a sign of weakness. There are also people who are just afraid to get a psychiatrist.
