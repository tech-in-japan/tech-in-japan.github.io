Outdoor Future London Academy Meetup

We invite you to the Future London Academy Meetup, which will be held on October 10 at the Raiffeisenbank office in Nagatino. Find out the latest news from London about DesignOps,

UX strategy, design culture, innovative product creation frameworks and much more.







Who read the report from Yury Vetrov jvetrau already heard that the FLA week has recently passed :

UX & Digital Design Week in London with Huge, Map Project Office, Phantom, Societe Generale, Deepmind, Smart Design, Trainline, Clearleft. And on September 21, the founder of Future London Academy, Ekaterina Solomeina, will fly to Moscow and together with Yura Vetrov will talk in more detail about the latest trends and new methodologies of British innovative companies and UX agencies.



The event will be useful:



  • UX directors and designers;
  • Directors of Innovation;
  • Product managers;
  • Service designers;
  • Owners and managers of creative businesses.


Venue: Raiffeisenbank office in Nagatino (Andropova Avenue, 18k2).

Beginning : at 19:00, the doors for guests open at 18:30.

Participation in the event is free, but by prior registration
