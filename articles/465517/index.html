Paper Board Game DoodleBattle

Hello! We present to you our first board game with paper figures. This is such a wargame, but only paper. And the user makes the whole game himself :) I would like to say right away that this is not another adaptation, but a completely developed project. We made and invented all the illustrations, figures, and rules up to every letter and pixel ourselves. So it goes :)







DoodleBattle: FlatArms is a turn-based wargame with a battlefield and rulers, where two squads complete dangerous missions, win and lose in a crazy fight for possession of secret information and technologies. Of course, the game is equipped with everything necessary from the envelope: figures, field, buildings, rules, a set of missions, rulers and cubes.



Events unfold around our time, somewhere in Dudlomir, near Doodlesity. While civilians, not suspecting anything, go about their business - megacorporations and secret and military organizations are engaged in the theft and protection of information, valuable materials and technologies. Conflicts, strategy, economics and random ... There is everything that we love so much in such miniature games.



Our figures are not wooden or even plastic, but voluminous paper models with colorful prints! And this means that you do not have to go broke buying a starter, assembly tools and pigments for painting your mines. You will need two hands, scissors, glue and a little time to assemble your squad and arm it with whatever you want.







Our paper constructor allows you to create your own characters. Arm and change their equipment during the game. Each time you can create different units and use a new strategy.



Buildings are freely assembled and disassembled for storage in an envelope, do not take up precious space on a shelf. Our figures are primarily toys, not dust collectors. A large number of additional figures, squads, buildings and machines allow you to make the game more diverse and interesting. You can even use them in any other games, invent your own rules, or simply create an entire city on your desk.

Key features of the game:



  • Paper figures with interchangeable weapons and Do It Yorself format. Collecting and cutting figures is sometimes no less interesting than playing! With the help of scissors and glue, you yourself can collect all the designs, weapons and characters.
  • Low cost for playing with voluminous figures. The basic complete set for the game costs only 860 rubles, and additional models - from 120 rubles.
  • Compact and easy storage. Buildings are freely assembled and disassembled for storage, and some models open and you can place a whole squad in them :)
  • The game is perfect for children and family activities. Collecting figurines is easy and even a child can handle it. In addition, it is a great hobby accessible to all, fostering accuracy and patience. Paper designers contribute to the development of fine motor skills, imagination and creativity.


Now we are creating the story of our little Dudlomir. And soon we are waiting for the advent of aliens, the expansion of Doodlosity and, of course, the invasion of zombies.
