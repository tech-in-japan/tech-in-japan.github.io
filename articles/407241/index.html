How to create an online platform for learning English





EnglishDom is an online English language school on Skype, we have been teaching since 2010. Now there are more than 300 people in our staff, we spent 600,000+ Skype lessons and helped 10,000 students achieve their goals in learning English.



In September 2017, we developed and launched our own online English learning platform - EnglishDom Class . How we created it, we will tell in this article.



User Survey



We already had Skype lessons, conversational clubs and an online English tutorial. All this helped to create the platform, because we were tracking which types of tasks were the most popular for students.



In the top tasks included:



  • choose whether the offer is true;
  • put the proposals in the correct order;
  • insert the word in the right place in the text.
  • choose the correct option;
  • enter the desired form of the word.


We decided to leave these types of tasks as basic for working with audio, video and text content, because, according to a survey of our students, they turned out to be the most effective.

Occupation structure



We brought out the final structure of the lesson, which should last 50 minutes:



  • Warm up

    This is a text task for immersion in an English-speaking environment (up to 5 minutes) and its task is to make contact between the student and the teacher, to tune in the desired way to work in the class.
  • Vocabulary

    A selection of 15 words on the lesson with tasks to them (up to 10 minutes). Section to form an active vocabulary with the help of interactive exercises, supported by voice acting and pictures.
  • Get to know

    Here you can find a grammar or new lexical constructions with tables for them (up to 10 minutes). Information is provided using tables and examples with translation and voice acting, and the format of the presentation is more like a dialogue, not a lecture.
  • Let's practice

    This is an interactive assignment to consolidate the theory (up to 10 minutes). Three types are presented - tests, writing the correct word and searching for errors in the sentence. The main task is to work out the previously studied theory and vocabulary and practice writing.
  • Life story

    Audio, video or text on the lesson with interactive tasks (up to 5 minutes). This section helps to improve listening skills, contextual perception of vocabulary and a quick acquaintance with the text.
  • Role play

    The main task is to simulate a real dialogue on procurement (up to 5 minutes). So the student fixes the learned vocabulary and learn how to properly build a dialogue.
  • Discussion

    Here are a number of questions on the lesson topic, so that the student learns to express his point of view (up to 10 minutes). This section helps you to form detailed answers in English and discuss.
  • Game

    These are tasks of various types with a positive emotional message that make a student creative (up to 10 minutes). The key goal of the assignment is to teach the student to improvise and speak correctly in English.


This is enough to familiarize yourself in detail or to fix one grammatical subject.



Control in class



We made the teacher a process leader and assigned the role of task navigator. In addition, the teacher can directly in class choose content that is more suitable for a particular student.



For example, a student does not want to come up with examples today or get involved in games in English - you can pass a role-playing situation or discussion, and vice versa - if you wanted to be creative - go into games.



Full sync



All work in the classroom has become synchronized:



  • the student sees what the teacher is doing and vice versa;
  • you can highlight the cursor with individual words, phrases and other content for the student;
  • you can see which words the student adds to the dictionary and writes to the chat;
  • The chat history of the lesson is available to both parties.


Thus, the teacher becomes a mentor, whose main goal is to select the right type of assignments, to motivate and engage in the educational process. The platform will do the rest, because, thanks to its clear structure, the student is guaranteed quality and progress, which is 40% faster than traditional teaching methods.
