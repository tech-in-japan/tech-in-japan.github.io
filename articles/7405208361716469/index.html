How to boil squid?

To cook squids, you should heat the water by adding salt, one bay leaf and a little pepper. After boiling, the peeled squid carcass must be placed in water and boiled for ten seconds. This time is enough for the seafood to cook. If you cook squids for longer than a minute, they become stiff, which makes them not so tasty and healthy.





However, if there is a need to quickly boil frozen squids, then you can increase the time to four minutes. Thus, this time is enough for the product to thaw and cook.
