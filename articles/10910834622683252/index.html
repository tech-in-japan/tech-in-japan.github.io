In order to make a comparison between it and oatmeal, you should get a little information about the product.





Hercules is oatmeal. They are very popular. The reason is that the cooking time is from three to twenty minutes.

If we talk about the cost, then this product is more expensive, because in order to get flakes, the grain is exfoliated, hard scales are removed. After that, it is steamed at high temperature.





The cereal swells, it is passed through the rollers. She turns into flakes. This treatment reduces cooking time. Depending on it, Hercules is divided into the following types:

The most useful view is the last. It is least processed, which means that it retains its basic properties.

According to an old recipe: oatmeal porridge

It will take one liter of water, two hundred and fifty grams of grain, one tablespoon of sugar, fifty grams of oil and salt.





  • The cereal is washed, filled with boiling water, covered with a lid.
  • Swells all night (at least eight hours).
  • Then sugar and salt are added to the mixture. It is mixed and transferred to a pot.
  • Put in the oven. The temperature is one hundred degrees. It languishes for two hours.
  • After that, the pot is taken out, oil is added to the porridge. Mixes up.




Oatmeal plus dried fruits

Dried fruits will change the taste of the dish, make it more healthy and rich. To make breakfast faster, take crushed cereals. In order to make a treat, you will need:





  • two hundred grams of flattened oats;
  • six hundred and fifty milliliters of water;
  • dried apricots - five pieces;
  • two tablespoons of raisins;
  • sugar and salt to taste.

Cooking:





  • Wash raisins and dried apricots and pour boiling water for twenty minutes.
  • Sort out cereals and rinse with warm water.
  • Put the oatmeal in the pan, fill it with water. Add raisins, cut into small pieces of dried apricots.
  • Bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce fire to a minimum.
  • Cook for twenty-five minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add sugar, salt.

Such a dish in the morning will help to recharge your batteries with energy and energy for the whole day.

Dietary delicacy on kefir

Oatmeal fritters according to this recipe are suitable for people who adhere to proper nutrition, but they can not stand the taste of oatmeal. By adding fruit, you change it. Useful qualities are preserved.





We proceed to the cooking process.





  • Using a coffee grinder or blender, the flakes are crushed to powder. Pay particular attention to the quality of the cereal. Otherwise, crepes may be bitter.
  • In a deep bowl with a blender, turn a banana into porridge. It is best to use an overripe fruit. In this case, sugar is not added.
  • Add kefir, baking powder, a spoonful of oil to a banana.
  • Add the flakes and flour to the resulting mass. It turns out the usual dough.
  • Heat the pan. Put a little oil, melt it. Spread the dough in small portions.




  • The finished dish can be poured with honey.

Fritters are obtained not only tasty, but also useful.
