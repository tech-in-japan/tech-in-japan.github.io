Why are pediatricians against red caviar?









Caviar, like most seafood, has long been attributed to the strongest allergens, along with oranges and chocolate. We have already mentioned this. And any mom knows what will follow the use of a product from this list during breastfeeding.

Sometimes an allergic reaction lasts a whole week, causing the baby a lot of trouble.





The second reason that doctors against red caviar during breastfeeding is more commonplace. Caviar, like any fish products, is a rather strongly salted product. Choose the right caviar nursing

Red caviar is a rather capricious product. And provided that the woman is breastfeeding, the choice of jars of this delicacy should be taken more than seriously.





First rule

Second rule

Third rule
