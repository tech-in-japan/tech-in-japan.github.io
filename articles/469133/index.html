What gadgets has Amazon introduced at its Hardware Event 2019





Amazon Hardware Event recently ended, a big presentation of new devices from Uncle Bezos. We have heard of few people about them, and in America these are some of the best-selling gadgets. More than a third of US households now have one version of Echo's smart speakers, for example.







This year, the company hosted an Apple-style event, with a bunch of journalists, only behind closed doors and without an online stream. In total, 15 of her own devices were announced to her, all with the support of "Alexa". At the same time, the lineup came out very varied. Each smart device is clearly tailored to its audience. Some of them are exclusive, others are just weird.







Consider the order of announcement. We will not touch on small updates (like the new version of Echo Dot with a built-in clock). Only the most delicious, only hardcore.
