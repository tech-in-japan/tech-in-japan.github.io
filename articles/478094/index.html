A selection of upcoming free events for developers in Moscow





I am a developer and I like to attend profile events. In order not to miss interesting and useful events for programmers, I created the telegram channel ITMeeting , where I publish announcements of events taking place in Moscow. And for those who could not come to the event or live in another city, I publish links to live broadcasts. I can notice that for good meetings, registration closes almost on the first day, since the number of places to visit is limited. Therefore, it is very important to learn about interesting events before others. From my own experience I can say that in case of registration closure, you can contact the organizers and ask them to consider your application individually. Perhaps they will go to meet you and confirm the application.
