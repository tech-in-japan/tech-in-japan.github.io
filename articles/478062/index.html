Professional community Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow launches a series of events on ML & AI





Machine learning and artificial intelligence systems are increasingly found in our everyday life, medicine, economics and finance, archeology, logistics, etc. The areas of activity can be listed further, which further emphasizes the relevance of the discussion of technical problems that ML & AI engineers are working on.



With this meetup, the Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow community opens a series of events in the field of Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence, so that engineers in this field can meet more often, share their experience and work results, learn something new from each other.



During technical presentations, engineers from companies such as Catalyst, Yandex, Kaspersky Lab and Rambler Group will share their knowledge with you. The program of the event consists of four presentations, from which you will learn what are the features of using machine learning in large projects, and how to develop your skills for effective use in your own projects.



The meeting will take place on December 5th in the office of Rambler Group, which is located at Moscow, Varshavskoye Shosse 9, Building 1, Entrance 5 (BC Danilovskaya Manufactory, Rows of Soldatenkov). The number of seats is limited, to participate you must register HERE .



Program Content:

19:00 - 19:15 Registration and opening of the mitap: Grab a nametag and say hello!



19:15 - 19:45 Sergey Kolesnikov from Catalyst - Catalyst valuable DL & RL research

19:45 - 20:15 Ivan Sosin from Kaspersky Lab - 3D Object Detection



20:15 - 20:30 Break: Stretch your legs, get some coffee



20:30 - 21:00 Anastasia Gaidashenko and Sergey Yudin from Yandex - The next era of search usage: query recommendation

20:00 - 21:30 Aleksey Guryanov from Rambler Group - Interpretability of machine learning models



21:30 - 23:00 Networking: Let's talk!
