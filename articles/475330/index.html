Miro Platform Plugin Contest with $ 21,000 Prize Pool

Hello! We launched an online competition for developers to create plugins on our platform. He will go until December 1. We invite you to participate !



This is an opportunity to create an application for a product with 3 million users worldwide, including teams from Netflix, Twitter, Skyscanner, Dell and others.







Rules and Prizes



The rules are simple: create a plugin on our platform and send it before December 1.



On December 6, we, the Miro platform team, will award the authors of the twenty best plugins:



  • $ 10,000 for first place
  • $ 5,000 for the second,
  • $ 3,000 for the third,
  • Amazon $ 200 Gift Vouchers for the authors of 17 other top apps.


To participate in the competition, it is enough to register with one or a team of up to 4 people, study the capabilities of the platform, create and send us a plugin.



What plugins can be implemented



Better than the teams themselves, no one knows what the challenges of teamwork are. Therefore, we launched the platform - a tool for creating custom solutions, for example, an automatic plug-in for retrospectives or automatic clustering of ideas after a brainstorming session.



Within the framework of the platform, we propose focusing on two large groups of tasks:

  • visual work on various types of content: from documents that product managers work with to prototypes of designers;
  • effective teamwork: for example, help bots in remote meetings and processes.


We talked about why we are building the platform , prepared livecoding for creating the application and examples of implemented applications and ideas based on the most frequent requests from users. If you still have questions, you can ask us here or in Slack, a link to which will come to you after registration for the contest.



Join the contest to create an application that will be used by 3 million users worldwide!
