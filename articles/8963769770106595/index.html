How to get rid of starch

Many nutritionists believe that the calorie content of baked potatoes in the oven is quite large, since this product has a high starch content. However, it is worth noting that starch is also found in other vegetables and fruits.













To get rid of a large amount of starch, you must first chop the vegetables and wash them thoroughly under running cold water. Then put the prepared slices on a paper towel so that it absorbs excess moisture. When the potato wedges dry well, put them on a baking sheet, sprinkle a little with olive oil. In this case, baked potato calories per 100 grams will not be so large, but all the nutrients will be completely preserved.
