Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow | Launch event





September 10 Join the first Facebook Developer Circle: Moscow! At the meeting, which will be held in our office, we will talk in detail about the community and talk about interesting technologies.



Among the speakers:



  • Wiki-Maria Gibbons (Facebook, EN) talks about the Facebook Developer Circle.
  • Marcus Brunner (Facebook, EN) gives an overview of the technologies used on Facebook.
  • Daniil Sokolyuk (Rambler Group, RUS) will share his experience using GraphQL: why is it needed and why it was started to be used in the Poster.
  • Kirill Vysokolov (Waves, RUS) will talk about the introduction of slots in React and how they can be used to flexibly and conveniently composition complex UI components.


Timetable:



19:15 - 19:45 - gathering guests

19:45 - 20:00 - opening of the meeting, presentation of the community

20:00 - 21:30 - speeches by speakers

21:30 - 23:00 - afterparty



The event is free, and registration is required .



Time: Gathering guests at 19-15.

Location: Warsaw highway, 9, p. 1, entrance number 5. Attic Rambler Group







Be sure to register and take your passport or other identification document with you so that the security of the business center misses you!



Come yourself and bring friends - it will be interesting!
