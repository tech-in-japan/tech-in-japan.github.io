Tangerines and oranges

Surely you happened to observe confusion near the store counter when customers did not know what is better to choose. Let's try to figure out which is more beneficial: orange or mandarin. Children usually prefer small tangerines; they are easy to clean and sweeter. Adults more often give choice to large, sweet and sour and refreshing oranges. Consider the composition of both fruits. By the content of B1, B2, B5, B9 and ascorbic acid, an orange is in the lead, and only by the number of B3 and B6 is mandarin ahead. Calcium and potassium are more in the first, and iron, calcium, sodium and magnesium - in the second. The energy value is slightly higher in mandarin. In general, as you can see, it is quite difficult to conclude what is more useful: orange or mandarin. The differences are not so radical. If you are on a strict diet, it is better to choose a fruit with a lower energy value, in other cases there is not much difference.

How to choose and store tangerines

You should choose beautiful fruits, with smooth skin, without rot and specks. If you do not navigate in the grades, go to the seller you trust - he will help you with the purchase. Remember that ripe fruits are not stored for a long time. If you bought them in advance, then try to extend their life. To do this, rub the skin with vegetable oil and place the fruit in a special compartment of the refrigerator and keep it at a temperature of +6 degrees. The humidity level is very important, you can not let them dry. Be sure to remove the plastic bag from the tangerines and place them on the net. If there are leaflets on the branches, do not break them. So fruits are stored longer. Now you know how wonderful the fragrant, sunny mandarin is.
