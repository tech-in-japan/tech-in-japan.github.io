Display



ZenFone 3 Deluxe is equipped with a 5.7-inch display, manufactured using Super AMOLED technology. Resolution 1920 x 1080. Protective glass - Gorilla Glass 4 with oleophobic coating. The width of the side frames of the display is very small, only 1.3 mm. The image has a very high contrast and a decent maximum brightness. The color gamut is more than 100% of the NTSC color space, while True2Life technologies are used in the smartphone, providing adaptive enhancement of color quality depending on the content. I really liked his color rendition, especially the color of the sky during the shooting. The colors are saturated, but not redundant, and even in the bright sun the image almost does not fade: the manufacturer claims a contrast level of 3,000,000: 1.



Sound



The smartphone has already applied the third version of the proprietary technology SonicMaster. This is a set of hardware and software solutions that allows you to achieve good sound as embedded 5-magnetic speakers, and the main audio section: ZenFone 3 Deluxe can play music in 192 kHz / 24 bit. I can say that the smartphone sounds as good as, for example, ZenFone 3 Zoom or ZenFone 3 Laser .



Photography



The main camera lens is covered with sapphire crystal. Matrix - Sony IMX 318 with a resolution of 23 megapixels. The camera supports HDR-shooting in real time. As with ZenFone 3 Ultra, Deluxe uses the TriTech Triple Tracking AF system: a combination of phase , servo and laser focus. Thanks to this trio, the smartphone focuses very quickly is able to ‚Äúcatch‚Äù the fleeting moments. The maximum exposure time is 32 seconds. There are shooting modes time lapse and slow motion, also available in 4K video (3840x2160). The optical stabilizer is used only when taking photos, and the video is stabilized using an electronic 3-axis stabilizer. The RGB sensor combined with the flash module is responsible for color correction during shooting. It allows the camera to more accurately choose white balance.



Deluxe is equipped with a color correction sensor, due to which corrections are made to the color temperature of images during shooting. And the habitual feature has become HDR in real time: on the fly, the smartphone processes the image coming from the camera so that when you link up the scene live you see on the screen what the picture will look like in HDR mode.

Software



The smartphone runs Android 6.0 with a traditional Zen UI branded GUI that supports the ability to quickly install graphics themes.











Also in ZenFone 3 Deluxe there is a convenient function of constantly displaying the clock, date and several system icons on the screen of a locked smartphone: missed calls, messages and battery charge level.











The fingerprint scanner can be used not only to unlock the smartphone, but also to answer incoming calls and release the camera shutter.







Performance



ZenFone 3 Deluxe comes with a four-core 64-bit Snapdragon 820 , manufactured using a 14-nanometer process technology, and a 6 GB RAM. For processing graphics chip is responsible Adreno 530.







ZenFone 3 Deluxe uses technology to increase the efficiency of using RAM - OptiFlex. In the settings, you can specify up to 10 applications to which the OptiFlex will be applied, which should speed up their work. Apparently, the selected applications are permanently stored in RAM (in whole or in part), therefore, when they are loaded and working, calls to the system memory are less often performed.
