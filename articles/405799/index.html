And what about competitors?



At the moment, we know about three large (relatively) companies that are working to create a network of high-speed trains, which work on the principle voiced by Musk in 2013. Shervin Pishevar, head of Hyperloop One, on Wednesday announced that his company had completed the second phase of vehicle testing in Nevada. So, in 2017, this company tested the chassis for the XP-1 transport capsule in a DevLoop tunnel in Nevada. Engineers were able to achieve controlled movement and vehicle levitation in a vacuum .





The company built a test tunnel with a length of half a kilometer, and pumped air out of it, reducing the pressure to 5 Pa. Thus, the chassis moved in a technical vacuum. In principle, the characteristics of the tunnel can accelerate the capsule up to 400 km / h, but during the test, the vehicle speed reached only 112 km / h. The tests were considered successful.
