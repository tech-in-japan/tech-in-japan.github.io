Chamomile from thrush: use, methods, contraindications

Many women and even young girls have a disease such as thrush (candidiasis). This ailment is treated very hard and for a long time. In folk medicine, chamomile from thrush is often used. This plant has a huge amount of nutrients.





A bit about the disease

Candidiasis is a fairly common infectious disease. After passing the tests in the patient's urine, there are a large number of Candida type fungi. The disease itself is accompanied by tangible discomfort and even pain. If a person for a long time does not seek help from a doctor, then thrush is treated much longer. Most doctors prescribe medications to the patient, but they are also advised to take a closer look at traditional medicine.









Thanks to two directions, you can deal with the disease much faster. Particular preference is given to medicinal plants. So, for example, many use chamomile against thrush. The plant contains substances that contribute to the rapid recovery of women.





Treatment of candidiasis with chamomile is carried out using decoctions for washing, douching and baths. However, many do not know how to make douching with chamomile with thrush. Therefore, you must read the article in detail.









The main causes and symptoms of the disease

The very cause of the disease, that is, the fungus, is always present in the human body. It is worth noting that this pathogen also has useful functions. However, in the presence of certain problems, the fungus begins to multiply and cause a person great discomfort. This happens if a person has:





  • diabetes;
  • infectious diseases;
  • critical days;
  • immunodeficiency;
  • pregnancy;
  • genital diseases.




You can get candidiasis sexually. But it does not apply to sexually transmitted diseases. Chamomile from thrush will help only if the diagnosis is clarified. Therefore, you need to know all the symptoms:





  • severe burning sensation;
  • genital itching;
  • bad smell;
  • white and curdled vaginal discharge;
  • redness and swelling of the genital area.

The chemical composition of chamomile

The great benefits of a medicinal plant are its rich composition. The following substances are included in a camomile:





  • vitamins - C, B4 and carotene;
  • essential oil;
  • active elements - chamazulen, flavonoids, matricin, apigenin, phytosterol and coumarin;
  • polyene mixtures;
  • gum;
  • tannin compounds;
  • organic acids;
  • macro-, microelements;
  • bitterness and mucus;
  • polysaccharides.

Thanks to the above chemical composition, baths and douching from thrush will be quite productive.













Useful properties of the plant

Due to its rich composition, chamomile has useful properties. In addition, many herbalists recommend this plant as the safest. In addition to gynecological problems, chamomile helps to normalize the functioning of the gastrointestinal tract, as well as the genitourinary system.





Since the medicinal plant has anti-inflammatory, antiseptic and disinfectant effects, it is often used for cosmetic purposes. Chamomile also helps to strengthen the immune system and stabilize the nervous system. Means prepared on the basis of chamomile have a beneficial effect on a woman during menopause.









In addition, the healing plant helps alleviate painful menstruation. It is also effective in diseases of the uterus and postpartum hemorrhages. Chamomile has a beneficial effect on pregnant women. She fights excessive gas formation in expectant mothers, and also ensures the normal functioning of the gastrointestinal tract. Since the plant has a sedative effect, it has a positive effect on the nervous system of pregnant women.





Thanks to the healing composition, chamomile is widely used in the cosmetology direction. It is added to various creams, tonics and lotions. These funds help whiten and rejuvenate the skin, rid the face of a large number of freckles, acne and age spots.





But the main thing how a chamomile can help is to get rid of the symptoms of a cold as soon as possible. In this case, it warms, relieves inflammation and fever. No less useful is the plant for kidney, liver and endocrine function. Chamomile is recommended for those who suffer from diseases of the cardiovascular system. But the correct dosage should be prescribed only by a doctor.









Healing plant is often used for bathing children. It perfectly eliminates inflammation and irritation on the skin of the child. Also useful baths help the baby sleep well. But many pediatricians do not recommend daily bathing with chamomile. Decoctions prepared on the basis of chamomile are often used for problems with mucous membranes or inflammation of the eyes. They can also be used as lotions for skin problems: ulcers, burns and rashes.





Folk recipes

Many women often ask their doctor how to douche with a chamomile from a thrush. To answer it, you need to familiarize yourself with effective methods of treating candidiasis.





In folk medicine, chamomile has been used for several millennia. During this time, many effective recipes against thrush have accumulated. It is used immediately as soon as the first symptoms of the disease appear. The following options are used to treat thrush with chamomile:





  • douching;
  • washing;
  • baths.

Douching with chamomile from thrush at home

Before starting the treatment procedure, it is necessary to prepare a decoction. It is prepared from chamomile flowers.





  1. First of all, you need to take enameled dishes and pour a liter of water there.
  2. Then set the pot on the stove and bring to a boil.
  3. Then you need to add two tablespoons of chamomile flowers.
  4. The resulting broth is again brought to a boil.
  5. Then it is cooled to room temperature and filtered through dense cheesecloth.
  6. The finished medicine is injected directly into the vagina using a syringe.

Doctors recommend this procedure every day in the evening from seven to fourteen days.









Prerequisites for douching

Before starting the procedure, you must follow a number of mandatory recommendations:





  1. Rinse the syringe with boiling water.
  2. Treat the tip of the medical device with alcohol.
  3. Lie on your back before the procedure.
  4. To avoid burns, you need to check the temperature of the broth.
  5. Therapeutic measures need to be carried out over a large basin or in the bathroom.
  6. Relax, slowly inject the syringe and release the broth.
  7. The rest of the mixture should spill out.
  8. Stay horizontal for about ten minutes.
  9. The syringe must be kept in a pouch.

Healing baths

For the procedure, you need to fill the bathtub with hot water for fifteen centimeters. Then there you need to add one hundred grams of chamomile flowers. When the water cools down a little and takes on a characteristic color, you can immerse yourself in the bath. The procedure must be performed daily for twenty minutes.





A decoction for the bath is prepared in the same way as for douching. After the procedure, it is not advisable to rinse the genitals with running water, as well as wipe with a towel. It is enough to get your skin wet with a napkin.





Washing

Many doctors tell their patients that you can wash with chamomile with thrush. However, this method is not as effective as douching and baths. This is because the procedure affects only the lower parts of the genitals. Despite this, washing helps to get rid of severe itching and burning.





To properly prepare the broth, you need to take two large tablespoons of chopped chamomile and mix with one liter of water. The broth should be infused for an hour. Then the resulting mixture is filtered through a fine strainer. The procedure is recommended to be performed once or twice a day.





Chamomile during pregnancy

During the period of bearing a child, it is necessary to approach any kind of treatment with extreme caution. The most important thing is to choose medicines that are not absorbed through the blood and do not harm the fetus. Chamomile from thrush during pregnancy meets all these requirements.









However, it is important to remember that a woman in a position should not be douched. It can provoke premature birth. Also, throughout the pregnancy, you should not do hot baths. The most suitable procedure during this period would be a wash, which can be done twice a day.





Chamomile from thrush: reviews

Many women report enormous benefits of chamomile for candidiasis. They claim that after procedures based on a beneficial plant, the condition improves significantly.





However, they say that douching and washing should be done along with medical treatment. The plant is able to briefly remove the symptoms of the disease. But later the thrush returns again. Therefore, gynecologists recommend the use of chamomile as an addition to the main treatment.





Contraindications

Despite the enormous benefits of a medicinal plant, it can cause side effects. Do not recommend the use of chamomile in the presence of the following problems:





  • advanced form of candidiasis;
  • menstruation;
  • the postpartum period;
  • menopause;
  • allergic reactions.

It is worth noting that in women after 40, the vaginal mucosa is very thin and vulnerable. Therefore, douching or washing with a medicinal plant can lead to unwanted discomfort and dryness. In addition, there is a possibility of the appearance of blood secretions. It is also not advisable to carry out procedures using chamomile before visiting a gynecologist.





Recommendations

During treatment with chamomile for thrush, it is necessary to observe intimate hygiene. You need to wash twice a day, have an individual towel and syringe, and also often change underwear. During this period, many gynecologists do not recommend the use of disposable pads. They are able to create a favorable environment for the propagation of the fungus.









In addition, during therapy it is necessary to abstain from sexual activity. To increase the body's defenses, you can use chamomile tea for five or six cups per day. However, two cups per day are sufficient for women in position.





Preparations based on chamomile are considered an excellent complement to medical treatment. But before using them, it is advisable to visit your gynecologist. It is the specialist who will help determine the degree of the disease and advise the correct use of chamomile with thrush.
