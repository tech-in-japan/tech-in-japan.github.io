Pain during sex in the vagina: causes and how to deal with it

What to do if pain during sex in the vagina systematically occurs? If this unpleasant symptom appears periodically, you must visit a gynecologist. After a thorough medical examination of the patient, the specialist will be able to identify the factor that influenced the appearance of pain in the woman's genitals.





Features of vulvovaginitis

Under such conditions, vulvitis develops. If inflammation captures the vaginal mucosa - this is a sign of vulvovaginitis. There are several forms of this disease. Among which are:

In the chronic form of vulvovaginitis, vaginal spasm may occur during sex. Due to prolonged inflammation, atrophy of the mucous membranes occurs. Under such conditions, hyperplastic processes begin. The atrophic form of vaginitis is dangerous in that it can provoke the development of cancer.

Dry female genital organs can occur in both young girls and more mature women. Most often, a pathological condition is observed before menopause. This problem is called "atrophic vaginitis."





Dry mucous membranes cause a lot of inconvenience. Discomfort is manifested during sexual rapprochement. In some cases, there is slight bleeding that is caused by microtrauma, as there was no moisture during sex. A frequent desire to empty the bladder is the main symptom of this disease.





The above phenomena can occur due to the development of genitourinary infection. In a healthy woman, the secreted secret from the penis is colorless and odorless. Only at the time of sexual arousal does lubrication begin to be actively released. If an insufficient amount of fluid is produced, then unpleasant symptoms may appear, which manifest themselves in the form of:
