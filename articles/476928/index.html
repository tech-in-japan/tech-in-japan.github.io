Tesla Cybertruck electric pickup: presentation results





November 22, Tesla held a presentation of a new electric car called Cybertruck. There have been rumors about this model for a year. During this time, fans managed to spawn a bunch of renderings of varying degrees of credibility, but not one of them was close to the original. The video and text broadcast of the event for Habr was conducted by the guys from Moscow Tesla Club .







Even before the presentation, Musk tweeted that the CYBRTRCK resembles an armored personnel carrier from a science fiction film, and the dimensions are more than the usual pickups. There are six seats in the cabin, cruising range - 400-800 km, air suspension. The pickup will have three modifications: with one engine and rear-wheel drive, as well as with two and three engines - with all-wheel drive. Of the special features are a socket and a compressor for connecting a variety of tools. Everything else was told by Sergey Kalashnikov below.
