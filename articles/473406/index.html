Free marathon "Data Science and AI: teach the machine to write the script for the series"

“It was in the Simpsons!” For 30 years, the Simpsons seem to have shown all the plot twists imaginable. Or not? Create your script for an episode of the cult series with machine learning.



Data Science is used not only in science and business, but also in hundreds of other areas: cinema, advertising, social networks. It is difficult to imagine an industry in which data analytics would not be useful. Everyone can find their favorite direction, even if they had never worked with analytics before. It’s also fun :–)



We propose to create a script for the animated series on the online marathon !





What awaits you



  • You will understand what the work of the analyst is;
  • Learn how to search for the data you need.
  • Get familiar with the basic professional tools;
  • have a great time :–)


Your mentor



Alexey Kuzmin is a technical director at DomKlik.ru and a lover of TV shows.



Program



Episode 1: exposure



Date: November 12, 17:00 - 18:00



  • Let's look at the real dataset of the series in SQL and find out what data on the characters of the series we have.
  • Let us analyze the replicas of the characters in order to create a script based on them.


Tool: SQL.



Episode 2: Character Phrases



Date: November 13, 5 p.m. - 6 p.m.



  • We will find characteristic phrases and keywords for the characters so that the AI ​​can "understand" to whom to generate which replicas in the future scenario.


Tool: Python.



Episode 3: Generating a New Script



Date: November 14, 17:00 - 18:00



  • Using machine learning, we will teach the AI ​​to generate text for the characters in the series and try to make a script out of it! Perhaps in some places it will be even more absurd and funnier than the original.


Tool: Machine learning.
