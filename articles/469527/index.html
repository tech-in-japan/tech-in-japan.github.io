Is the intercom a spy ear?

Back in the days of analog telephone disks, there were rumors about listening to the line, even about the ability to listen to the situation without picking up the phone and talking on it. With the advent of push-button telephones, the problem seemed to go away, moreover, in connection with the principles of switching signals on a twisted pair cable, this was problematic.



The article describes the version - what if, today, this opportunity migrated to intercom networks, moreover, it is used for tacit interference in private life?



In the digital age, nothing should seem transcendental.

Perhaps in your home you should also carefully choose words and topics for everyday conversations, no matter how harmless they would be, if not today, then in the near future.
Today, almost every apartment has an intercom system, in particular, an UKP - a subscriber unit (apartment intercom), made in the form of a telephone receiver, referred to in the common people as an audio handset.



First, a little theory about the types of intercom



According to the principle of operation, intercoms are analog and digital .

By appointment, they are divided into individual and multi-tenant (multi-unit), which, as a rule, are installed in the entrances of multi-apartment buildings, providing communication between visitors and apartment owners.



The multi-door intercom operates according to a mini-automatic telephone exchange (analog or digital).

Analog (coordinate addressing system) work on the principle of conventional telephone communication using an analog switch (mini telephone exchange) and a special multi-core cable connecting the switch to apartments, they are more common and less expensive.



Digital wiring on the porch use only two wires (two-wire cable) to communicate with all apartments in the porch, and the digital intercom has the ability to perform additional functions, such as internal communication (intercom) between the tenants of the porch and the concierge serving this device. There are still video intercoms, as well as GSM with a call forwarding function.

About audio handset



In addition to the speaker and microphone, it also contains a control button for the electromagnetic / electromechanical lock of the access door.



It works on the principle of so-called duplex communication - it is a two-way communication that can be carried out simultaneously.



When you press the "open" button, as well as picking up the handset of the apartment intercom, the telephone line resistance changes , which is perceived by the microprocessor device (switch) and it sends the corresponding signals.



All circuit diagrams of analog audio tubes are almost identical , simple, but at the same time cleverly executed. The bottom line is that the speaker in the hanging state of the tube is always directly connected to the line , however, like the microphone through the resistor.



For those who do not know how to read radio circuits, I will explain by the example of the most common TKP-12M analog tube (from Metakom).









To de-energize the entire circuit, only turn off the device with a switch , if any. But anyway, when you pick up the tube, the circuit is again powered . The speaker is turned on directly, and when answering - through a resistor (voltage divider). Trimmer resistor R5 is designed to adjust the balance between the speaker and the microphone. Another radio component that affects the audibility from the speaker is the capacitor C4, but not the point.



Here the problem is not even whether the microphone is listening or not, but that the loudspeaker is the ear in absolutely all audio tube designs . Not many people know that the same speaker (telephone capsule) may well act as a microphone , albeit not with the best sound quality and characteristics. In this case, the considered scheme uses a dynamic SD-150 capsule.



About capsules



Telephone capsules are electromagnetic , electrodynamic , piezoelectric .



Electromagnetic contain a permanent magnet and a ferromagnetic membrane, electrodynamic contain a permanent magnet coil with a movable element, and piezoelectric based on the piezoelectric effect.



Unlike the loudspeaker head (speaker), the capsule does not have a diffuser or horn, so its use as a microphone is problematic (but still possible).



So, our capsule has good electro-acoustic characteristics. Sensitivity above 90dB in the range from 200 to 4000 Hz, R ~ 120 Ohms.
