Can I eat bananas for pancreatitis: allowed foods

What is pancreatitis? This is a disease of the pancreas, in which the death of its tissues can begin. If you do not catch on time and do not start treatment, everything can end very sadly. That is, fatal.





Creepy forecast, isn't it? How does treatment begin? First of all, with adjustments to nutrition. What can I eat? And what to exclude? Can I eat bananas for pancreatitis and other fruits? Now we will explain everything in detail.









Forms of pancreatitis

This disease can be chronic and acute. What characterizes both forms? In chronic pancreatitis, there is no pain. A person can live with him for years and not even guess about his illness. Until an attack occurs.





Acute pancreatitis - this is an attack. He is accompanied by nausea and debilitating vomiting, excessive sweating, severe pain. Moreover, the pain depends on what exactly worsened: the head of the pancreas, its tail or it is completely.





Symptoms of Acute Pancreatitis

As already mentioned, this is pain. If an exacerbation occurs in the tail of the organ, a person experiences severe pain in the left hypochondrium, it gives to the chest and left side. If we are talking about the head of the pancreas, then the pain is felt in the region of the right hypochondrium. If the entire organ is affected, then the pain is girdle-like.









What to do?

Urgently call an ambulance. An acute attack is accompanied, in addition to pain, by debilitating vomiting. He vomits constantly, but he does not feel relief. In addition, diarrhea may occur. It is difficult to wash off, has a very pungent odor. And pieces of food are visible in it.





In case of failure to provide proper medical care, the patient's condition worsens. And this can be fatal.





How to be treated

In the acute form, treatment is carried out in a hospital. These are medicines and a strict diet. In chronic pancreatitis, the patient is treated at home. First of all, he must strictly follow a diet. If, of course, he wants to get rid of the hated disease.





What will have to be abandoned forever?





  • Alcohol and tobacco.
  • Fatty food.
  • Pickles, smoked meats, marinades.
  • Bakery products.
  • Spicy dishes.
  • Fried food.

What can I eat?

What is the nutrition for pancreatitis? What products are acceptable?





To begin with, with acute form in the early days, hunger is beneficial. Two - three days the patient drinks only water. Then gradually begins to eat.





As for the chronic form, here the diet comes first. You should pay attention to viscous cereals and soups - mashed potatoes. This is now the sufferer's main food. Below is a list of acceptable products.





  • Viscous porridge from oat, semolina and rice groats.
  • Soups - mashed potatoes on vegetable broths. Mashed soups.
  • Soup - noodles on a weak chicken broth.
  • Dried white bread in small quantities.
  • Lean boiled meat: chicken, turkey, beef.
  • Boiled fish.
  • Jelly, jelly and compotes.
  • Boiled vegetables.
  • Fruits: apples and bananas.
  • Dairy products: low-fat kefir and cottage cheese. You can mild cheese, but not during the period of exacerbation.
