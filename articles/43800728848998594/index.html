How to dry dates at home

Arrange fresh fruits in an open area under direct sunlight. Periodically, they must be turned over. Thus, a product is obtained that retains its useful properties as much as possible.





Before eating, be sure to rinse with boiled water. Do not dry dates in ovens and ovens, do not fill in with syrup, as nutrients are lost.





Date Storage

If you have prepared a lot of the product in question, you need to know how to save it.









Keep dates at room temperature in a tightly closed container. They are placed in the refrigerator when it is necessary to preserve the fruits for a long time, for example, a year. But it is better to harvest dates in such quantities that they are spent in a few months. Storage in the freezer extends the shelf life of dates to five years.





You can make pasta from the fruit. To do this, peel the dates, beat with a blender until smooth, put in a plastic container, tightly close and store it in the cold (up to two weeks). Do not place the product in plastic bags.
