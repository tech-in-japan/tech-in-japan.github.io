Tubation of the liver





Tubage - washing the liver, gall bladder and ducts, as well as bile ducts. This procedure is carried out under the supervision and prescription of a doctor on an outpatient basis.

















Absolute contraindications for this procedure will be gallbladder stones, erosive bowel lesions, acute pancreatitis or exacerbation of chronic pancreatitis, cholecystitis, peptic ulcer and duodenal ulcers, inflammatory liver diseases such as hepatitis. Probe tube is not prescribed for diseases of the oral cavity, pharynx, esophagus and ischemic lesions. Thus, before the procedure, it is necessary to consult a doctor, and not to self-medicate.
