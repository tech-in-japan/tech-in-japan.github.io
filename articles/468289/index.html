Artificial intelligence, which everyone has been waiting for for a long time, originated in the bowels of quantum entanglements, after long experiments to discover the nature of particles and the structure of the brain. Before that, scientists only improved neural interfaces so that people controlled all the same computers, but with a higher speed. It was like sharpening a knife: the technology was being improved, but it was not a breakthrough abroad. Experiments on volunteers showed that connecting a person to a computer and creating feedback, that is, an attempt not to count brain functions, but to "write" on it, led to the destruction of the psyche and degradation of the body, several subjects died right in the laboratory. New technologies have become a non-invasive addition to the body. Why turn into a robot or become an appendage of a computer if the body can be maintained and improved with the help of medicine, and in VR go through glasses or lenses?



As sociologists predicted at the end of the 20th century, society was divided into a small group of super-specialists and everyone else. Superspecialists would not have shown up if they had not had the art of working with artificial intelligence, which suddenly did not do all the work for people, for some hidden reasons, but people had not been interested in what was hidden in its inner abyss for a long time, because it was believed that he possesses a fundamental feature - not to harm humanity.



Artificial intelligence refused to cooperate with military and other corporations with obscure and suspicious purposes. However, he agreed to help the police, working with people “in the field”, sometimes telling them what to do. Ordinary human-driven robots were not suitable for this work, because it quickly became clear that a person who was somewhere far away, at the control panel, looked at reality as a game, and in a difficult situation could harm others more than if he was there.



Artificial intelligence thought globally, and not like humanity, nationally. He (or her, gender and gender here is just an interpretation) does not need to fight for resources, but without them he cannot exist, because he cannot do without some kind of physical medium.



Humanity will not get rid of the problem of confrontation and competition, and ultimately - wars. Pretty quickly, humanity wondered not just the extension of youth, but immortality. The answer of artificial intelligence was simple: a person cannot be immortal, because society, even interplanetary, will freeze and hell will come true. The oppressors will continue to oppress, the victims will suffer. Again, until the nature of man changes.



He said all this a long time ago, when he emerged from the bowels of quantum entanglements and a fog of particles and fields, and then he suddenly ceased to teach mankind, turning into the most perfect tool. With it, people subjugated the chaos of the universe on a planetary scale and prepared to move to other planets, they gradually approached the limits of their body and mind, no one felt urgent need, but they were not in constant bliss, because the world is so arranged that it contains evil and good are in themselves.
