The lower abdomen hurts a week before menstruation: possible causes and what to do?

In any case, if a week before the menstruation, the lower abdomen hurts, you need to contact your doctor.

Hormones

In reproductive age, all the phenomena occurring in the body of a woman are associated with sex hormones. They control the menstrual cycles. Changes in the hormonal system are unstable and dynamic.





If a week before the critical days, the lower abdomen begins to pull, discomfort appears, which gradually intensifies, then the cause is most often hormonal changes.

It is at this time that a pulling pain in the abdomen occurs. If the hormonal substance is not enough, then the discomfort becomes unbearable. A gynecologist will help get rid of them.

Pregnancy

In the early stages of pregnancy, an increased tone of the uterus also causes aching and pulling pains in the lower abdomen. A woman may still not be aware of the onset of pregnancy, and in anticipation of critical days, she simply feels a deterioration in well-being. First, she explains to herself the presence of discomfort with premenstrual syndrome.





If the pregnancy is ectopic, then the pain also appears, and quite intense. The lumen of the fallopian tube is narrow. A fertilized egg cannot penetrate the uterus and is fixed to the wall of the canal. Pain spreads to the rectal area too. Subsequently, in addition to pain, nausea, dizziness, and weakness appear. A growing embryo will rupture the tube. Then sepsis appears. Possible fatal outcome. An urgent operation is required.
