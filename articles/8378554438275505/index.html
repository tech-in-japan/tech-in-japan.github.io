Causes of the disease

  • Overweight. Because of this, the disease is often called "obese people diabetes."

  • Elderly age. Usually, people of advanced age suffer from this type of diabetes. But there are times when the disease is observed in children.

Contraindications

If a person is allergic to bee nectar, honey should not be used for type 2 diabetes. Contraindications also apply to those patients whose disease is difficult to treat. Proper nutrition

Diabetes is not a sentence. With this disease, you can live normally, but with one condition: nutrition must be correct. First you need to adjust your diet so that there are no sudden surges in blood sugar.





The diet for this disease is aimed at the complete exclusion of foods containing simple carbohydrates. They contain instant sugar, which immediately raises blood glucose levels.

















Eating in patients with diabetes should be carried out strictly on time: from three to six times a day. In between, you can have a snack, but not gorge. It is necessary to refuse sweet, flour, fatty, fried, salty, smoked, spicy. It is advisable to make a table of useful and harmful products. This helps control nutrition.
