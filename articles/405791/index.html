Suddenly - Mop



Xiaomi got even to the mops - and that was Xiaomi Handheld Electric Mop . Who lived in the Soviet past, he certainly remembers the noisy slow-moving floor closures for parquet. So, they have successfully evolved into smart vacuum cleaners and no less smart, but more functional floor brushes. In fact, this is a compact floor cleaning machine - a mop sprays the smallest water suspension and removes absolutely all the dust, wipes off even complex stains from the floor. The time of continuous cleaning is 50 minutes, the rotational speed is up to 1000 times per minute, there is light for dark and hard-to-reach places. The mop has three types of "wool": soft for dust, hard for stains, terry for collecting wool, lint and other fur of domestic animals and their owners. The handle can move to any angle up to 90 degrees to the floor - so the dust under the beds and closets is also doomed. Price with coupon XMMOP - $ 159.99.
