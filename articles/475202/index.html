First impression of Xiaomi MIUI 11





So that cloudy autumn day came when another update arrived on my device (Xiaomi mi8). I already forgot the last time the last one arrived, I even thought that Xiaomi forgot about my device. And suddenly, a complete system update to MIUI 11. Let's see what the ...



Install. The first thing that is immediately visible is the new design, of course it is better than the previous one, but the modified icons reminded me very much of the application icons in iOS, and so they all confused my phone with the iPhone, but then I almost believed it. This design could be configured on MIUI10, using alternative themes, but now out of the box. But if you don‚Äôt like the standard theme, you can easily put your favorite theme from a large store of alternative themes. Now you can turn on dark mode - a dark theme, and it will be everywhere, in all applications. The theme can become dynamic, for example, turn on in the evening, and in the morning there will again be an ordinary light theme. This update has many features that have become dynamic.

Sound



Steep changes were made in the sound scheme, now there are dynamic effects and signals that work differently depending on the time of day and may never sound the same throughout the day. Just remember that the update will not update your speaker system, the sound should become better, but it will be more interesting.

Calculator application
