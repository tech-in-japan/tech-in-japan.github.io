Myths about using quail eggs













Many are accustomed to consider quail eggs a remedy for all diseases. Indeed, quail eggs do a lot of good for the normal functioning of our body. For the face, nails and hair, they are also indispensable, but do not attribute magical properties to them. Experts refute to everyone the widely known fact that these eggs are quickly digested in the body. Studies have shown that with long boiling eggs, they are digested more slowly. Therefore, the best option is to eat soft-boiled eggs.





It is well known to everyone that quail eggs do not contain cholesterol in the yolks, unlike chicken eggs. But here people are mistaken. To date, there is a lot of information that there is even more cholesterol in the yolks of these eggs than in the yolks of chicken eggs. Due to the lecithin content in small eggs, cholesterol is not deposited on the walls of blood vessels.





Quail eggs are also famous for the absence of various pathogens, in particular salmonella. Indeed, an intestinal infection caused by this bacterium is extremely rare in this case. But there are others, however, they are less dangerous for humans. In any case, it should be washed thoroughly before eating a raw egg .
