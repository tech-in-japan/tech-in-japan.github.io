December 6-8 - Rosbank Tech.Madness Hackathon

Participate in our third insane hackathon Rosbank Tech.Madness with a prize pool of 600,000 rubles. Applications are accepted through the site until November 24. Interesting? Then welcome to kat, all the details are there.







When?



At the very beginning of winter, from December 6 to 8. We promise: regardless of the temperature in the street - it will be hot!



Where?



In the ultramodern head office of Rosbank in Moscow, 34 Masha Poryvaeva Street.



For whom?



Teams from 3 to 5 people can participate in the hackathon, which may include analysts, backend / frontend developers, and mobile developers. Moreover, applications are accepted from single participants who are ready to join the team.



What tasks will you have to solve?



Objective tasks will be randomly distributed among the hackathon participants only at the opening ceremony on December 6, 2019, until the hackathon finals, participants will know only thematic areas. This will create equal competition among teams.



What for?



For the sake of eternal glory in Valhalla, of course! Well, a little for the sake of experience, interesting tasks and a prize fund of 600,000 rubles.



How to win?



Winners will be determined by the following criteria, each of which will have its own weight:



  • Code quality;
  • Functionality of the solution;
  • Design and ergonomics;
  • The potential of product integration with banking business processes and systems;
  • The quality of product presentation by the team, including the presentation of the product in the final.


How to register?



Applications are accepted until November 24 through the form on the hackathon website techmadness.ru .



Ready to give vent to your madness? Then send the application.
