Dishes for type 2 diabetics: recipes and tips

Diabetes is, of course, a serious illness and threatening with serious consequences and complications. However, this diagnosis is by no means a sentence: if you follow the rules of nutrition, the instructions of the endocrinologist and a certain lifestyle, you will not encounter any particular difficulties in the usual rhythm of existence. If, of course, you have a disease of the second type, without the need to inject insulin.





The word ‚Äúdiet‚Äù is disheartening for all people. However, the dishes for type 2 diabetics are not so bad. Their recipes are quite diverse, and you can eat almost without prejudice to your habits and preferences. Unless, perhaps, some time will be spent getting used to previously ignored cooking methods.













What is a diet?

It‚Äôs actually difficult to call her a diet. Rather, it is a diet regimen and discipline. They include only a few points:





  1. You need to eat regularly, and not occasionally. Gradually, you should accustom yourself to sit at the table at the same time.
  2. Meals per day should be at least five, but it is better to plan your life so that there are six. Servings should be small. This rhythm of nutrition prevents the manifestation of hyperglycemia - a jump in sugar levels after eating.
  3. Low calorie content. It is statistically found that most people with diabetes-2 are overweight. They are more than 80 percent of the total number of patients. Therefore, dishes for type 2 diabetics with excess weight should be with a particularly small, calculated calorie content, in order to gradually bring the weight back to normal. On the other hand, a person with a normal age and height weight does not need to count calories.
  4. Remove all processed fats from the table: margarine, mayonnaise, sauces, pastry (especially with creams).

That‚Äôs all the limitations. However, they should be treated with increased responsibility and observed with the utmost severity.

















What is absolutely impossible and what is needed

When preparing dishes for type 2 diabetics, recipes should not include:





  • Any sausage. Boiled is still occasionally acceptable, but all smoked meats - away forever.
  • All semi-finished products. And if you are diagnosed with diabetes and you are not used to standing by the stove, you will have to urgently learn how to cook.
  • Fatty meats: pork and lamb.
  • High fat dairy products. It is advisable to completely switch to low-fat, dietary types. For the same reason, sour cream should be avoided, and in extreme cases buy it light, not fatter than 15%.
  • Hard cheese is allowed selectively, only one with a low fat content.
  • Sugar should be replaced with sweeteners recommended by your doctor.

However, there are also products that are mandatory for inclusion in dishes for type 2 diabetics. There is special advice from the endocrinologist: to lean on seafood and sea fish; consume more cereals, fruits (not too sweet, grapes, by the way, are prohibited), vegetables, herbs and bread from coarse flour. Do not neglect dairy products, just pay attention to their fat content.













Cooking right

In addition to some restrictions on the ingredients, there are recommendations on the method of processing products that go to dishes for type 2 diabetics. Recipes are used only those where cooking, steaming, stewing or baking are supposed. From fried foods will have to wean.













There are rules for pre-training. The meat is bought exclusively the most lean; the skin must be removed from the bird. Moreover, in chicken, one should give preference to breast and wings, and fatty and not too useful legs should be avoided. If you use vegetable oil in stewing, it is added at the very end to prevent it from being converted into something far from useful.













Pumpkin soup

Especially useful are dishes for type 2 diabetics from pumpkin, and among them primarily soups. They are prepared easily, while tasty, nutritious, but not high-calorie. One of the most beloved by the people is done this way: a small piece of chicken, 150 grams (the entire norm allotted for the day) is laid in water. When it boils, the broth merges, and the pan is filled with fresh liquid. This procedure is repeated twice, after which the broth itself is cooked for about half an hour. Half a kilo of pumpkin is cleaned, cut lightly, mixed with onion rings and stewed until cooked. Cooked meat is passed through a blender, after which the stewed vegetable joins. Upon reaching uniformity, chicken stock is poured. When serving pumpkin soup puree, small pieces of a small slice of dorblu and mint leaves are laid in a plate.













Musaka with meat

As a second course for type 2 diabetics, recipes offer a huge selection. One of the most seductive seems to us like this. By all rules, with the discharge of the first water, a slice of lean beef is cooked for half a kilogram and cranked through a meat grinder along with two stewed onions. Two eggplants and zucchini are peeled from the skin with the stalks and cut into thin circles, then crumble in amaranth flour (it is sold in the departments for diabetics and successfully helps them fight the disease) and stewed separately to softness. Stuffing is salted and kneaded with two eggs. The bottom of the form is spread by cabbage leaves, which are laid out on top of eggplant, sprinkled with crushed garlic. Next is minced meat, zucchini on it, and so on, until the finished products are finished. The top is laid out with tomato circles; light sour cream is beaten with egg and salt and poured on them. The final touch is grated cheese. For a third of an hour in the oven - and enjoy the wonderful taste of a diet dish!

Beef ribs
