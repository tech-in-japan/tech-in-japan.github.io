How much did developers of various qualifications earn in the first half of 2019





In late July, we published a general salary report for the first half of 2019 , then looked at the salaries and popularity of programming languages , and then compared the salaries of developers from different regions adjusted for the cost of living .



Today we continue to deepen our understanding of salaries and look at the salaries of developers of various qualifications. Let's look at the state of salaries in the first half of 2019, and then at the same salaries in dynamics over the last 2 years, and finally we will analyze the qualifications in each language separately.



As always, for the study we take the data from the ‚ÄúMy Circle‚Äù salary calculator , in which users indicate the salaries they receive in their hands after deducting all taxes, and can also look at any other salaries in IT.





Salary of developers of various qualifications in the first half of 2019



To begin with, let's take a look at the salaries of developers of various qualifications.



Starting from an intern with a median salary of 30,000 rubles, the salary of each next qualification grows as follows: June - 1.7 times (50,000 rubles), middle - 1.8 (90 000 rubles), senior - 1 , 7 (150,000 rubles), lead - 1.1 times (170,000 rubles).



The lead salary in development as a whole is 3.4 times the June salary.







Now let's look at the salaries of developers of different qualifications in each language separately:



  • In June, salaries range from 40,000 (PHP, Kotlin) to 60,000 (Go, Python, Swift) and 70,000 rubles. (Ruby on Rails)
  • In middles - from 80,000 (1C, C #, PHP) to 120,000 rubles. (Objective-C, Swift).
  • Seniors - from 130,000 (PHP) to 180,000 rubles. (Objective-C, Swift).
  • Leads - from 150,000 (1C, PHP) to 200,000 rubles. (Go, Kotlin).






In general, a common pattern is observed in all languages: with increasing qualifications, the jump to the next level of salary gradually decreases. From June to Middle, the jump is the largest - 1.9 times, from Middle to Senior already smaller - 1.6 times, and from Senior to Lead quite small - 1.1 times.



There is one exception - Kotlin. Here the leap from June to Middle is very different from other languages ‚Äã‚Äãand is 2.4 times.





It is interesting to compare the maximum career step in each language.



As we saw at the beginning, on average, the salary of the highest qualification, the lead, is 3.6 times higher than the salary of the youngest, June. If we look at each language separately, we will see that the largest gap between these qualifications is among Kotlin developers, it is 4.6 times. And the smallest - at Ruby on Rails developers - 2.4 times. In the latter, it is obvious that this is due to one of the highest salaries of the Jones, in comparison with other languages.







Salary of developers of various qualifications in dynamics over the past 2 years



Now let's see how the median salaries for all qualifications have changed over the past two years.



We see that in all qualifications there was a gradual increase in salaries. Only the leading developers during this time had a slight decline, after which the growth resumed.







Over the past two years, the largest jump in the median salary of the June was 25%, followed by trainees, middle and senior, with a margin of 2%. The wages of the leaders changed the weakest - only 9%.
