Boiled beets with breastfeeding solves the problem of constipation in children. If the baby does not fulfill the need for more than 3 days, the mother can eat boiled beets. How to cook beets for a nursing mother

It is undesirable to eat beets in their raw form, since this is a biologically active product. Nursing mom is not allowed to drink beetroot fresh. Vegetable is contraindicated in women suffering from urolithiasis. With impaired metabolism and diabetes, you need to limit the use of beets.

















To preserve all the useful properties of beets, it is advisable to cook it for a couple or in a slow cooker. You can also stew it with vegetables in olive or vegetable oil. You can not add spicy seasonings and spices, mustard. Cook the beets preferably no more than 1 hour and without adding salt. You need to do this in the peel and without cutting the tails, because this way all the nutritional properties of the root crop will be preserved.





Beets are rich in iron, which makes it very valuable for anemia and a weakened body. Beetroot also includes fiber, betaine and organic acids. The vegetable is also useful for the thyroid gland, as it contains a lot of iodine.
