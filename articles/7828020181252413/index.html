Syrup preparation





If possible, you can make Jerusalem artichoke syrup at home. To prepare a natural medicine you will need:





  • earth pear tubers - 2-3 pcs.;
  • fructose - 250 g.

Before starting to prepare the syrup, you need to wash the tubers, dry them and remove the peel. Then you need to grind them on a grater. Add fructose to grated ground pear and mix thoroughly.





Let the gruel stand for about 2 hours, then transfer it to the refrigerator for 2 days. Then squeeze the raw materials and boil the liquid for 10 minutes. Pour into a glass jar and store in a cool, dark place.





The whole cooking process takes two days. Self-preparation of this syrup for the purpose of its use in diabetes is not recommended. If the tubers are overripe, the syrup will be high in sugar.
