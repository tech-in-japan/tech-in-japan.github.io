The formation of cholesterol plaques on the vessels leads to diseases such as bowel necrosis, stroke, heart attack and thrombophlebitis of the extremities. Causes of vascular contamination





Blood clots are cholesterol plaques that settle on the inner walls and lead to a gradual narrowing of the lumen in the vessels. This extremely dangerous phenomenon provokes the occurrence of atherosclerosis and thrombosis. Determine their presence using ultrasound or MRI. The main reasons why cholesterol accumulates are as follows:





  • The main and most obvious is malnutrition. The use of fried and fatty foods, alcoholic beverages, smoked meats and pickles disrupts metabolic processes and interferes with the normal absorption of cholesterol.
  • Smoking and alcohol affect the quality and permeability of blood vessels, making them thin and fragile. Because of this, their narrowing occurs, and given already deferred cholesterol, the lumen becomes barely noticeable.
  • A sedentary lifestyle disrupts the metabolism and leads to obesity. Excess weight negatively affects all organs, without exception.
  • Scientists have long noticed that stress and prolonged depression can cause crushing damage to human immunity and cause a lot of the most serious diseases.

If plaques have already arisen, they should be disposed of. The medical institution carries out procedures such as plasmapheresis and cryoapheresis. Like any other disease, atherosclerosis is easier to prevent than to get rid of it later. Therefore, it is extremely important to consider all of the above tips and apply them in everyday life.
