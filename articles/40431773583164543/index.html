Hypoallergenic mixture: types





There are several types of similar products: based on goat milk, soy, protein hydrolysates. But even to these seemingly hypoallergenic mixtures, the baby may experience an undesirable reaction. Therefore, you should not put experiments on the child "if not this, then another", it is better to consult a pediatrician or allergist.













Hypoallergenic goat milk based mixture









Often babies have an allergic reaction to cow's milk or soy. In this case, mixtures made from goat milk are recommended . Its proteins and fats are more easily absorbed by the child's body and do not provoke allergic reactions. In addition, baby milk formula in goat milk is also suitable for healthy children. You can also try using a fresh product.





Soy-based hypoallergenic mixture





For children who suffer from lactose deficiency, genetic diseases, or they have intolerance to cow protein, soy mixes are recommended. They do not contain lactose. But with this product you need to be extremely careful. Now it often began to happen that it is on soy protein in children that an allergic reaction occurs. Before starting feeding with this mixture, you should talk with your doctor.





Hypoallergenic mixture based on protein hydrolysates





In severe forms of intolerance to cow's milk and soy, nutrition based on protein hydrolysates is recommended. In addition, this kind of mixture is used when the baby's gastrointestinal tract is disturbed. Nutrition from protein hydrolysates is prescribed for preventive purposes and for mild allergies.
