Can the liver be breastfed: features of use and recommendations

The best cooking methods for HS

The liver is prepared in different ways, and it can be used as a complement to different dishes. In addition, the ingredient is quickly prepared, and this is important when the child is very small.

Before proceeding with the preparation, it is better to first soak the liver in a small amount of milk, this allows you to remove the bitterness, especially if beef or pork offal is used.
