Walking stick that measures pressure



Fujitsu concept .







In addition to biometric data such as temperature, pulse, pressure, which would be displayed on the LCD, it was supposed to equip the cane with position sensors and GPS.







On the alarm button, also integrated into the device, the location data was sent to the servers of the special center, which could provide timely assistance.



GPS could be used not only as a tracking location, but also for navigation: the Fujitsu cane would have (and maybe someday) be able to guide the owner, notifying about turns and other maneuvers.



A much simpler solution is a cane with a built-in flashlight, which is designed for use in low-light conditions. It looks logical and understandable, however, it is likely that it will not be difficult to make it on your own.
