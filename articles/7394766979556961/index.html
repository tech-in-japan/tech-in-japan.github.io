What should be tea for weight loss? Useful and harmful additives in tea

Slimming tea is a very attractive remedy for many overweight people. It would seem that it could be easier - drink tea and lose weight, while doing nothing!









However, it should immediately be determined that those teas that are sold in stores or pharmacies as a means to reduce weight often contain laxatives and diuretics. This explains their "slimming" effect, which boils down to the removal of fluid from the body.





With an intestinal upset caused by the use of tea with laxative herbs and fruits, all useful substances (vitamins, minerals) cease to be absorbed from food. A long-term intake of such a drink contributes to a decrease in immunity, leaching of calcium salts from the body, disruption of the kidneys and digestive system.





How to choose slimming tea?





To choose a quality and healthy tea, try to buy a certified product, on the packaging of which its composition is indicated. Real slimming tea, as a rule, consists of green varieties of tea with various herbal supplements, does not have GMOs or chemical components, the ingredients of the drink are carefully selected.









If buckthorn bark, cassia leaves or senna leaves are present in the tea you are offering , you buy a laxative. The benefits of this tea will be minimal.
