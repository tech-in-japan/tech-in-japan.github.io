Mini arcade games. Without advertising. Study

Hello!



We would like to start the development of a number of mini-arcade games for mobile devices, but for this we need to conduct research.



For starters, what is meant by the word "mini-arcade"? In this concept, we put the simplest, like Tetris, games (usually with endless progress). There will be no accounts, the game will be played offline, in general, so that you can get a phone and play anywhere in the world. Such a game will have attractive, but primitive graphics, so as not to create a heap on your smartphone or tablet. We would like to make them so that they do not slow down even on older devices, unlike many of today's games. And finally, we would like to remove annoying, spoiling interface advertising. If you want to leave the cubes, why do you need a new building for one and a half million?



We invite, we ask you to take a short survey, which would help us a lot. Thank!
