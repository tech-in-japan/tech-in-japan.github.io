What are these supplements, why take them?



As the author of the book says, nutritional supplements are micronutrients that are sufficient to receive in very small doses.







Their weight is measured:

In micrograms (designation Œºg) - one million grams. That is, if the dose is 1000 ¬µg., Then it is only 0.001 grams. One thousand micrograms equals one thousandth of a gram!

In milligrams (mg designation) - one thousand grams. That is, if the dose is 100 mg., Then it is 0.1 grams. One tenth of a gram.







Supplements are taken to obtain the necessary for the brain of substances that are not enough in our daily food. Or, which are quickly consumed by our body due to unfavorable external environment - pollution, lack of sun, etc.







When some substances are not enough, then, gradually, the brain begins to work worse + there may be some irreversible changes (old age comes, so to speak, and we start eating vegetables). Therefore, to prevent this, we take these supplements.
