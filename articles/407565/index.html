Bounty for ICO. How to make money without spending a penny

Each project that launches its ICO creates a bounty program. This is a reward program for helping to promote and promote ICO . Services are paid in project tokens, which you can either immediately sell or hold in the hope of a higher cost in the future. And most importantly, many of the Bounty campaigns will not require any large labor costs from you, you will not have to run around in search of investors for commissions, everything is much simpler.



Let's consider what Bounty campaigns are in most projects and how much money can be earned from this.

What is a signature







On the forum, each user can set a text message in his profile, which will be automatically added to each of his posts on the forum.



ICO campaigns buy this place from the user and use it as an advertisement. Those. the user simply communicates on the forum, writes various comments and at the same time advertises some ICO.



How it works



There are restrictions on the installed signature on the forum, depending on your rank. For example, if you only registered on the forum, then you will not be able to use bb tags in your signature, and the signature length will be limited to 50 characters. How much can you earn



Income in different Bounty is completely different, but on average, a member with the rank of ‚ÄúMember‚Äù (Activity: 60, Flow time: 2.5 months) receives about $ 500 a month from participation in the subscription campaign.



Most ICOs require 10 messages per week from participants. Suppose you want to write meaningful messages and you spend 10 minutes for each message, which means that 10 messages will take you 100 minutes a week or 7 hours a month. The most amazing thing is that you can use as many accounts as you like, it is not even prohibited by forum rules . You can register dozens of accounts and write each with 10 messages per week and receive $ 70 per hour.



2. Social networks



To participate in this type of Bounty you need accounts on social networks - twitter and facebook. Accounts should look like real ones with a lot of live subscribers and your real activity. Of course, this should ideally be the case, but most participants create fake pages with friends and followers. It is for this reason that we did not include social networks in our Bounty campaigns. Nevertheless, Bounty in social networks remains one of the most popular campaign after signatures.



How it works



All that will be required of you is to subscribe to the official ICO pages on social networks, like each entry and make it repost or retweet. What could be easier? You can participate in parallel with a large number of ICOs with one account, so you will not even need to create several accounts.



How much can you earn



Since participation in such campaigns is much simpler and does not require practically any efforts from the participant, then there are much more participants in them than in subscription campaigns. A large number of participants greatly erodes the overall budget and a participant can count on income in the region of $ 20 per month. But you can simultaneously participate in 50 campaigns, in this case, your income will be already $ 1000 per month.



3. Translation into local language



Easy ways are over, then you will have to work further. The third popular type of Bounty is a translation from English to local.



What is usually translated:



  • Announcement on the forum
  • ICO website
  • White paper


The translators in the Bounty campaign of the ICO use the least of all compared to the other participants (signatures, social networks), so the total budget is divided into fewer people and the income of one translator for a complete translation set can be $ 500 or more.



Where to find bounty?



As already stated above, all projects publish their ICO announcements on the bitcointalk forum without fail. They also publish their Bounty programs there. If you want to participate in a subscription campaign or a social networking campaign, then you just have to look through the Russian section of the forum and English . The Russian section is one of the biggest after the English and even more Chinese.



We look at the topic headers and look for the prefix [BOUNTY] or [ANN]. If the topic title starts with [BOUNTY], then this topic is directly devoted to Bounty, but often projects combine their announcement with Bounty and publish it in the main topic with the [ANN] prefix.



Risks







Up to this point, everything seemed just fine, right? Those. on the one hand, you did not invest money, but spent your time and did work that may not be paid.
