Game Design Lecture Evening

October 2 (Wednesday) 2019 at the Higher School of Business Informatics of the NRU HSE a free Lecture Evening on game design will be held.







From 19:00 to 22:00 our teachers, experts in the gaming industry, will share their experience with the guests of the event.



Event program



1) The practice of game design: how to effectively solve applied problems - Konstantin Sakhnov, managing partner of DatCroft Games.



  • List of typical tasks of a game designer;
  • Effective work with narrative;
  • Exact mathematics and balance;
  • Quick edits and testing on the fly;
  • Typical analytics tasks.


2) How mechanics tell stories - Sergey Gimelreich, game designer, game producer.



  • We are taking the first steps towards understanding the language of game mechanics as a tool for narrative design. Already, many game designers recognize that beyond the simplest interaction with game objects lies something more - a new dimension that can change and enhance the transfer of psycho-emotional experience to the player.


Start of registration: 18:30



Video recording of lectures will be posted on our channel . Subscribe to receive a notification.



Location: st. Trifonovskaya, d. 57, p. 1 (metro station Rizhskaya), conference hall (2nd floor).



Age limit 18+. Please bring an identification document with you.
