Is instant noodles harmful?





Instant noodles are certainly very popular these days. The reason for this success is the ability to quickly and tasty enough to eat in almost any conditions, if only there was boiling water, which is why it is used on trips, especially by train, taken on hikes and on vacation. Such a product is also indispensable at home or at work, when there is no opportunity or time to cook full-fledged food. In short, the unambiguous positive quality of such noodles is the speed and ease of preparation.





However, most people who eat this product have a very logical question: is instant noodles harmful to our health? After all, rumors have long been circulating that eating it is not only not useful, but also dangerous.





In order to give an answer to this question, you should first understand what exactly instant noodles are, what it consists of and how it is made.





So, the manufacture of this product usually occurs by dehydrogenation. It is the removal by drying at high temperatures of all moisture contained in food. Rapid swelling of noodles in hot water is carried out not only because of this, but also because it contains rather large amounts of emulsifiers, thickeners and starch.





Seasoning of standard noodles consists of salt, sodium glutamate, as well as powder made from soy sauce, and various flavors, the task of which is to imitate the taste of beef, shrimp, chicken, etc. Of all this composition, sodium glutamate is the most harmful, which, as has long been known, acts on taste buds and is addictive, in this case, to the taste of noodles. Frequent ingestion of it threatens digestive system disorders, and sometimes it can even cause serious diseases of the gastrointestinal tract like gastritis or ulcers.
