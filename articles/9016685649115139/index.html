Redecorating. Effective Acne Diet

Oh, these acne! They can appear at the most inopportune moment! The life of more than one teenager was poisoned by these red rashes on the face and body, and for adults this phenomenon causes a lot of trouble.





Acne can be the result of malnutrition, so they appear not depending on age, but on the quality of human nutrition. In the case of uncontrolled consumption of a wide variety of products, not only acne can appear, but also such ailments as gastritis and dysbiosis. These diseases, as a rule, are accompanied by deterioration of the skin condition, the appearance of black spots and various kinds of rashes. When solving this problem, comprehensive treatment should be carried out. It is important to pay attention not only to the external manifestations of the disease, but also not to forget that the acne diet is no less effective than local antibiotic therapy.





Diet against acne. General recommendations





Do not eat less than four hours before bedtime. It is recommended to eat food at intervals of three to four hours. Snacks are undesirable. It is important to follow a diet, eat in a relaxed atmosphere, chewing food well. The normal functioning of the stomach is impeded by reading, watching TV shows or movies.





What foods restrict acne diet?





It is not recommended to consume (or you need to reduce consumption to a minimum) the following:





- fatty foods cooked in large quantities of vegetable oil, pork, beef, fatty fish.





- spicy seasonings, pickles, various marinades, chili peppers.









- pastries and sweets (cakes, pastries, chocolate, cookies).





- fast food (hamburgers, cheeseburgers, french fries) and canned food.





- highly carbonated drinks, coffee, black tea (strong), energy drinks and alcohol.





With abuse of the above products, increased androgen activity may occur and, accordingly, sebum secretion will increase. Another problem may be irritation of the gastric mucosa, and invariably following it, acne.





What is an acne diet?





A diet aimed at combating acne should be saturated with foods rich in vitamins and beneficial microorganisms. Such food helps to remove toxins, toxins and harmful bacteria from the body. In addition, immunity increases.





Fresh fruits and vegetables are the best acne products. Bananas make up for vitamin C, B6 and PP deficiencies, peaches contain the beta-carotene and vitamins B, C and A we need, and apples are rich in potassium, calcium, magnesium and pectin. Vegetables can be consumed both fresh and boiled. They also contain many vitamins and minerals that are good for your health.





The body also needs:





low-fat fermented milk products (cottage cheese, kefir, milk)





- cheese





- eggs (however, it is not recommended to abuse them)





- seafood (give preference to low-fat fish, seaweed)





- dried fruits (dried apricots, raisins, prunes)





- a large amount of liquid (herbal infusions, freshly squeezed juices, green tea).





Digestive problems help overcome legumes, nuts, bran, rice and fiber-rich oats.





Studies aimed at confirming that the acne diet does exist have proved that following a special diet for three months allows you to get rid of half of acne. The recommended diet was followed by the first group of adolescents, while participants in the experiment from the second group were allowed to ignore what they were eating. As a result, they observed either a deterioration of the skin condition or the absence of any changes.





The result of the study was the conclusion that an effective acne diet implies the exclusion of high-carb foods from the diet.
