Types of Tokens



  • Access Tokens (JWTs) are tokens that can be used to access protected resources. They are short-lived , but reusable . They may contain additional information, such as lifetime, or IP

  • Refresh Token (RT) - these tokens perform only one specific task - obtaining a new access token. And this time you can‚Äôt do without an authorization server. They are long-lived , but disposable .
