How to sprout wheat at home

You were inspired by the useful qualities of the product and you have no contraindications to its use? Then it's time to learn how to sprout wheat at home. Sprouted grain is prepared in several ways. The most popular option is:





  1. First you need to separate the good grains from the bad ones. Dark, not whole copies, do not hesitate to send them to the bin.
  2. Place the remaining grains in a large container and add water. Remove popped up grains too, as they have no value and will not germinate. Then rinse well under the tap, and at the end rinse with boiled water.
  3. Arrange the washed grains on a saucer, pour a little filtered water so that the top layer is not completely immersed in water. Try not to make a layer more than 2 cm.
  4. Top with gauze folded several times. You can cover with a plate, just leave a small hole for air.
  5. Put in a warm place for 1.5-2 days. During this time, make sure that the grains do not dry out and periodically add water.
  6. Sprouted wheat will be ready when white sprouts hatch. To get the maximum effect from the healing product, the sprouts should not exceed 2 mm.




Try not to pour a lot of water during germination. Grains should be in a warm, humid environment, but if there is too much water, they will begin to deteriorate or will not germinate. To avoid this, there is another way to make sprouted wheat:





  1. After you have sorted and washed the product, place it in a jar (half-liter or liter).
  2. Pour a little clean water into the jar and drain it so that the grains remain on the bottom and walls of the container.
  3. Place the jar with the neck on the saucer and place the entire structure in any place at room temperature (21-22 ¬∞ C).

With this method, wheat germination occurs more intensively, and there is no risk of rotting of the grains.









How to store sprouted wheat

To get all the benefits from the product, it is better to use it immediately when the sprouts are ready. During long-term storage, and even more so during heat treatment or freezing, all useful properties are lost. Therefore, it is recommended not to store sprouted wheat for food for more than a day. And for this period, place the grains in a container or jar with a lid and put in the refrigerator.

Sprouted Wheat Vegetable Salad Recipe

This vitamin salad is not only tasty and healthy, but also very nutritious. It is quite possible to replace lunch with it. To make a salad you will need:





  • wheat germ - 2-3 tablespoons;
  • cucumber - 2-3 pcs. medium size;
  • bell pepper - 1-2 pcs.;
  • avocado - 1 pc.;
  • parsley - 1 bunch;
  • lemon juice - 1 tablespoon;
  • olive or other favorite oil, preferably unrefined - 1 tablespoon;
  • salt to taste.

Cooking process:





  1. Cut the cucumber into circles, and then divide each circle into 4 parts.
  2. Dice the bell pepper.
  3. In a cup, combine chopped vegetables with wheat sprouts.
  4. Next, prepare the sauce. We clean and release the avocado from the bone.
  5. Put a little chopped avocado, herbs, lemon juice, oil and salt in a blender.
  6. Grind to a homogeneous consistency. Dress the salad.




Fruit Salad with Sprouts Recipe

This simple vitamin salad is great for breakfast. You will need:





  • wheat seedlings - 1-2 tablespoons;
  • apple - 1 pc.;
  • prunes - 7-9 pcs.;
  • water - 1 cup.

Prunes need to be soaked for several hours, preferably at night. In the morning, drain the water and remove the bones, if any. Cut prunes or chop in a blender. Grate the apple on a medium grater. Mix sprouts, apple and prunes in a cup. Ready salad can be sprinkled with lemon juice.
