How many calories are in fresh chicken breast













So, let's calculate and determine the caloric content of this offal. 100 grams of chicken breast (without skin) contains carbohydrates - 0.02 grams, protein - 21.8 grams, fat - 3.2 grams. Knowing these parameters, we can determine the caloric content of this product. Steamed chicken breast pockets





What low-calorie foods can you make with chicken breast? The very first thing that comes to mind is to cook this product for a couple. The chicken breast in a double boiler is prepared very simply. It is enough to pre-soak it in a sauce made from kefir, aromatic herbs and salt. After an hour's stay in it, the meat will become soft and soft. Now you need to let the breasts flow around (place them on the wire rack). Meanwhile, cut into cubes potatoes, bell peppers, tomatoes. Salt the vegetables, add cream to them (may, but not desirable, mayonnaise). Make cuts in the breasts so that you get "pockets" that you fill with vegetables. Put all this miracle in the capacity of a double boiler and cook until fully cooked (25-30 minutes). And one more tip: those who want all the juice from vegetables and chicken breasts to not leak out can wrap each breast in foil and cook in it. Only in this case it is necessary to take into account that the cooking time will increase by 10-15 minutes.





For those who are interested in how many calories are in steamed chicken breast with vegetables, let's say that the calorie content is almost minimal and equal to 164 kcal per hundred grams of the finished dish.





















Baked Parmesan Chicken Breast





Chicken Parmesan is a simple but very tasty dish. In order for you to get a real baked Parmesan chicken breast, it is necessary to mix dry and ‚Äúwet‚Äù ingredients in two containers separately. In one bowl, mix Dijon mustard with apple juice, 2 cloves of garlic passed through a garlic press and a couple tablespoons of melted butter. The mixture should be like a pancake dough.
