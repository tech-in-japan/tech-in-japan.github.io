Voxgun - a service for creating professional video content without any extra effort





Hello! Today I wanted to tell you about a new interesting project - a service for creating video for the Voxgun business. This tool allows you to create video content with professional voice acting and animation in less than 10 minutes and without specialized skills.



How it works



Using the video editor, the user can select pre-animated scenes and recorded voice fragments voiced by professional speakers. From these elements you can assemble a video in less than 10 minutes.







The service is useful for creating promotional videos for products and services, tutorials for various applications, content for social networks and other tasks.







What is important, not only audio fragments from the service library are available to users, they can also order voice acting of unique phrases in any language. It takes less than 24 hours to record the original soundtrack.



Automation



Using the Voxgun API, you can automatically generate video based on the parameters passed. For example, there is an example when a car dealer using a service automatically generates video descriptions of cars sold:







Cost



The service works according to the Pay As You Go model. Users pay only for the generation of high-resolution video with the original soundtrack. The final price of the video depends on the number of animated scenes and the selected voice acting. On average, videos without voice-over text cost $ 2, with text voiced by the announcer - about $ 50.







The cost is calculated in real time - the user always sees how much he will need to pay. There are no subscriptions on Voxgun, video storage and access to the API are free.
