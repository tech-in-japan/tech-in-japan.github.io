What fruits can I eat with gastritis?





Proper nutrition is important for any ailments, and for diseases of the digestive system it should be followed with great care. With gastritis, it is useful to eat various vegetables and fruits. In this case, it is necessary to remember the existing restrictions. In the case when gastritis is characterized by an increased level of acidity of the stomach, it is not recommended to eat grapes and peaches, melons and lemons. Grapefruits are also prohibited. If acidity is lowered, then these fruits, except melons and grapes, are allowed. With gastritis, nutritionists recommend eating strawberries, cherries, pears, watermelons and apples.
