Fasting days on kefir and cottage cheese: menu, features, results and reviews

Fasting days are considered good for health and maintaining a slim figure. At its core, these are mini diets that are recommended to be followed within 12 or 24 hours. What you need to know in order not to harm your own health with such dietary restrictions? We will try to figure out how to spend the fasting days correctly on kefir and cottage cheese.

















What are the benefits of kefir fasting days?

Not all people are able to withstand long diets. Adhering to a certain diet for one day is much easier than controlling yourself for a week. Fasting days help the digestive system relax and "reboot." One-day diets are recommended no more than once a week. Fasting days on kefir and cottage cheese are often praised for the instant weight loss effect. Indeed, if you follow the correct daily menu, you can lose up to 2 kilograms in weight. The thing is that with the unloading kefir bottom, the body is completely cleansed. This dairy product has a positive effect on the digestive system. Kefir is rich in vitamins A, B and D, as well as calcium. The use of this product has a positive effect on the condition of the skin, hair and nails. Yogurt is also useful as a means to combat edema.





















Contraindications kefir-curd diet

Any food system has limitations and contraindications. Kefir mono-diets should not last more than three days. With their regular use, the option of fasting days is optimal . Cleaning the body with kefir is not recommended for diseases such as ulcers, gastritis and kidney disease. At the same time, fasting days on kefir and cottage cheese can be carried out by people with diabetes. This disease is not a contraindication for such a mini-diet. Any fasting days are not recommended when immunity is weakened and increased mental and / or physical stress. Refuse from kefir diet should be with individual intolerance to this drink. If during sour milk days you constantly feel hunger and discomfort, it also makes sense to try a different nutrition system.





Kefir for weight loss and body cleansing: product selection rules

Kefir-curd diet will surely appeal to all lovers of food. During this fasting day, you will not experience hunger. Be careful, not all dairy products are equally healthy! When choosing kefir and cottage cheese in a store, start by studying the composition. Natural dairy products cannot be stored for longer than 5 days. For weight loss, kefir is suitable , the fat content of which is not more than 2%. The product must be made using fermented sour milk bacteria. By a similar principle, you should choose and cottage cheese for the diet menu. Natural origin, low percentage of fat content and lack of artificial additives.

























Basic rules for fasting day

With any mono-diet, it is important to observe the drinking regime. Drink plenty of clean water. This will help compensate for the loss of fluid and start the process of cleansing the body. From the daily diet, salt and sugar must be completely eliminated. You can drink 1-3 cups of coffee or tea during the fasting day, but it is not recommended to sweeten drinks. How much kefir can I drink? If your goal is to cleanse the body and lose weight, use no more than 2 liters of kefir. Fasting days on kefir and cottage cheese also require control of the energy value of the diet. The daily norm for such a diet is no more than 700 kilocalories. You can get the maximum benefit by preparing for a one-day diet the day before. On the day before unloading, reduce fat intake and give up junk food. Also, after a one-day diet, start eating regular dishes gradually.

















Standard menu option

The easiest option for unloading kefir-curd day is the unlimited use of selected products. If your goal is not only to cleanse the body, but also to lose weight, calculate the daily amount of food by its calorie content. Divide the daily diet into 5-6 meals. How to spend a fasting day on kefir or cottage cheese correctly? You can sit down at the table as soon as you feel hungry. It is advisable to eat slowly and in small portions. Your main goal is to eat only cottage cheese and kefir all day, stopping at a measured amount of food. There are alternatives to this one-day diet. One of them involves eating in a certain pattern, while the other even allows you to cook simple dishes.

Diet recipes

Not everyone likes the taste of dairy products. If you are not mentally prepared to eat cottage cheese and kefir all day, we offer you some simple recipes. A great addition to the diet menu are bran. Choose wheat, oat or rye to your taste. For each serving of cottage cheese, simply add a tablespoon of certain bran. As a result, you will get a useful product that perfectly cleanses the body and makes up for the lack of slow carbohydrates. If you decide to spend a fasting day on cottage cheese and kefir, the menu can be varied by adding honey. With it, you can improve the taste of a sour-milk drink. Add one teaspoon of honey to one cup of kefir. Fresh berries will be a great addition to summer fasting days. They can be added to cottage cheese and kefir. For a winter one-day diet, try using your favorite dried fruit as a flavoring. A day is allowed to eat no more than 50 grams. Divide this amount of dried fruits into equal parts and add to the cottage cheese.
