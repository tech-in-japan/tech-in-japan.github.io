This is not just a tracker that measures steps, pulse and so on. No, it allows you to find out how the current activity of the user is useful for his body. After all, you can wander for hours, and it will bring a minimum for the benefit of a trained person.

This indicator is derived from the results of many years of research work of Norwegian experts, which is called the HUNT Study . This indicator is a comprehensive characteristic of the assessment of the impact of physical exertion on the cardiovascular system and the likelihood of heart disease.
