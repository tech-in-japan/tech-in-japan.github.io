How to become a cyber lawyer

The most high-profile bills of recent years are related to the regulation of Internet space: the Spring package, the so-called bill on sovereign runet. Now the digital environment is the subject of close attention of lawmakers and law enforcement. Russian legislation regulating activities on the Internet is only being created and tested in practice. They began to actively monitor Runet from 2012, when Roskomnadzor received its first authority to supervise web resources.



There are norms and requirements that must be met by the activities on the Internet of companies and ordinary citizens.



Clients of lawyers have questions in many areas related to the Internet: what is considered intellectual property, how to handle personal data, what you need to know about the rules for distributing content on the network, and how best to advertise on the Internet. These are pressing issues that relate to the activities of many companies. Not all lawyers have fully mastered digital law, therefore, those who are versed in issues of digital law are more in demand today.
