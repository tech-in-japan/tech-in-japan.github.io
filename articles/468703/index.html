So, you did not go to the state sector, avoid small studios oriented to the domestic market and work in a company with good prospects. How to increase your income?



It is generally accepted that revenue in IT depends on time and loyalty:

Income = Skills * Time * Loyalty
Intuitively it seems that you need to work well. Do not be late for the daily. Understand your part of the project. Set long-term goals. Overtime, if necessary. Do not conflict. Strive to become indispensable.



But over 12 years in IT, I have seen many examples of how this does not work . How technically strong guys earn less than the market. They are waiting for the next review, hoping that this time they will finally be appreciated.



But this does not happen. We have to save on muffins and blueberry smoothies again. And the purchase plan for the Tesla Model 3 is shifting again.



In fact, your income depends on other parameters:

Income = Skills * Spoken English * Understanding the business * Ability to negotiate
