5G - no longer mythical speeds



5G is being talked about more and more often - test launches of the fifth generation networks took place in professional forums, in mobile operators in the leading countries of the world (including Russia). Already, you can safely say that 5G will become a new telecommunications standard, and until this time is within reach. Faster internet connection and improved bandwidth will significantly increase content consumption: users will be able to comfortably drive 3D photos and 4K video over the network. At the same time, 5G is predicted to be more careful with device batteries - well, in simple terms. But the main advantage of 5G is the ability to communicate devices inside the hubs of the Internet of things. Experts expect tangible IoT value growth until 2022.
