Developer salaries in Armenia

Salaries in the IT sector of Armenia are not amenable to the general salary conditions established in the country: the order of numbers is much higher than the average salary, salaries are comparable, if not with Moscow, then regional in Russia, with salaries in the tech sector of Belarus.



We calculated the average salaries of developers in Armenia, described the reasons for these figures, how they compare with salaries in Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, Germany. And how much does the senior developer have on average left with salary after deduction of taxes, rent and basic costs in each country.

What is the difference between the financial climate of Armenia?



  1. The salary on hand is always discussed.

    In Armenia, some IT companies are taxed on a preferential system, such as start-ups and foreign companies that have opened an office in Armenia. The total tax burden on the wage fund varies from 10 to 30 percent. The trend is to immediately discuss salary minus all taxes.
  2. No one considers or discusses the annual salary, as is done, for example, in Europe or the United States.
  3. In general, salary is not public information. Units indicate salaries on job boards or publicly negotiate a fork.
  4. The scatter between the salary of a junior and an already experienced specialist is huge compared to the scatter of salaries in Europe or the States. The average salary for a junior position is $ 580, senior - almost 6 times more.
  5. The Armenian tech sector, in comparison with other countries, is a rather small market. The percentage of developers relative to the general population is high, but this is still not enough to close all open positions. Therefore, sometimes companies are repelled by a person and his skills, rather than the role open to the company. Accordingly, the salary is discussed individually, and not in accordance with the internal grade.
  6. In the local IT sector, all salaries are paid in white.
  7. On average, the market salary is often completely covered by money, not an option. Among the companies that offer the option are noise reduction startup krisp , health-tech startup Vineti , the largest developer of VMware virtualization software.
  8. Of the features that do not directly affect the level of salary, but reduce the cost of living, Yerevan is a small city, the location of the office is never discussed during a conversation with a potential employee.
