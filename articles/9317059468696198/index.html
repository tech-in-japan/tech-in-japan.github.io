To begin with, vegetarians are different. True followers of this lifestyle do not eat meat, including fish and chicken. But, there are other types of it. So, those people who eat eggs and dairy products, excluding poultry, fish and meat from their diet, call themselves lacto-ovo-vegetarians.





If you remove eggs from the above list of products, then it will already be lacto-vegetarians. But vegetarians with great pleasure indulge themselves with chicken eggs, but they absolutely will not dairy products.





There are those who have not abandoned fish, but the bird is not included in their diet. They call themselves sand-vegetarians.





Veganism is the most severe form of vegetarianism. Anyone who adheres to this direction in their diet completely excludes dairy products, eggs, honey, gelatin, that is, everything that is somehow connected with the animal world.





Many, having read these lines, will be perplexed at all of the above, because for most people it is completely unclear what vegetarians eat in this case. In fact, the answer to this question is quite simple.





The fact is that in our daily lives we do not attach importance to many products, perceiving meat as our main food. The vegetarian diet is quite saturated.

Believe me, you can live without meat. And it has already been proven by more than one person. Currently, on our planet, ten percent of the total population has preferred a vegetarian lifestyle. And they are right in something.

What do vegetarians eat yet? Well, of course, berries and fruits. Without them, as you know, nowhere. In addition to the fact that they can be consumed with great pleasure in their raw form, the fruit makes wonderful salads, various desserts and much more.

As for dairy products and eggs, they are also related to the vegetarian lifestyle. However, all vegetarians include this kind of food in their diet.
