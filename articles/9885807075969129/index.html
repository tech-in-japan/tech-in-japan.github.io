Chamomile tea. Sunny healing drink

Herbal medicine, or treatment with herbs, is currently very relevant. Already a fairly large number of people have first-hand experience of the effectiveness of this treatment. Even the ancient Greeks, Romans and Egyptians knew that chamomile tea relieves tension and irritation. Modern studies have proved that chamomile flowers perfectly eliminate inflammation and fight various infections. Due to these properties, a wonderful flower drink has long been used for medical purposes, both inside and out.





Chamomile tea has the most beneficial effect on the nervous system, normalizes and soothes, gives an excellent full sleep. Often it is used for diseases of the intestines and stomach, as it has anti-inflammatory properties. This is a great way to lower gas production. It will help to restore the disturbed microflora in the intestines, prevent biliary congestion and become the best prevention from the formation of stones in the gall bladder, namely chamomile tea. It is even assumed that this healing drink has an anticonvulsant effect.





Solar beauty camomile is widely used in herbal medicine and cosmetology. Experts recommend including this healing herb in your home medicine cabinet. If there is such an opportunity, prepare it yourself. Gather flowers in dry, clear weather and dry, hiding from exposure to direct sunlight. Store the collection in a cotton or paper bag. The most useful is chamomile tea, brewed from freshly picked flowers or picking, which is no more than one year old. Therefore, do not harvest daisies too much. It is better next year to replenish stocks of fresh inflorescences.









If the teeth are cut in the kids , there is a pain in the tummy or there is a suspicion of developing colds, this delicious healing drink is used. Young children do not yet know how to gargle on their own with a sore throat, and here comes to the rescue chamomile tea, which added a spoonful of natural honey. He will not only eliminate all the symptoms of the disease, but also like the baby, and also help to sleep peacefully.





During menstruation in women, tea helps reduce spastic pain. In diabetes, it helps normalize blood sugar. For eye inflammation and conjunctivitis, chamomile tea is used to quickly relieve inflammation. Take two tablespoons of dried flowers and fill them with hot water, wait until the resulting infusion has cooled slightly, and strain it. Dip cotton pads in tea, squeeze a little and attach to eyes. You can use this wonderful drink to rejuvenate and improve the appearance of the skin. Make ice cubes from it and wipe the skin with them. The tonic and antibacterial effect of this procedure helps to quickly restore excellent complexion and good cleansing. A delightful natural shade to light hair will be given by regular rinses with chamomile infusion.





Especially often used chamomile tea during pregnancy. This is due to the fact that during this period, the majority of medications are contraindicated. With its help, gargles are performed during colds, baths are made, if gynecological problems have worsened, they are used as lotions against the itching that has arisen. One cup of tea per day will be an excellent sedative, as well as prevent the possibility of constipation.





But still, chamomile tea should be drunk with caution during pregnancy, and the consultation with your doctor will be the best option. It can have a negative effect on the body only in case of a serious overdose, and in order to prevent it, drink no more than two cups of the healing drink per day. In reasonable amounts, neither the fetus, nor the mother, the chamomile is simply not able to do harm.
