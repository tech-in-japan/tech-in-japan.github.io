The announcement of the smartphone Xiaomi Mi Mix Alpha

A few hours ago, the big Xiaomi press conference ended, at which the company introduced its new flagship Mi Mix Alpha. Alfach is implemented in the original concept, in which the display occupies not only the front but also the back panel of the smartphone, as well as its ends. The company itself claims that the screen occupies "180.6% of the front panel area." Whatever it means.



Full coverage is violated only by a ceramic insert under the camera module. This solution allowed us to implement a unique user interface: the data necessary here and now (weather, notifications, service icons, etc.) are displayed on the side face, widgets are displayed on the rear panel. There is no front camera in the device, and the need for it has now disappeared - after all, you can now simply turn the device over and look at the same screen. Brace yourself, a selfie is coming from a 108 megapixel camera!
