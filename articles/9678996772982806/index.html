They have a sweet taste due to fructose and sucrose, which is found in large quantities in dates, fruits and vegetables. There is no place for harmful substances, preservatives, dyes, emulsifiers and stabilizers. Shelf life of snacks













Bite fruit bars , despite their naturalness, have a fairly long shelf life. At a temperature from 0 to + 20C and humidity - 75%, the shelf life of the product is six months. Preservation is ensured by special packaging that protects it from sunlight, air and moisture.
