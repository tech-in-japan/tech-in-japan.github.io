DJI Zenmuse X7: details





The excitement in the style of Apple with intrigue around the presentation ended, it's time to add details from yesterday's post .



The Zenmuse X7 is a compact Super-35 format camera equipped with a built-in stabilizer designed for professional filming with the Inspire 2 drone.



  • Matrix: Super-35
  • Photo: 24 MP
  • Video: 6K CinemaDNG
  • 5.2K Apple ProRes
  • Dynamic range: 14 steps
  • Weight: 631 grams






Matrix







X7 is equipped with Super-35 photomatrix, capable of shooting uncompressed video in 6K resolution at 30 frames / s and 3.9K at 59.94 frames / s. The size of one pixel reaches 3.91 microns, and the diagonal of the sensor is 26.6 mm when recording video. A matrix of this size is more sensitive to light, and the dynamic range in 14 steps is wider than the Zenmuse X5S camera (12.8 steps).

Noise suppression





The X7 SNR is 44 dB, which is 5 dB higher than the X5S noise canceling ability, and the tonality range reaches 9.41 bits.

The focal length varies in the range of 16-50 mm. The maximum resolution reaches 8K. All four lens models are made from lightweight carbon fiber.

Image processing system



Upgrading CineCore to Inspire 2 to CineCore 2.1. It supports video in 6K at 30 frames / s with CinemaDNG and 5.2K at 30 frames / s with Apple ProRes.







DJI Cinema Color System







The system consists of a new D-Log curve and a D-Gamut RGB color space. Whitepaper (PDF)







D-Log operates with 15 dynamic range steps (2 steps wider than the Zenmuse X5S). The X7 stores image details in both dark and light areas, which increases editing capabilities.







D-Gamut captures the entire DCI-P3 color space, traditionally used when filming. D-Gamut is created for shooting video and photos from the drone and covers a larger field of green tones, providing a beautiful gradation of green in the pictures.







In addition, D-Gamut presents skin tone smoothing: tones are distributed to the most appropriate color zones, giving the skin a beautiful natural color. When using 3D-LUT color management technology, you can smoothly change skin tones even with large differences in exposure.



Price, delivery and where to order



Zenmuse X7 in US stores will sell for $ 2,700. The lenses are 16mm, 24mm, and 35mm for $ 1,300, and 50mm - $ 1,200. Full set - for $ 4,300. Ship orders will begin in early November, buy Zenmuse X7 in Russia with an official guarantee.
