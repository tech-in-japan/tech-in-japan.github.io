What is ovulation





To understand how and how to stimulate ovulation, you must be able to determine its onset, to know the definition of this term. This is called the exit from the ovary of the egg into the cavity of the fallopian tube as a result of the rupture of the dominant follicle. It happens that a woman undergoes an anovulatory cycle, which can be repeated for several months in a row. This is due to the fact that the dominant follicle does not mature in the ovaries and the egg does not release for its subsequent fertilization into the uterine cavity. According to studies, about 25% of women face this problem.





Before contacting a doctor, a woman can determine a malfunction in the body at home. To do this, you need to observe how the menstrual cycle goes. For example, some note that the interval between bleeding has increased, and the discharge has become more scarce. These are indirect signs of the presence of hormonal failure and possible problems associated with the onset of ovulation.









Some women experienced in planning women measure basal temperature, noting its value in the graph. Conducting observations over several cycles allows us to talk about the reliability of the data. Do not exclude the changes in the body of a woman, which can be tracked by secretions. The fact of ovulation manifests itself in the form of transparent, viscous discharge similar to egg white.





However, the most obvious way is to conduct folliculometry, which allows you to visually determine the number of mature follicles, as well as the presence of a dominant among them. This study is carried out for several days before the start of the proposed ovulation. It also allows us to establish its absence.

As for the growth and development of the dominant follicle, then ultrasound can determine how this process proceeds in the ovaries.
