TV and software



ZenFone Go TV is equipped with a SONY SMT-EW100 TV tuner, thanks to which you can watch digital TV channels on your smartphone. To do this, simply insert the above-mentioned adapter-antenna and launch the pre-installed SoloTV application.



The full application interface looks like this:







You can display a scrolling list of channels:







There is another display of the list of available channels:







Total available about 40 channels. After a couple of seconds, the interface elements disappear, and only the TV picture remains:







To change the volume, just drag your finger up or down on the screen. Settings menu:















If you start Solo TV without an antenna, then a weak signal appears:







Channels switch quickly, the delay is small, not longer than a couple of seconds. The reception quality is quite satisfactory: in my home the signal of the same cellular networks is not very good, two or three divisions, but the television broadcast on the smartphone went confidently, only if you look closely, you could notice a slight decrease in the frame rate - for example, there was a small lip-tolerance.



The smartphone has a traditional set of ASUS proprietary utilities, including the Mobile Manager:

Performance



The smartphone runs on a Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 quad-core processor , whose clock speed reaches 1.4 GHz. The amount of RAM - 2 GB, internal memory for data storage - 16 GB. Graphic chip - Adreno 305.



By modern standards, the processor and graphics chip are not the most powerful, but they do well with all everyday tasks. The smartphone cheerfully opens up heavy sites, without any problems twists Full HD-movies. Of course, modern 3D games are not his hobby, but I can't say that they crawl on him.



When testing for the duration of the work, the battery capacity dropped from 100% to 20% in 7 hours and 53 minutes.

Conclusion



ZenFone Go TV is a travel smartphone, a gadget on the road. It is not a pity to throw him into a backpack or bag, in order to watch an interesting show, a game of your favorite team or a TV show on a train or bus. You can adapt ZenFone Go TV as a regular low-cost TV in the car, so as not to get bored in traffic jams. At the same time, this "TV" will be a spare phone. Or you can turn on the informative TV show for children and thereby brighten up the child a long boring trip by car. In general, ZenFone Go TV is a TV in your pocket. The main thing, do not forget about the antenna.
