Calorie content of watermelon and its unique properties

Watermelon is everyone's favorite berry. It contains many useful substances, fiber and delicious, aromatic juice, which puts it in the forefront of the summer diet. And the calorie content of watermelon is only 35 kcal per 100 grams. At the same time, it consists of 80% pure carbohydrates - easily digestible fructose. This berry contains a small amount of glucose and sucrose, but these doses are quite acceptable for use by pregnant and lactating women, diabetics and people with obesity.





It is believed that watermelon contains few vitamins. Of course, if you eat only watermelons, then you can forget about the vitamin balance. But in the pulp of watermelon contains B and B2, pectins, beta-carotene, iron, sodium, calcium and phosphorus. In addition, it is a source of trace elements such as magnesium and potassium.





Potassium is responsible for regulating the water balance in the human body and contributes to the normalization of cardiovascular activity. Magnesium is necessary for the muscular system, including bone and cardiac tissues.





The watermelon contains useful fiber that cleanses the intestinal wall and positively affects the functioning of the gastrointestinal tract. Watermelon dietary fiber lowers blood cholesterol, which is very important for people who are overweight.

Watermelon "diets" are contraindicated for people suffering from diseases of the gastrointestinal tract and kidneys, as well as those who have recently undergone abdominal surgery, for example, cesarean section or removal of the appendix.





The unique properties of watermelon make it easy to remove excess salt from the body, relieve swelling in the presence of vascular and heart diseases, relieve fever, improve water-salt metabolism and even cure the chronic form of gastritis, which is greatly facilitated by its low calorie content.





Watermelon should not be mixed with other foods. You can use it exclusively on an empty stomach, since it is not absorbed, but simply passes on, immediately into the intestines. Low calorie watermelon allows you to add it to various dietary salads and desserts. From the pulp you can make delicious juices, smoothies, fresh juices and even watermelon honey. Moreover, you can even eat watermelon peel, because it makes a delicious jam.

Watermelons can be consumed exclusively during their ripening, as long as they have the necessary beneficial properties. This is the period of late summer and early autumn.
