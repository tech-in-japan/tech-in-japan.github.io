Design and ergonomics



The smartphone comes in a compact red and white box. Inside, everything is modest: a 5V / 1A power supply unit, a USB cable, a clip to remove the tray.











Externally, the smartphone can be called partly impersonal, its appearance is fully consistent with the budget positioning. Neat round corners and stripes on the back panel create a strong association with the phone you know yourself.







The case of Xiaomi Redmi Note 5A can pretend to be metallic, but in fact it is still plastic, which the manufacturer casually mentions on its website .



The frames around the display of the smartphone are small, the device is convenient to intercept when using with one hand.



The buttons are placed on the right side, are clearly distinguishable blindly, have a short stroke. The left side is empty, there is only a built-in tray for a pair of SIM-cards and a memory card. Finally, we have three full-fledged sockets and no longer have to choose between the second sim card and the memory expansion. This moment immediately makes senseless overpayment for the version of Xiaomi Redmi Note 5A with 32 GB of memory. It would be wiser to increase the volume of the card.











On the top edge, besides the second microphone and 3.5 mm jack, an infrared port was found, which is especially nice given the budget of the device. He helps me manage a pair of TVs at home and office air conditioning.











At the bottom, under the two arrays, a conversational microphone and a multimedia speaker are hidden. The latter is quite loud and does not suffer from wheezing at high volume levels. But the microphone in my copy is very pumped up. The interlocutors complain that they can hear me very badly, but my voice is like a bucket. Whether Xiaomi was greedy with a microphone, or if I got an unsuccessful device.



There is no fingerprint scanner in my version. To overpay for it or not is a good question. If you increase the budgets for the purchase of a smartphone, I would look in the direction of a full line - without an index "A" - with a more advanced hardware.







The buttons below the display do not have the backlight, but after a week of habituation, there are no problems blindly. The smartphone is available in three colors: gray, gold and pink.



Screen



Despite the fact that Xiaomi Redmi Note 5A received a display only with HD resolution, I do not leave the feeling that it makes up the lion's share of the unit's prime cost. The 5.5-inch IPS matrix has wide viewing angles, customizable color rendering and, subjectively, good brightness indicators. The effect of graininess or a fuzzy picture is not.







During the day, the screen does not fade in the sun, and at night the sensor intelligently lowers the brightness to a value that is comfortable for the eyes. The display is covered with a protective glass with a good oleophobic coating. Fingerprints on the glass almost do not remain.



Cameras



The smartphone Xiaomi Redmi Note 5A received a main camera of 13 megapixels, as well as a frontal selfie module of 5 megapixels.



The numbers in the resolution of the smartphone cameras are just numbers with a pinch of marketing. The real quality of the pictures leaves much to be desired. The smartphone can be well photographed by day on the street. Autofocus is not the fastest, so the device will not forgive the hand shake during shooting.

Autonomy



Another unobvious bonus of the relatively low resolution of the display and low-power processor is that the smartphone runs for a long time on one battery charge.



In the PC Mark test (simulates different scenarios of smartphone operation), the average screen brightness of the Xiaomi Redmi Note 5A lasted a little longer than 8 hours. And this is quite a real 1.5-2 days of active exploitation.







Non-removable battery with a capacity of 3080 mAh was very tenacious. True for this had to pay a little. From the complete power supply for 5V - 1A, the smartphone charges for more than three hours. I think that buying a more powerful unit will significantly speed up the process.



findings
