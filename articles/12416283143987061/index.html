Contraindications

Herbalife weight loss products are not recommended for use during planning and during pregnancy, during the recovery period after childbirth. After breastfeeding, you can start taking medications.









Herbalife products containing caffeine are not recommended for use with existing problems with pressure, heart and blood vessels. It is also undesirable to use supplements for people suffering from insomnia, with sleep disorders and problems with the nervous system.





Cocktails are not recommended for acute lactose intolerance, as this can affect the onset of symptoms of flatulence and diarrhea.









No need to add cocktails to the diet for people suffering from diseases of the digestive system. Fiber, which is part of some products, can cause exacerbation of diseases.
