Marinating meat

Another process that has always been of interest to any amateur chef. The Lazerson Principles program, dedicated to preparing meat for barbecue, offered just two rules and its own set of spices to be marinated. Seasonings are an intimate affair, so leave them to the discretion of the cooks. And we state the principles.





  1. No acid. No acid is allowed in the marinade - not only vinegar, but also lemon juice. The cooking guru advises soaking the meat in onion puree made with a blender, seasoned and diluted with water.
  2. Whatever spices were planned for the marinade, mustard and vegetable oil should be added to them: the first for softening, the second for better distribution of pork flavors.
  3. It is necessary to salt the meat already during baking, otherwise it will let the juice out without waiting for the skewers.

In all other respects, the principles of the preparation of Lazerson are similar to those of ordinary cooks: meat should be pickled for several hours, ideally, night.













Tobacco Chicken

Georgian dish is popular everywhere. The culinary teacher suggested such principles for its preparation.





  1. A chicken, not a broiler: a carcass should weigh a pound, maybe a little more. Everyone will probably agree with this.
  2. All joints are beaten off so that the chicken becomes almost flat. There also seems to be nothing to argue with.
  3. Chicken marinated in vegetable oil with lemon juice for at least two hours.
  4. Fry the bird grated with garlic with chili in butter mixed with vegetable oil.




Mashed potatoes
