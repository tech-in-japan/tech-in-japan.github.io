Honey with royal jelly: beneficial properties. Honey with royal jelly: recipes, properties, features

The most useful beekeeping product is honey with royal jelly. The useful properties of such a natural medicine are interesting not only to fans of alternative methods of treatment, but also to many doctors. Studies confirm the effectiveness of this unique tool in the treatment and prevention of many diseases.

















What it is?

Many years ago, people began to use royal jelly. The properties and application of this healing product, called royal jelly, were of interest to many. At that time, only representatives of the royal nobility had the opportunity to use it. Royal jelly is collected only a few weeks a year. The collection process is quite time-consuming, which affects the cost of the product.





A young bee has a special gland in which royal jelly is produced. At first it is a whitish-transparent mass, similar in density to jelly, which later becomes dull yellow. Royal jelly has a peculiar aroma and a slightly sour taste. Proteins and vitamins predominate in its composition, in addition, there are many other components necessary for the healthy development of a living organism, such as fats, carbohydrates, micro and macro elements. Such a composition provides unique pharmacological properties of royal jelly.









The product must be stored in the refrigerator, because under the influence of light and room temperature it quickly decomposes and loses its useful qualities. To extend the shelf life, honey is used as a preservative, and a new useful product is obtained - honey with royal jelly, the useful properties of which can hardly be overestimated. In addition, in its pure form, royal jelly can not be used, since an overdose of it is undesirable.









How to get it?

The most optimal ratio of components is 1: 100, that is, one hundred grams of honey is combined with one gram (or a little more) of royal jelly (but not more than five grams). The choice of honey for the preparation of the product has its own characteristics. Since a fresh dainty contains substances from which the beneficial properties of royal jelly are destroyed, it is best to use mature honey (last year‚Äôs perfect).





Mix the components immediately after the royal jelly has been removed from the mother liquors. Everything needs to be done quickly in order to minimize exposure to air and light. The resulting product is stored in the refrigerator, the shelf life is 1 year.





Beneficial features

Many are interested in what is useful and how does honey with royal jelly look like . Such a drug almost does not differ in appearance from ordinary honey, it only takes on a slightly creamy tint. Long-term studies have shown that this valuable product is useful to almost everyone, regardless of gender and age, in addition, this mixture fully preserves the beneficial properties of both components.









Honey with royal jelly is a medicine donated by Mother Nature, which can increase the body's resistance to infections, helps relieve fatigue, normalizes sleep, memory, increases working capacity, normalizes blood pressure, the state of the central nervous system and well-being. In addition, this tool is used in the complex treatment of infertility and impotence.

















Medical use

There are many diseases in which the use of honey with royal jelly can be very useful. This remedy helps women to get rid of many gynecological problems, and also increases the possibility of conception. It should be noted the popularity of honey as a means to lose weight. For men, this product helps fight diseases such as adenoma and prostatitis. Due to the high content of trace elements, special honey is useful for children instead of vitamins. In the elderly, the use of such a product significantly improves the quality of life.





In the treatment of bronchitis, tonsillitis and pharyngitis, honey with royal jelly is also very effective. The beneficial properties of this drug are unique, but still it should not be abused, since there is a risk of the body becoming addicted or product rejection. Of course, you can not use honey as the main treatment. To successfully get rid of diseases, it is necessary to combine traditional and folk methods of therapy correctly.

















Application in cosmetology

To treat skin diseases, improve skin condition and fight wrinkles, honey with royal jelly is used. Recipes, properties, features of this unique product are widely used by cosmetologists. This tool helps to cure eczema, psoriasis, various dermatitis in an advanced stage. Honey masks and wraps improve complexion, make skin soft and silky. With regular procedures, you can even get rid of wrinkles.





Features of use

Adults need to take the drug one teaspoon three times a day half an hour before meals. Depending on age, children can be given half or a quarter teaspoon up to two times a day. Keep honey in your mouth and do not swallow as long as possible. Royal jelly should not be consumed before bedtime, as this can cause insomnia.

















Contraindications

You can not use honey with royal jelly for people who suffer from diseases of an infectious nature in the acute stage, have Addison's disease, problems with the adrenal glands, as well as cancer. In addition, individual intolerance to beekeeping products is a contraindication.
