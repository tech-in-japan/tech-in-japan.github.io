Famine is also different





Nutritionists, it turns out, have divided hunger into physiological as well as psychological. This moment is very important. You should clearly understand at any moment that in the first case, the feeling of hunger is a signal of the body about the need for energy and nutrients. This feeling can never be ignored. It must be satisfied. In the second case, when psychological hunger occurs, a person feels some psychological problems and unconsciously tries to solve them with food. This condition can and must be overcome.





In order to deal with your hunger, try to give up food for three hours. In the event that for you this task seemed impossible and thoughts about eating did not let you do anything, the conclusion is clear - you do not distinguish between these two types of hunger. For physiological hunger characteristic is weakness, poor mood, loss of strength. Be sensitive to your feelings.





Do not deprive yourself of breakfast





A large number of people who want to lose weight refuse to eat breakfast - but in vain. The answer to the question of how to reduce appetite throughout the day lies in a tasty and healthy breakfast. Try not to rush while eating. It is important to slowly bite and taste every bite. Try not to miss the moment of satiation. Chew any food thoroughly, at which time cutlery can be laid on the table. Make portions smaller and don't get distracted by your computer and watching television. Before filling the plate with food, just drink a glass of ordinary water and distract from thoughts of hunger. If it was a psychological hunger, then it will pass.

Food basket





Products that reduce appetite are very diverse. These include many fruits, walnuts, hot peppers and many others. Remember that drugs are not able to correct your perception and attitude to food, so often they give a short-term effect.
