To implement the technology, a non-invasive neurointerface is being developed under the Next-Generation Nonsurgical Neurotechnology program. Its creators plan to learn how to control devices with the help of thought without the need to connect something to the human brain through surgery.



Six different teams received contracts from the Pentagon to develop the technology in question, and go to their goal in different ways.
