Coffee benefit and harm

Most scientists have concluded that coffee has properties that help in improving performance and relieve fatigue, and also have a stimulating effect on mental activity. Caffeine improves blood supply to the brain. In addition, coffee stimulates psychomotor activity that increases brain activity. The use of this drink increases the productivity of brain activity several times. However, it is important to remember that it is forbidden to drink on an empty stomach, as in this case it can cause problems with the gastrointestinal tract.
