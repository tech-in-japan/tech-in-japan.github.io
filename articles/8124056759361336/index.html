Bon soup. Recipe









Ingredients:





  • Turnip onion - 6 pieces.




  • Garlic - 20 grams.
  • Carrots - 4 pieces.
  • Green pepper - 2 pieces.
  • White cabbage - 1 piece.
  • Seasoning to taste.

First of all, it is necessary to fry finely chopped onions with garlic and other spices over low heat. Then put in the pan all the above vegetables, pour water so that the ingredients are hidden. As soon as the consistency of the ingredients is soft, you should add the fried onions, darken the Bon soup for another five minutes and start the meal.
