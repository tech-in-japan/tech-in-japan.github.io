Features of the preparation of dishes

There are many recipes that include this product. Not all of them are suitable for women during lactation. In the process of preparing buckwheat during breastfeeding, you need to pay attention to the following aspects:





  1. First of all, the grains must be thoroughly washed. Water removes harmful compounds from the surface of the cereal. They can negatively affect the health of the mother and baby. Unopened grains must be discarded.
  2. Then moisture is removed from the cereal. Place the grains in a preheated skillet and fry over medium heat for five minutes. Stir from time to time so that the product does not burn. This method of preparation allows you to make the meal appetizing and crumbly.
  3. Then the grains are put in a pan, pour cold water. The volume of liquid should be two to three times the amount of cereal. Add a little salt to the dish and put on the fire, having previously closed the lid. Bring to a boil. Cook porridge until then, until it absorbs all the moisture. Fire can be reduced so that the product does not burn.
  4. Then the cereal is cooled for fifteen minutes. Open the lid and mix the dish.
