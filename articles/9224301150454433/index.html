Vegetables

Cooking yourself

Pediatricians advise to refuse traditional cakes and pastries, purchased flour products because of not so useful structure. The only way out is to master suitable homemade baking recipes. We will tell you what sweets you can cook on your own when breastfeeding .





Cottage cheese casserole. Experts do not recommend yeast dough due to the risk of bloating and colic in a newborn. A great alternative for nursing mothers is the curd dough. Casserole is always fast, satisfying and tasty.













Ingredients:





- 500 g of cottage cheese;





- egg - 3 pcs.;





- semolina - 2 tbsp. spoons;





- 50 ml of milk;





- wheat flour - 1 teaspoon;





- sugar - 3 tbsp. spoons;





- butter - 1 tbsp. a spoon.





Technology:





  1. Cottage cheese must be wiped to a homogeneous mass.
  2. Pour the semolina with milk and leave for five minutes.
  3. Separate the proteins from the yolks and refrigerate in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  4. Beat the yolks with a mixer, and then gradually introduce sugar.
  5. Separately mix flour and semolina, add yolks and cottage cheese.
  6. Proteins need to be whipped and carefully combined with the bulk.
  7. Grease the form with butter, put the dough there.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes, temperature - 180-200 degrees.

If you wish, you can add raisins to the casserole. In order for the dried fruit not to sink to the bottom during baking, wash, dry and roll it a little in flour, and then put it into the dough.





Cookies with fennel

A light snack is not only tasty, but also healthy. Not all pediatricians will answer you that sweet can be breastfed. Most likely, they simply do not know the recipe for a great shortbread cookie with the addition of fennel seeds.













To prepare a healthy treat you will need:





- one egg;





- 100 g of oil;





- wheat flour - 50 g;





- sugar - 0.5 cups and 2 tbsp. l .;





- 1 teaspoon of fennel seeds;





- vanilla sugar - 1 teaspoon;





- salt - 1/4 tsp;





- pine nuts for decoration.





Technology:





  1. Combine the egg, sugar and butter.
  2. Add ground fennel seeds, salt and vanilla sugar.
  3. Insert the sifted flour into the dough and knead it by hand.
  4. Roll up a small sausage, wrap in a film and put in the refrigerator for two hours.
  5. Cut the dough into small pieces (width - 0.5 cm) and put them on a baking sheet covered with parchment.
  6. Bake 15 minutes (180 degrees) until golden brown.
