The most useful products

Humanity is progressing every year, however, along with this, our nutrition is only getting worse. Therefore, I want to highlight the most useful products and understand what they are useful for. These can be called a great many. Here is a list of useful products that you can highlight.





1) Eggs. This is an inexpensive, useful product. Today, eggs are available to everyone, rural residents are especially lucky in this regard, since village eggs are considered the most useful . They contain a lot of proteins, fats, trace elements and vitamins (almost all glad, there are 12 types). If you eat at least one boiled egg every day, you can strengthen your memory and extend your creative mood, in addition, our brain receives irreplaceable nutrition. The egg is probably the most healthy product of all. Vitamin E, which is part of eggs, gives our skin elasticity and preserves our beauty longer. In addition, it is no secret to anyone that they are very useful for the work of the heart and eyes. Egg lovers have strong teeth and bones. So in the list of "most useful products" they can be awarded first place.





Note that abuse leads to an increase in blood cholesterol.

It has a lot of phosphorus, calcium, iodine, potassium, especially in marine fish.





It should be noted that each type of fish is good in its own way. For example, in herring there is a large amount of iodine, carp is famous for its high vitamin B12 content, cod is good for the brain, and salmon contains essential acids such as omega-3 fatty acid.
