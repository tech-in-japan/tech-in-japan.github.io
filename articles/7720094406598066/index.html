Fasting days for weight loss - try it and you will be surprised at the effect.

Fasting days for weight loss will help you get rid of a few extra pounds and cleanse your body of harmful substances. They are much more sparing than strict diets. Thanks to these days, weight is lost gradually and the results can be saved for a long time. Just one day of some abstinence in food will not cause you negative emotions and bad mood. In addition, fasting days for weight loss are varied and you can constantly change them. According to the composition of the products, they are divided into carbohydrate, protein, fat, as well as combined. Those who decide to try a fasting day need to know some rules. It is best to arrange for your body to unload once, at most, twice a week. If you do this all the time, then select certain days and stick to your schedule. Experts also recommend drinking a lot of water. You will need up to two liters of fluid. In order for fasting days for weight loss to bring the desired effect, alternate them. Pregnant women should consult their doctor before making such a decision. In addition, some types of fasting days are prohibited for people who are not allowed to do a health condition. Be sure to first consult a nutritionist.
