Azure Technology Laboratory in Moscow





On October 15, we invite you to the autumn Azure Technology Lab, one of the most significant technical events of Microsoft in Russia. Twice a year, we gather leading IT professionals, business representatives and everyone who wants to learn the most about Azure services. The participants of the event receive not only the most relevant information about one of the leaders in the hyper-scaler market - Microsoft Azure, but also receive training in modern technologies, services and solutions that help organizations of various sizes of businesses occupy leading positions in the market.



How to register? In order to register for an event, you must select and register at one of the Azure laboratories that interests you the most.



Well, the details and the program under the cut!



What will happen?



10.00 - 12.30 Plenary session where you will learn about the development of the Azure platform, as well as hear real stories of the use of the Azure platform in leading Russian organizations, what changes your colleagues have noted in their work, how they plan to take them into account in IT strategies and on what will concentrate efforts in the near future.



13:30 - 18:30 Azure laboratories - six hands-on sessions, where you will find a cloud-intensive intensive on the main scenarios of Microsoft Azure under the guidance of the best technical experts. Register for your favorite track (s) to get on the list of participants for the entire event.



What will be the laboratory



  • Modern infrastructure: the clouds have come to stay - you will learn all about modern approaches to building the enterprise infrastructure.
  • Infrastructure management with a focus on security - The development of cloud technologies and the integration of IT in modern business processes inevitably increases the requirements for ensuring information security.
  • Developing and deploying applications in the cloud - Developers need to do what they do best - developing great applications. The Azure cloud platform takes care of the rest!
