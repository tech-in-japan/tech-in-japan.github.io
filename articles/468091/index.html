The bill on creating a single database with citizens' data was adopted in the State Duma in the first reading

Actually, the essence of the news is in the headline, and the details are described here and here .



And from myself I want to comment on this news.



I think many people dreamed of getting rid of the search for a million references and evidence that you really have (or not) real estate, a car and a job. Digital transformation should make this dream a reality in which confirmation of any fact can be found without writing formal requests and pieces of paper.



In addition, the availability of a large amount of data opens up the possibility of using statistics mechanisms, machine learning to identify trends in the development of society, improve services.



In cooperation with the Agency for Strategic Initiatives, as part of the National Technological Initiative, we are creating a talent platform that collects information about the achievements of students in order to build individual educational trajectories and recommendations to universities. For example, to understand which place and in which competitions is really important for successful training. And already at this stage they saw many opportunities for individualizing education, for example, creating a system in different areas of activity.



Probably the first difficulty that comes to mind is security. What happens if an attacker receives my data? And even if access to your personal account?



Recently, we saw a lot of news about data leaks, including from the state. organs. What will make this database work correctly? Experts have yet to answer these questions.
