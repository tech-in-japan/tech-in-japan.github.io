What about iron?



New iPhones are built on the A13 Bionic chip. It is made according to the "improved" 7-nanometer process technology. There are six main cores: two high-performance and four energy-efficient. These are respectively two Lightning at 2.65 GHz and four Thunder at 1.8 GHz.



There are 18 cores on this chip. In addition to the main cores, there are four more graphic cores, and the eight-core Neural Engine.

At the presentation, Apple stated that all the cores have become more powerful, but at the same time consume less energy than the previous generation:



  • high-performance - 20% more powerful and 30% energy efficient,
  • cores for basic tasks - 20% more powerful and 40% energy efficient,
  • graphics module - 20% more powerful and 40% energy efficient,
  • Neural Engine - 20% more powerful and 15% more energy efficient.


The chip is made by the Taiwanese company TSMC on the new process technology, which itself calls the N7 Pro. How it differs from the N7 + process no one knows.



Officially, RAM in all smartphones in the 4 gigabyte line. Although there is information on the Internet that there is another 2 GB of RAM, tightly reserved for the camera and Deep Fusion image processing technology.





How much memory is available?



  • iPhone 11: 64, 128, and 256 GB
  • iPhone 11 Pro (Max): 64, 256, and 512 GB The "firmware" does not have a 128-gigabyte version.


Is there a difference between 11 Pro and 11 Pro Max apart from the diagonal?



No. These are two essentially identical models with different display sizes and batteries of different capacities. The processor, cameras, memory, case materials, colors - all this is identical.
