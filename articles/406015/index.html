Cancel roaming. Nuances and problems

Recently, the FAS ordered the operators to cancel roaming, and gave 2 weeks to this case. The operators went nuts and did nothing , respectively, the HYIP erupted over what they were byaki. And, in general, the operators are not white and not fluffy, but still not as bad as can be considered.



In this article I want to explain what the problems are, and why 2 weeks is a populist hitting and not a real desire to solve the problem globally.



To begin with, we will define the types of roaming. For with terminology, all are full of seams, and among journalists, who are constantly raped by scientists from both FAS and readers. Roaming is very different.



  • Intranet. It is his most likely offer to cancel. This is a kind of roaming,

    when you are in a different region, but in the network of the same operator.
  • National. A lot of statements that it is going to cancel it. But this type of roaming means that you are in your own country, but in the network of another operator. And in general, to cancel it is very, very strange. For its cancellation will most of all hit small operators who do not have networks in other areas, and it will be necessary to pay for it.
  • International. Everything is clear, roaming in another country. It is not planned to cancel


Further, for definiteness, I will assume that they are still going to cancel intranet roaming, as the strangest of the existing ones.



Europe and America



Just want to make a reference to Europe and America, where there is no roaming, and everyone loves to poke in that direction. But we must not forget that communication is much, much more expensive there. You can say that there and the level of salaries is higher. This is true, but operators buy equipment for the same money as in Russia, in Germany, in the USA (maybe even more expensive). And this is a very serious item of expenditure, which does not depend on the level of salaries of users of operators.

In addition, in Europe, roaming was canceled not immediately, in one day, but for a long time and systematically prepared. They wrote laws, gradually limited the maximum amount for roaming, allowed operators to prepare, and only a few years later, canceled it.

Licensing



Operators receive licenses not for the country, but for each region separately, buy other operators, so in fact it turns out that two different regions are served by different legal entities. Technical problems



As I already wrote, the equipment in different regions of the operators is different, there is a decent zoo, which is not very conducive to coordination. Now they are gradually enlarging, but this has side effects that a failure in a large data center leads to a global connection collapse in several areas. A few months ago, MegaFon happened exactly like that. According to the official version, the problem was related to the fact that the place on the SSD-drives in the data centers ran out, the subscriber data fell on the HDD, this resulted in timeouts and everything fell. Naturally, in the nearest store you cannot buy a couple of SSDs to add cache, and the problem turned out to be very serious.



Reasonable cancellation



Let's think about the meaning of cancellation. If you pay the operator enough money, you already have no roaming, this problem does not concern you. The problem is only for those who pay little or are sitting on tariffs without a monthly fee. Accordingly, the operator does not receive money from these users, but spends on maintaining the network, and will have to spend even more without getting anything in return. At the same time, according to reports, 9% of subscribers travel to other regions, and some of them do not pay for roaming anyway. Is the problem worth such a zealous decision?



Who will pay for everything?



Well, the answer is obvious - we, the subscribers. Even if we assume that the operators will not raise tariffs, but simply reduce their profits, they will cut costs along with the profits. For example, on the development of the network. What will not be noticeable instantly, but over time will lead to a deterioration in the quality of communication.
